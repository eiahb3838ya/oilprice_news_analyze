update 6-oil steady on supply optimism but economic worries loom
london jan reuters oil prices steadied on wednesday after climbing about percent in the previous session on expectations that production cuts will tighten supply and that possible chinese stimulus might help the global economy
brent crude oil futures were at per barrel at gmt up cents or percent
west texas intermediate crude futures were down cents or percent at a barrel
it seems the oil market is looking at saudi arabia aggressive supply cuts and chinese aggressive stimulus said jonathan barratt chief investment officer at probis securities in sydney
china central bank on wednesday made its biggest daily net cash injection via reverse repo operations on record
markets took the announcement as evidence that authorities are shifting to a policy of easing to counter a slowdown in the world economy
on tuesday china national development and reform commission signalled it might roll out more fiscal stimulus
prices climbed very much in line with global stock markets which received some support from indications by chinese officials that beijing is considering an economic stimulus package consultancy jbc energy said
but mounting signs of an economic slowdown in china and across the globe may yet keep oil prices in check
earlier this week china reported poor december trade data with exports and imports contracting from a year earlier
white house estimates showed on tuesday that the economy is taking a hit from a partial government shutdown
the outlook for the global economy darkened further after britain parliament on tuesday shot down prime minister theresa may deal to leave the european union
opec cuts support crude fundamentally oil markets are receiving support from supply cuts by the organization of the petroleum exporting countries including top exporter saudi arabia and major producer russia
opec production cuts will limit inventory builds to those justified by higher demand which should settle the market in a sustainable range above per barrel standard chartered bank said
however surging crude production which hit a record million barrels per day late last year threatens to undermine the efforts
