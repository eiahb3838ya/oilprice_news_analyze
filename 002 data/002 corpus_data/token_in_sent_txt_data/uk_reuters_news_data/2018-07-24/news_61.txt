shell's knarr field back in production after strike
oslo reuters shell s knarr field which shut output during a rig workers strike that lasted ten days earlier this month is back in production the company said on tuesday
knarr is back in production now said shell spokeswoman kitty eide declining to comment on the current output of the field
the field produced about barrels per day before the strike mostly oil with some natural gas liquids and gas
the field s are japan s idemitsu wintershall and dea
