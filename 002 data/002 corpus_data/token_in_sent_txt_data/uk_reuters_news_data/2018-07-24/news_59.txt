fifteen nigerian senators defect from ruling apc party -senate president's aide
lagos july reuters fifteen nigerian senators defected from the ruling all progressives congress party on tuesday leaving it with minority in the upper house of parliament an aide to the senate president said on twitter
senate president bukola saraki said senators had decamped without giving details a faction of president muhammadu buhari s ruling party said this month it no longer supported his government
