venezuelan troops fire tear gas at colombian border: witness
urena venezuela feb reuters venezuelan troops near the colombian border on saturday fired tear gas at a group of people seeking to cross into the neighboring nation to work according to a reuters witness
the government of president nicolas maduro has shuttered borders as opposition leaders prepare to bring humanitarian aid into venezuela against the wishes of the ruling socialist party
