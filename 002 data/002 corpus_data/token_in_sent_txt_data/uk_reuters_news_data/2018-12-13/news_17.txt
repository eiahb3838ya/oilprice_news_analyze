saudi ambassador to washington welcomes yemen's hodeidah ceasefire
dubai dec reuters saudi arabia s ambassador to the united states prince khalid bin salman welcomed the united nations backed agreement between yemen s warring parties to a ceasefire in the red sea port city of hodeidah on thursday
the agreement announced today will help bring back security to the region including the security of the red sea a vital water way for international trade he said on his twitter account adding that he hoped houthis would stop working on behalf of the iranian regime s interests
reporting by tuqa khalid editing by richard balmforth
