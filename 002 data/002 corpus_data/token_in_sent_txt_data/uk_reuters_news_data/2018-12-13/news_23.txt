yemen's houthis agree in principle to u.n. role at sanaa airport
rimbo sweden dec reuters yemen s houthis have agreed in principle on a united nation s role in sanaa airport a spokesman for the armed movement said in sweden on thursday
the houthis are happy with what was achieved in the consultations said the spokesman speaking after sponsored peace talks with yemen s internationally recognised government
a role would include carrying out safety and inspection checks at the airport
