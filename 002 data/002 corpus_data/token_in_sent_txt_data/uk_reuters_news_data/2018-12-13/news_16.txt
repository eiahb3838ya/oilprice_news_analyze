unite union recommends 2,500 uk offshore oil workers accept new pay offer
london reuters britain s unite union on thursday recommended that of its members covered by the offshore contractors association oca accept a new pay offer averting the threat of a strike that could have affected north sea oil platforms
an online consultative ballot will open on monday december closing on tuesday january with unite recommending acceptance of the pay offer a union spokesman said in an emailed statement
