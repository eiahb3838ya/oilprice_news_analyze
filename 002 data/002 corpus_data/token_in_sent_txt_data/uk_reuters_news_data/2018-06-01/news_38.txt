total determined over plans to drill amazon basin, says ceo
paris reuters total has not abandoned plans to drill a basin of the amazon river after brazil s environmental agency rejected its application for a license for a fourth time the french oil and gas major s chief executive said on friday
ceo patrick pouyanne said the company would not cede to any kind of blackmail
he had begun speaking as several greenpeace activists hung from ropes above him in protest at the company s exploration plans
our position on the amazon project has not been abandoned
we have our rights and we have obviously to respect brazilian laws pouyanne said
it is also clear that brazil s environmental regulators are under immense pressure from other organizations
we are exchanging information with them he added
