porsche denies has halted sale of new vehicles in europe
hamburg june reuters volkswagen s porsche unit on friday denied a report that it was halting the sale of new cars in europe but said supply would be limited temporarily
german daily frankfurter allgemeine zeitung had reported that porsche had stopped the sale of new cars in europe due to the switch to new lab test standards wltp and rde
it cited no sources
