putin discusses extension of opec+ deal with his security council
moscow reuters russian president vladimir putin discussed questions related to an extension of the oil cut deal with his security council interfax news agency reported on friday citing kremlin spokesman dmitry peskov
the topic of the extension of the deal was also touched on the back of the president s contacts with saudi crown prince mohammed bin salman interfax quoted peskov as saying
