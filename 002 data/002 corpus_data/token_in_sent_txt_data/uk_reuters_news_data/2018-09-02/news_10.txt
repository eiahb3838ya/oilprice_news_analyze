iraqi majority bloc in parliament contains 177 lawmakers - state news
baghdad sept reuters an iraqi political alliance announced on sunday contains lawmakers from electoral lists making it the majority bloc in the newly elected parliament an updated document published by the state news agency showed
the agency had earlier published an incomplete version of the document before more political groups had signed it
the alliance which contains groups led by cleric moqtada and prime minister haider now has enough seats to form the next iraqi government
