update 1-saudi crown prince meets us's mnuchin, saudi state tv says
dubai reuters saudi crown prince mohammed bin salman met treasury secretary steven mnuchin in riyadh on monday saudi state tv said in a tweet
mnuchin said on sunday saudi arabia s explanation of the killing of journalist jamal khashoggi was a good first step but not enough adding that it was premature to discuss sanctions against riyadh over the incident
the saudi crown prince and mnuchin stressed the importance of strategic partnership and the future role of this partnership through vision the saudi tv tweet said referring to the kingdom s long term development plan
the official said on sunday he would not attend a major investment conference to be hosted in riyadh this week and that his visit was to hold talks on joint efforts towards countering terrorist financing and curbing iran s military and political influence
