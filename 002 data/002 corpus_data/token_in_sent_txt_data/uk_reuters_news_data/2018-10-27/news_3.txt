iran parliament approves new economy minister ahead of new u.s. sanction
dubai oct reuters iran s parliament on saturday approved a new economy minister and three other ministers in a reshuffle of the cabinet s economic team by president hassan rouhani ahead of new sanctions targeting key oil exports from nov state television reported
academic farhad dejpasand won a vote of confidence as the new minister of economics and finance in a reshuffle that also included new industry labour and roads ministers during a parliamentary session that was carried live on state tv
