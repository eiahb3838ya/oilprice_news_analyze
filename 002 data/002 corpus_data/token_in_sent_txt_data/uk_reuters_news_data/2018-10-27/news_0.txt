ukraine to raise households gas prices to market level from 2020
kiev oct reuters the ukrainian government plans to raise household gas prices from to match what state energy company naftogaz charges the country s industrial companies the government said on saturday
gas tariffs are heavily subsidised in ukraine which has committed to raise them gradually to qualify for more financial assistance from the international monetary fund
it had already announced on that they would go up by nearly a quarter
that announcement secured a new billion standby aid deal from the imf helping the country maintain financial stability and the trust of investors as it heads into a national election next year
