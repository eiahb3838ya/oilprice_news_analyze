pope tells top oil executives world must convert to clean fuel
vatican city june reuters pope francis addressing top oil company executives said on saturday that the world must convert to clean fuel
civilisation requires energy but energy use must not destroy civilisation he told the group at the end of a conference in the vatican
he told them that climate change was a challenge of epochal proportions adding that the world needed to come up with an energy mix that combated pollution eliminated poverty and promoted social justice
