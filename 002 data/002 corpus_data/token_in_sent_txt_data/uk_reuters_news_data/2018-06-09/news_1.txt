iran to increase oil production by 460 million barrels in three years- oil minister
beirut june reuters iran is pursuing a plan to increase its oil output by million barrels within three years oil minister bijan zanganeh said on saturday according to shana the news site of the iranian oil ministry
the plan will focus on increasing output from oilfields including in ilam khuzestan gachsaran falat qareh and fars zanganeh said
the bulk of the work to increase the output at the oilfields will be carried out by iranian companies zanganeh said
more than percent of the equipment for developing the output is iranian zanganeh said according to shana
