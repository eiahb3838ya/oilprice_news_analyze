update 1-venezuela pdvsa begins interest payments on 2020 bond -sources
adds context bondholder comment by corina pons and paul kilby york april reuters venezuela s state oil company pdvsa has begun making payments towards million it owes in interest on a bond maturing in to some holders according to three sources in the financial sector
the government began quietly halting interest payments on some billion in publicly traded debt last year
at least one venezuelan bondholder committee has hired a financial adviser ahead of potential litigation in the face of continued breaches
the latest payments would have reached some holders through custody firm dtc the sources said on monday
pdvsa used citgo petroleum its refining unit in the united states as percent collateral on the bond
venezuela may be complying with the payment in order to avoid putting the valuable asset at risk in a potential legal battle
venezuelan president nicolas maduro has blamed financial sanctions imposed by the united states for preventing international transfers
however the sanctions do not block routine operations such as debt payments
earlier this month the government paid interest on pdvsa s bond which was mostly bought by goldman sachs group in a controversial operation last year
that transfer upset some bondholders still waiting payments
with the new pdvsa payment legal action from investors in other bonds could take place said a holder of other venezuelan debt who asked not to be identified
