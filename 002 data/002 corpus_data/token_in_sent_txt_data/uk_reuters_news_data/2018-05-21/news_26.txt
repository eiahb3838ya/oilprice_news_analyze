merkel, poroshenko discuss gas transit through ukraine and minsk agreement
berlin may reuters german chancellor angela merkel and ukrainian president petro poroshenko spoke by telephone on monday about merkel s visit to russia last week efforts to implement the minsk agreement and gas transit through ukraine a spokeswoman said
both parties agreed to remain in close contact on these issues and to deepen cooperation martina fietz a spokeswoman for the german government said in a statement
merkel held talks with president vladimir putin in sochi russia on friday and stressed the need for the continuation of gas transit via ukraine even after the completion of the nord stream gas pipeline which will circumvent the country
