update 1-russia sovereign wealth fund calls for increase in state debt
adds details quotes context moscow may reuters the head of russia s billion sovereign wealth fund called on monday for the government to issue more domestic debt to fund infrastructure projects
the level of domestic debt is insignificant now
it is far lower than in other countries kirill dmitriev who heads the russian direct investment fund rdif told a media conference call on monday organised ahead of the st petersburg economic forum an annual business gathering
if these funds are invested in lucrative infrastructure projects it would contribute to additional growth he said
rdif leverages its own capital by investing in partnership with foreign funds
it will meet with president vladimir putin during the economic forum this week along with the heads of around other investment funds from countries which manage more than trillion in assets
russia will need to spend at least trillion roubles billion over the next six years to meet economic goals set in new decrees that putin signed after being sworn into the presidency at the start of may
rdif and its foreign partners plan to invest trillion roubles billion in the next two years
they have previously invested trillion roubles in more than projects dmitriev said
during the st petersburg forum rdif plans to announce more than projects for hundreds of million dollars with investment from france japan and saudi arabia in infrastructure agriculture chemicals and medicine
