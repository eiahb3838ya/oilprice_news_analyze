pakistan's engro says "no obligation" to renegotiate lng contract
islamabad oct reuters pakistani conglomorate engro corp ltd said on friday it had no obligation to renegotiate a contract for a liquified natural gas terminal with the government a day after the new petroleum minister said it would seek new terms
the government does not have a contractual right to its terms and we are accordingly under no obligation to renegotiate the same the company said in a statement
