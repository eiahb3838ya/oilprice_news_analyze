update 1-china's state power in talks for brazil hydro, thermal plants -source
sao paulo reuters china s state power investment corporation is in talks to acquire two additional hydroelectric dams and two thermal plants in brazil a person with knowledge of the matter said thursday
one of the hydroelectric dams is usina tres irmaos an megawatt dam in sao paulo state controlled by brazilian infrastructure group triunfo participacoes e investimentos sa tpi the person added requesting anonymity to discuss private talks
brazilian newspaper valor economico first reported the talks over tres irmaos on its website on thursday
state power and triunfo declined to comment
but the source added a negotiation with triunfo would not be easy because the company was singled out by brazilian prosecutors this month for not collaborating with a corruption probe investigating the its operations
the chinese power company is expected to present a binding offer by the end of the month for control of the santo antonio hydropower dam in the northern state of rondonia
talks to acquire the dam have gone on for almost two years
state power is also considering a bid for two coal thermal plants that france s engie sa owns in the southern brazilian states of santa catarina and rio grande do sul the source said
engie which put the plants up for sale six months ago said the sale process is ongoing without elaborating on bidders
adriana waltrick state power s brazil country head told reuters in may that the company wanted to keep investing in acquisitions after paying billion for a license to operate the são simao hydropower plant previously run by companhia energética de minas gerais
