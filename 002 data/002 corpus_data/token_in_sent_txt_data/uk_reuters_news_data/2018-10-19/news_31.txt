petrovietnam says tokyo gas may help with power plant project in vietnam
hanoi oct reuters tokyo gas is interested in cooperating with vietnam s petrovietnam power corp or pv power to develop a natural power project in the southeast asian country pv power s parent said on friday
tokyo gas wants to help secure liquefied natural gas supplies and funds for the construction of the nhon trach power plants in the southern province of dong nai vietnam oil and gas group said in a statement on its website
it did not say if tokyo gas would hold a stake in the project
tokyo gas did not immediately respond to a request for comment made via its website
the statement follows a meeting between senior executives from pv power and tokyo gas in hanoi this week pv power said
the two plants with a combined capacity of megawatts would be operational from according to pv power
