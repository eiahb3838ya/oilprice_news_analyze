eletrobras amazonas unit auction delayed to dec. 10
sao paulo nov reuters brazilian development bank bndes has pushed back the auction of a unit of centrais elétricas brasileiras sa originally scheduled for next week to the power company said in a securities filing late on thursday
those interested in bidding for amazonas distribuidora de energia sa a distribution company based in the northern state of amazonas must file necessary documents on the filing said
the filing did not specify any reasons for the postponement
the auction is a key step towards the privatization of eletrobras as the power holding is known and has been marred by repeated delays
reuters reported on that eletrobras could take on an additional billion reais in debt in order to pave the way for the amazonas privatization
