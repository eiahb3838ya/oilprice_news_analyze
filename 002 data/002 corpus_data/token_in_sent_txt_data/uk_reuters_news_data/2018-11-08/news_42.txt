norway's ivar aasen and edvard grieg oilfields shut
oslo nov reuters production at the ivar aasen and edvard grieg oilfields in the north sea are shut due to a shutdown of the sture oil terminal following the collision of a tanker and a frigate off the coast of norway on thursday
ivar aasen is shut down said an aker bp spokesman
edvard grieg was also shutting down production a source with knowledge of the matter told reuters
