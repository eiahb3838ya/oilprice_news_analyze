pgnig ceo sees more deals on lng supplies to poland
warsaw nov reuters polish gas firm pgnig expects to sign one or two more contracts for liquefied natural gas lng deliveries to poland as the country seeks to reduce dependence on russian fuel pgnig chief executive piotr wozniak said
earlier on thursday pgnig said it had signed a deal on lng supplies with cheniere marketing international
