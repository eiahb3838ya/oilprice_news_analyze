aker energy said the proposed transaction which is subject to approval from ghanaian authorities gives it a stake in the ultra deepwater tano cape three points block which holds an estimated million barrels of oil equivalent and has the potential for a further million barrels
aker roekke s main listed investment vehicle owns percent of aker energy while trg his privately held holding company owns the remainder
