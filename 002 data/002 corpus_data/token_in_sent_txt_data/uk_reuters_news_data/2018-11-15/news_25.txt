german court imposes diesel ban on western city and motorway - n-tv
berlin nov reuters a german administrative court on thursday ruled that the western state of north must establish a driving ban for diesel vehicles in certain areas of the city of essen and on parts of a busy motorway german broadcaster reported on its website
both essen and the motorway affected are located in the ruhr region germany s industrial heartland and one of its most densely populated areas
