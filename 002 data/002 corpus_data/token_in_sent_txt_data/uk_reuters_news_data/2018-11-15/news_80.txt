france has sufficient power supply for the winter -rte
paris nov reuters france has enough power supply to see it through the winter but the country needs to be vigilant from to the end of february during an expected cold spell when supply is expected to be tight said grid operator rte on thursday
rte said in its winter and outlook that up to electricity demand and supply is expected to be balanced but without any margins due to the expected shutdown of some thermal power generation units
after winter france will have enough margin which could allow it to gradually shut down five coal power units in cordemais havre gardanne and and the fessenheim nuclear power plant under certain conditions
