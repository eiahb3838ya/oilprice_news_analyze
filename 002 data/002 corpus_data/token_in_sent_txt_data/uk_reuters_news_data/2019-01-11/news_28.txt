croatian hydro power plant remains shut after two killed in fire
zagreb jan reuters croatia s mw dubrovnik hydro power plant was taken offline and will remain shut until an investigation into the cause of a fire which killed two people and injured three others has been completed system operator hops said on friday
the plant has been offline since until further notice said nada kolega a spokeswoman for hops
the plant in the southern village of plat on the adriatic sea accounts for nearly percent of croatian power production and also supplies consumers in neighbouring bosnia
the fire broke out on thursday morning
its owner power utility hep said on friday that rescue services had recovered a second body from the sea while the search for a third missing worker was still under way
