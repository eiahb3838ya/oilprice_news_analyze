uganda aims to impose penalties on distributor for power outages
kampala jan reuters uganda aims to impose financial penalties from march on its sole power distributor umeme ltd for any frequent and prolonged supply outages the regulator said
the public has long complained about high tariffs and long power cuts while unreliable supplies add extra costs to businesses which often run costly generators to keep operations going during outages
the electricity regulatory authority era said in a notice published on its website that it intended to set targets for reliability and quality of power supplied by umeme which secured a licence in to be the sole distributor
