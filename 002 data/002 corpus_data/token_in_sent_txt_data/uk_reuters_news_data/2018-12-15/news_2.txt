governments agree rules for implementing 2015 paris climate agreement
katowice poland reuters nearly nations agreed on saturday on a rulebook for implementing a landmark global deal to tackle climate change
after two weeks of talks and two years of work nations overcame political divisions to reach consensus on a more detailed framework for the paris agreement which aims to limit a rise in average world temperatures to well below two degrees celsius degrees fahrenheit above times
