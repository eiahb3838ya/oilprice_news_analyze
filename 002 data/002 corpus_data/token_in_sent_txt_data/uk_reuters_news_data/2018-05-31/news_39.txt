malaysia's petronas to take 25 pct stake in lng canada
may reuters malaysia s petronas said on thursday it had agreed to buy a percent stake in the lng canada project
petronas will buy the equity stake in the project in british columbia canada through its north montney lng lp unit it said in a statement
lng canada is a joint venture between royal dutch shell plc petrochina co ltd mitsubishi corp and korea gas corp
upon completion shell will be the biggest shareholder with a percent stake in lng canada
