iran will not change regional policies under u.s. threats - foreign minister
ankara aug reuters iran will not rein in its influence in the middle east despite mounting pressure on tehran to curb its regional activities foreign minister mohammad javad zarif told qatar s al jazeera tv
iran will not change its policies in the region because of sanctions and threats al jazeera on monday quoted zarif as saying
in may president donald trump exited iran s nuclear deal with major powers saying it failed to address iran s ballistic missile program its nuclear activities beyond or its role in conflicts in yemen and syria
