french student unrest gathers momentum
paris dec reuters french student protests intensified on tuesday with various incidents around the country of demonstrators setting buildings on fire and of violent clashes with police
over the course of this week students have gradually started to get involved in the yellow vests protests against president emmanuel macron
local authorities told reuters that part of the high school in blagnac near toulouse in southwest france had been set on fire on tuesday
there were also clashes in lyon marseille bordeaux and the city of orleans while schools were blocked in creteil and versailles near paris
earlier on tuesday france s prime minister suspended planned increases to fuel taxes for at least six months in response to weeks of sometimes violent protests in the first major by president emmanuel macron s administration after months in office
