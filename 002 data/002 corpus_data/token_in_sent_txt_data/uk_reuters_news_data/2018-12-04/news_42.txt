french pm to announce three-month suspension of fuel-tax increase -- party lawmaker
paris dec reuters french prime minister edouard philippe plans to announce a suspension of increases to allow time for discussions on the way forward a senior member of the governing republique en marche party said on tuesday
the decision is in response to nationwide yellow vest protests against the increases with the next one scheduled to kick in from january
the ruling party lawmaker said philippe would also announce measures to soften planned increases in power and gas prices to lighten the burden on france s squeezed households
reporting by marine pennetier vey and richard lough writing by luke baker
