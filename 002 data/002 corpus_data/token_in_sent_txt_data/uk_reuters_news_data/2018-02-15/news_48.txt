aoun told tillerson lebanon sticking to its internationally recognized borders, rejects israeli claims over disputed area in lebanese waters - statement
beirut reuters president michel aoun told secretary of state rex tillerson on thursday that lebanon was committed to preserving calm on its southern border and urged washington to play an effective role to help resolve beirut s land and maritime disputes with israel
aoun urged the united states to work on preventing israel from continuing its assaults on lebanese sovereignty by land and sea a statement from the presidency said
he said lebanon was sticking by its internationally recognised borders and rejected israeli claims over a disputed maritime zone
