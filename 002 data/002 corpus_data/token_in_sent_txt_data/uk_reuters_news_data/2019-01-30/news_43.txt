mexico's lopez obrador says pemex better now than in last 30 years
mexico city jan reuters mexican president andres manuel lopez obrador said on wednesday that corruption is being eliminated from state oil company pemex adding that the company is better now than it has been in last years
rating agency fitch on tuesday downgraded the credit rating of pemex which holds billion in financial debt the highest of any state oil company in latin america
the move sent mexico s peso currency tumbling
