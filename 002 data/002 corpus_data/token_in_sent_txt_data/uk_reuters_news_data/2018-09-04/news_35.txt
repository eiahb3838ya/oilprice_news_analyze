update 5-typhoon slices through western japan, killing at least six
tokyo reuters japan issued evacuation advisories for more than a million people and canceled hundreds of flights as typhoon jebi sliced across the west on tuesday cutting power overturning cars and killing at least six people
jebi or swallow in korean was briefly a super typhoon and is the most powerful storm to hit japan in years following rains landslides floods and heat that killed hundreds of people this summer

nhk and broadcaster tbs put the number of deaths at six
tides in some areas were the highest since a typhoon in nhk said with flooding covering one runway at kansai airport near osaka forcing the closure of the airport and leaving about tourists stranded
