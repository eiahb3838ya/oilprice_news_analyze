japan issues evacuation advisories as typhoon jebi approaches
tokyo sept reuters japan issued evacuation advisories for almost people and cancelled hundreds of flights in the face of strong winds and heavy rain as typhoon jebi roared north and was set to make landfall later on tuesday
jebi whose name means swallow in korean is the latest harsh weather to hit japan this summer following deadly rains landslides floods and heat that killed hundreds of people
the storm was bearing down on western japan and set to make landfall on shikoku the smallest main island before raking across the western part of the largest main island honshu and osaka the nation s city later on tuesday
evacuation advisories were issued for some people as the wind and rain began picking up
