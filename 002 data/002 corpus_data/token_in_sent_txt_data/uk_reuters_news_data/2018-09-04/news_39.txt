france's macron appoints former green lawmaker environment minister - presidency
paris reuters france s president emmanuel macron on tuesday named francois de rugy a former green lawmaker now in the ruling party as his new environment minister the presidency said in a statement
the post traditionally includes the energy portfolio
de rugy is known as a pragmatist who backed macron s decision last year to delay the phased reduction of nuclear energy in france s electricity production
