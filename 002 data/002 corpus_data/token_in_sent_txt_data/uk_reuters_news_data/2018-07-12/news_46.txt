trump did not threaten to leave nato, macron says
brussels july reuters french president emmanuel macron said donald trump did not directly threaten to leave the nato security alliance during a summit on thursday
some news agencies reported the president had threatened to pull out of the alliance over frustration with member states lack of commitment to agreed goals including spending percent of gdp on defence
but macron said no direct threat was issued
nato sources said that while trump warned that the could go it alone if others didn t step up it fell short of a direct threat to formally withdraw
