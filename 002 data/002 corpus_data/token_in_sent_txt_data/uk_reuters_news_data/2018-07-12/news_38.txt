libya's national oil corporation reopens el feel oilfield
tunis july reuters libya s national oil corporation noc said on thursday that it was reopening the el feel oilfield after resolving a dispute with guards over pay and benefits that shut it down in february
production at el feel was expected to reach bpd in two days and bpd three days later noc said in a statement
el feel is operated by mellitah oil and gas a joint venture between noc and italy s eni
