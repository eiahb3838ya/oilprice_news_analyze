petrobras loses tax cases worth $1.8 mln
brasilia reuters brazil s oil company petroleo brasileiro said on tuesday that it lost two cases before the carf tax appeals board that would cost it about billion reais billion
both cases involved taxes on remittances abroad to pay for the rental of drill ships used for exploration the company said in a securities filing
petrobras said it intends to appeal the decisions
