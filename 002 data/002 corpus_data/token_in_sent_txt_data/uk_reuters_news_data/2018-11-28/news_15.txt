germany sticks to nord stream 2 pipeline amid ukraine conflict
berlin nov reuters criticism of the nordstream pipeline which will allow russia to bypass ukraine in transporting gas to europe has not affected germany s view of the project a government spokesman in berlin said on wednesday
earlier ukraine s ambassador to germany had said the project which germany is committed to should be put on hold after russia seized three ukrainian ships near crimea on sunday
german government spokesman steffen seibert added that it remained the case that ukraine should still be a transit country for gas from russia to europe
