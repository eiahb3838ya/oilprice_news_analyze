update 2-china imports first u.s. crude in two months, traders still wary
teapot refiner yuhuang receives first crude cargo in nov vlcc offloads mln bbls southern green canyon crude in shandong traders still wait for direction from beijing china had become largest buyer of exported oil adds data about crude exports to china by florence tan and chen aizhu dec reuters china imported its first crude oil cargo in around two months last week according to industry sources and refinitiv eikon data a deal made by an independent teapot refiner as larger players held off amid trade tensions
chinese buyers have largely avoided oil during the two countries trade war fearing the imposition of tariffs
china had risen to become the largest importer of crude oil in before cutting off shipments as the trade war escalated
the world s two largest economies agreed over the weekend to stop imposing new tariffs on each other s goods for days though chinese oil traders said they were still wary and looking for direction from beijing
independent refiner shandong yuhuang petrochemicals received its crude shipment last week a company executive said
we decided to buy this cargo as it has good value for money said the executive who declined to be named due to company policy
for the first seven months of china imported an average of barrels of crude per day bpd according to energy department figures surpassing canada as the biggest foreign buyer of barrels
but crude imports from the united states slumped to the lowest in more than two years after beijing threatened to impose tariffs in august even though it later took crude off its sanctions list
chinese customs data last recorded imports from the united states in september and a search in its database did not show any imports in october
the white house said in a statement on sunday that beijing had now agreed to buy an unspecified but very substantial amount of agricultural energy industrial and other products
still chinese buyers remained wary waiting for further instructions from beijing
we re still gauging the policies it s only a window a chinese oil buyer said adding that the oil could arrive between january and march
very large crude carrier vlcc new courage discharged about million barrels of southern green canyon crude at lanshan in shandong province on refinitiv eikon data showed
unipec had chartered the super tanker to ship american oil to china
new courage first called at cezi island in zhoushan zhejiang province where it offloaded on about barrels of latin american crude according to the data
sinopec the parent company of unipec declined comment
the chinese oil buyer added that mars crude which has been offered at about a barrel above dubai quotes for delivery to north asia remained uncompetitive with middle east oil
but west texas intermediate wti midland which was recently sold at about a barrel above dated brent could work he said
unipec and royal dutch shell plc are seeking to book supertankers to load crude in the gulf coast in late december for china traders said but added that these ships have options to be diverted elsewhere in asia
