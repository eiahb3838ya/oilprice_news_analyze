update 1-total says fuel stations running dry due to "yellow vest" protests
paris reuters total on monday said several dozen of its gasoline stations had run dry as a more than long protest over fuel tax hikes began to impact fuel reserves and distribution
a total spokesman said yellow vest protesters because of the jackets they wear and which must be carried in all vehicles in france were obstructing access to fuel depots
as a result of the unrest some fuel stations out of the company s network across the country were empty because they could not receive supplies the spokesman added
protesters have been blocking roads across france impeding access to fuel depots shopping malls and some airports
on saturday rioters transformed upscale paris neighbourhoods into battle zones
in the morbihan area of brittany in western france authorities were considering restricting the volumes that motorists could buy to prevent more fuel stations running out of petrol and diesel a local official said
