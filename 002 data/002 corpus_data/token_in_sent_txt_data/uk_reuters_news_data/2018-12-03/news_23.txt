eu proposes restoring anti-dumping duties on argentine biodiesel - document
paris dec reuters the european commission has proposed reinstating duties on imports of argentine biodiesel after massive shipments of cheap biodiesel from latin america into the european union threatened its industry an eu document seen by reuters shows
the plan follows an investigation requested by eu producers which have been hit hard by imports since the eu scrapped duties last year
the proposal needs to be approved by eu member states
the european commission decided not to reinstate provisional import tariffs on argentine biodiesel in september though it considered imports were subsidised and a threat to the eu industry saying it needed to collect further information
