libya's el-sharara oilfield will reopen when our demands met - protesters
benghazi dec reuters a spokesman for protesters occupying libya s oilfield said on wednesday the facility would be reopened when their demands are met
the spokesman mohammed ahmed added in remarks to reuters that the internationally recognised prime minister fayez told protesters on a visit to the field that their urgent demands would be implemented within a week from next saturday dec
libyan state oil firm noc declined to comment on reopening oilfield
sarraj could not immediately be contacted for comment
