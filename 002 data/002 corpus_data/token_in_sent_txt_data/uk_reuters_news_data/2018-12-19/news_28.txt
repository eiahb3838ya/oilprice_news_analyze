south sudan exports 1 mln barrels of oil through sudanese ports - state news agency
cairo dec reuters south sudan on wednesday exported million barrels of oil from its toma south oilfield via its northern neighbour sudan s ports sudanese news agency suna said
sudan s oil minister azhari abdel qader said the exports were a result of the efforts of president omar and his counterpart salva kiir to achieve peace and stability in south sudan suna said
