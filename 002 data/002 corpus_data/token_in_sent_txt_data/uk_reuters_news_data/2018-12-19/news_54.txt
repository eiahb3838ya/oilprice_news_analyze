sempra energy to supply lng to poland
dec reuters utility sempra energy said on wednesday it had signed a agreement with poland s gas firm pgnig to supply billion cubic meters of liquefied natural gas lng annually
the deal comes as the european union is looking to reduce its reliance on russia for its gas needs by developing lng infrastructure to tap other suppliers including the united states
this agreement marks an important step toward poland s energy independence and security energy secretary rick perry said
the financial details of the deal were not disclosed
