libyan pm orders reopening of el sharara oilfield, wells still off -engineer
benghazi libya dec reuters libya s internationally recognised prime minister fayez has given orders to reopen el sharara oilfield which was shut by protesters but production has not restarted yet a field engineer said on wednesday
oil workers were waiting for orders from the chairman of state oil firm noc mustafa sanallah the engineer told reuters
