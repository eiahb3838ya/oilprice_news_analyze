edf suspends nuclear fuel renewal at tricastin 2 after anomaly
paris feb reuters french utility edf said on monday that it had suspended the removal of spent nuclear fuel from its megawatt tricastin nuclear reactor after a technical anomaly was detected during the process
tricastin has been offline since for statutory maintenance and renewal of some of its nuclear fuel rods
it was expected to resume production on april
edf said the nuclear building was shut down as a preventive measure after the issue was detected while experts and edf teams work to resolve the issue
it said french nuclear regulator asn and the government were informed about the event which it said had no impact on the environment or the safety of the facility
