venezuela to revise bilateral relations with eu govts that recognize guaido
caracas feb reuters venezuela s government said on monday it would revise bilateral relations with european union member states that have recognized opposition leader juan guaido as legitimate
the government expresses its most energetic rejection of the decision adopted by some european governments in which they officially submit to the administration s strategy to overthrow the legitimate government of president nicolas maduro it said in a statement
