iran's top judge says eu's preconditions for new non-dollar trade channel "unacceptable"
dubai feb reuters iran s top judge said on monday that tehran would never accept the humiliating conditions set by the european union for trade with iran to avert sanctions iran s tasnim news agency reported
after nine months of procrastination and negotiation europeans have created a mechanism with limited capacity only for food and medicine tasnim quoted sadeq as saying
iran will never accept their strange and humiliating conditions of joining the fatf the financial action task force and negotiations on its missile france germany and britain have opened a new channel for trade with iran to avert sanctions reimposed on iran after president donald trump s decision in may to exit a nuclear deal between iran and six major powers
