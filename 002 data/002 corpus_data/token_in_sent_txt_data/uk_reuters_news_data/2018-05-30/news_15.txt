greece gets five expressions of interest in hellenic petroleum sale
athens may reuters greece received five expressions of interest for a majority stake in its biggest oil refiner hellenic petroleum the country s privatisations agency said on wednesday
initial interest was submitted by alrai group holdings limited a consortium comprising carbon asset management and alshaheen group gupta family group alliance glencore energy uk and vitol holding greece and paneuropean oil and industrial holding are jointly selling a stake of at least percent in the refiner
the deadline for making submissions expired at gmt on wednesday
