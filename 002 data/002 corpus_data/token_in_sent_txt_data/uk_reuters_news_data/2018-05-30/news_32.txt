exclusive-india's reliance to halt oil imports from iran from oct-nov - sources
new delhi may reuters india s reliance industries ltd owner of the world s biggest refining complex is planning to halt oil imports from iran two sources familiar with the matter said in a first sign that new sanctions are forcing buyers to shun oil purchases from tehran
reliance s call to quit buying oil from iran followed washington s decision to abandon the nuclear accord which has raised the threat of sanctions on entities dealing with iran after periods of three to six months expire
