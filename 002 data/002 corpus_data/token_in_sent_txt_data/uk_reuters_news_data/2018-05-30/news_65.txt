china southern power reports record power demand this month
beijing may reuters china southern power grid co ltd reported record power demand this month the government said on wednesday the latest sign that china s power market is already under pressure as weather forecasters predict a summer
the state power company has increased its load to a record of gigawatts this month up percent from the previous peak load and an increase of percent the national development reform commission said in a statement
it is the first time the peak load has reached a new high before the official start of the northern hemisphere summer on june when power demand surges as a result of air conditioner use
china southern power is one of two power transmission firms covering five provinces in the south including yunnan hainan guangxi guangdong and guizhou with the rest of the country covered by the state grid an unusually warm spring and forecasts of a summer are expected to boost electricity demand as households turn up their units or fans to keep cool
coal prices have soared as investors have bet on potential supply tightness due to strong demand from the nation s power stations
coal accounts for the majority of china s power generation
some percent of households in china have air conditioning according to the international energy agency which warned in a report published earlier this month that the rising use of cooling systems is the most critical blind spot in the energy debate
