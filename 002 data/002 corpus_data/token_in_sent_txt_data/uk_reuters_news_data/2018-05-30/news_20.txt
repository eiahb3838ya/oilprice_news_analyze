new reuters india s reliance industries ltd owner of the world s biggest refining complex plans to halt oil imports from iran two sources familiar with the matter said in a sign that new sanctions are forcing buyers to shun oil purchases from tehran
reliance s move expected to take effect in october or november came after president donald trump abandoned a nuclear agreement this month and ordered the reimposition of sanctions on tehran

reliance whose modern refining complex at jamnagar in gujarat can process about million barrels per day bpd of crude has told officials of the national iranian oil co nioc that the firm would stop oil imports from tehran in october or november one of the sources said
