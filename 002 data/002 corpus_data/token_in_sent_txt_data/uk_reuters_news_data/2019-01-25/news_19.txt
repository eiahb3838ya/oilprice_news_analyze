edf says strike extended at cordemais 5 coal power plant until feb. 4
paris reuters french utility edf said on friday that a strike at its megawatt mw cordemais power plant will continue until reducing power output
production at cordemais was currently zero it said in a website update
workers have been protesting against plans to shut the unit as part of a government pledge to exit electricity production from coal
the government this week said it will study the possibility of converting cordemais coal power generators to burn biomass
