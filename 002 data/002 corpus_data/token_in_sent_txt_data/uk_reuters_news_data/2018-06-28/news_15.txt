officials at libya's zueitina oil port not authorising noc tripoli tanker - sources
benghazi libya june reuters officials at libya s zueitina oil port have not authorised a tanker contracted by national oil corporation noc to berth a port and industry sources said on thursday
zueitina is one of the oil ports that the libyan national army lna said it would transfer to a parallel noc which is based in the eastern city of benghazi after fighting this month at the ras lanuf and es sider terminals
