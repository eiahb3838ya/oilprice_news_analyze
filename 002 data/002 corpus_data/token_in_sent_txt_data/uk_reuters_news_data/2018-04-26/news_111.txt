indonesia finmin tells people to be calmer in response to rupiah weakness
jakarta april reuters indonesia s finance minister said on thursday that people should be calmer in response to the rupiah trading near its lowest in more than two years and that exporters should make use of the currency weakness to boost sales
people should be better informed so they are calmer that this movement in the currency market was coming from america sri mulyani indrawati said explaining that the rupiah s weakness against the dollar was the result of rising treasury yields and fiscal and monetary policies
the rupiah touched per dollar in earlier trading on thursday the weakest since january
she maintained her outlook for indonesia s budget deficit at percent of gdp despite the rupiah s depreciation and said that the deficit could even be lower as she expected increased revenues from the oil sector
