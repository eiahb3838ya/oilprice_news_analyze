pence, venezuela's guaido to meet in bogota on monday -pence aide
washington feb reuters vice president mike pence plans to meet with venezuela s opposition leader juan guaido in bogota on monday on the sidelines of a meeting of the lima group of regional leaders a pence aide said on saturday
the meeting comes after troops loyal to venezuela s president nicolas maduro blocked convoys of humanitarian aid from crossing the border on saturday and is aimed at showing continued support for guaido who most western nations recognize as venezuela s legitimate leader
