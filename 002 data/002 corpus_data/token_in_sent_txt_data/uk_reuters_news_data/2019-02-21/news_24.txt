crews to abandon 2 venezuelan tankers stuck in portugal -operator
lisbon feb reuters the crew aboard a venezuelan oil tanker that has been stuck in the middle of the river tagus in portugal s capital lisbon for nearly two years due to unpaid debt is set to be dismissed managers bernhard schulte shipmanagement bsm said on thursday
bsm also said in a statement sent to reuters that another venezuelan tanker the parnaso which is in a dry dock at the port of setubal south of lisbon will also see its crew removed later this week due to a lack of payment from the owners pdv marina a subsidiary of venezuela s oil company pdvsa
