libya's noc concerned by developments at el feel oilfield - spokesperson
tunis feb reuters libya s national oil corporation noc said on thursday it was concerned by developments at the el feel oilfield after forces said they had taken control of the field
noc is concerned about the developments near el feel oilfield and is monitoring the situation closely to ensure the safety of its staff an noc spokesperson said
