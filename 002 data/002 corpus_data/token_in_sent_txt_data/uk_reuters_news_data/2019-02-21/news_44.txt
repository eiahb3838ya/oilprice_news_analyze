nigeria opposition candidate atiku abubakar would continue niger delta amnesty programme - policy advisor
abuja feb reuters nigeria s main opposition presidential candidate atiku abubakar would continue the country s niger delta amnesty programme a policy advisor told reuters on thursday
the controversial programme gives stipends to former militants in the delta region
incumbent president muhammadu buhari sought to overhaul the programme early in his tenure triggering a renewed series of attacks on oil facilities but later continued with the existing arrangement
reporting by paul carsten additional reporting by tife owolabi in yenagoa
