venezuelan protesters burn tyres at statue in chavez birthplace
barinas venezuela reuters protesters vandalised the most important statue of venezuela s former leader hugo chavez on thursday in his birthplace of sabaneta the latest outbreak of discontent over the opec member s economic and political crisis
demonstrators took to a town square before dawn witnesses said burning tyres around the granite sculpture of a chavez after several days of protests in the region over power cuts and erratic food supplies
since the socialist leader s death in venezuela s opposition has held several waves of protests against his successor president nicolas maduro
there have been various attacks on symbols of chavismo with one statute having been taken down
workers at the statue in sabaneta said it was not badly damaged
