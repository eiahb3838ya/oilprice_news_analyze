brazil's eletropaulo to raise $500 mln for investment, pay debt
sao paulo march reuters brazil s eletropaulo the country s largest power distribution company plans to raise million for investments and debt repayment according to documents seen by reuters on thursday
the company whose two largest shareholders are brazil s development bank bndes and power company aes corp plans to set up a subsidiary abroad as a vehicle to tap international capital markets according to documents the firm sent to brazil s power sector regulator aneel
eletropaulo needs aneel s authorization to establish the subsidiary which would issue the bonds and send the proceeds to the parent company in brazil
once capitalized eletropaulo would use the proceeds to fund investments in power distribution and to pay back debt said the company in the document requesting permission to set up the subsidiary
