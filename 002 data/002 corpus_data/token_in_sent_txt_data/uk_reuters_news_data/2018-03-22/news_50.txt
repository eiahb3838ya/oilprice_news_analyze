update 1-bmw says electric car mass production not viable until 2020
frankfurt reuters bmw will not mass produce electric cars until because its current technology is not profitable enough to scale up for volume production the chief executive said on thursday
bmw unveiled its first battery electric car in and has been working on different generations of battery software and electric motor technology since then
the roadster model due to hit showrooms in may is equipped with what bmw calls its electric drive technology
advances in battery raw materials and chemistry has increased its range by percent over the previous version bmw said
bmw is working to make electric car technology more modular and scaleable to make mass production commercially viable
we wanted to wait for the fifth generation to be much more cost competitive chief executive harald krueger told analysts in munich
we do not want to scale up with the fourth the cost advantage between bmw s fourth and fifth generation electric vehicle technology was a two digit number in percent terms krueger said
he did not offer precise figures
if you want to win the race you must be the most cost competitive in the segment otherwise you can not scale up the volume he said
bmw is working on a sixth generation of its technology and is investing a euro amount in battery cell research to better understand mass production the company said
bmw makes electric cars at plants across the world but has focussed mainly on hybrids which combine combustion engines with battery powered electric motors rather than vehicles reliant solely on batteries for their power
bmw said it wanted to add new electrified models by of which vehicles will be fully battery electric variants
thanks to a new production method available from onwards bmw will be able to make all its cars with pure electric hybrid and combustion engined variants
in bmw will start making a fully electric mini at its plant on the outskirts of the british city of oxford and will start production of a battery electric version of the offroader in
bmw is increasing the number of factories where it makes the offroader
this year it will start making the in china and south africa in addition to spartanburg in the united states
bmw has chosen contemporary amperex technology co ltd catl as its partner in china for battery cell production krueger said
