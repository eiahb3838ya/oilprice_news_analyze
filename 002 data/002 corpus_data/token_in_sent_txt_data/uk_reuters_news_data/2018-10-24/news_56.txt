turkey will not allow greek interference in its east med activities - minister
ankara oct reuters turkey will not allow greece to interfere in its activities in the eastern mediterranean defence minister hulusi akar said on wednesday after ankara said last week a greek frigate had harassed a turkish energy exploration ship in the region
in an interview with anadolu agency akar said no project was possible in the eastern mediterranean without the involvement of turkey and northern cyprus a breakaway state only recognised by ankara
