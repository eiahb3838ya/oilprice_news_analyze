trump calls court decision on keystone pipeline political, a disgrace
washington nov reuters president donald trump on friday blasted a federal court decision halting the construction of the keystone xl pipeline saying the judge in montana had made a political decision and calling it a disgrace
district court judge brian morris ruled late thursday that the government did not complete a full analysis of the environmental impact of the project
