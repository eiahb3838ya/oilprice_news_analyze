update 1-kuwait public works minister resigns after damaging flood
cairo reuters kuwait s minister of public works hussam resigned on friday following heavy rainfall that caused damaging flooding across the country state news agency kuna reported on friday
out of my moral responsibility
i have submitted my resignation to the prime minister said
the army and national guard were mobilized to help clear water from critical facilities and roadways and the kuwait oil company announced a state of emergency kuna reported
no deaths or injuries were reported
parliament speaker marzouq said a meeting would be held on sunday to discuss the impact of the flooding kuna reported
