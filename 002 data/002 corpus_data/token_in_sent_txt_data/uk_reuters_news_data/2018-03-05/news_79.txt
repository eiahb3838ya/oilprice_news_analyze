senior iranian military official jazayeri says no official has the right to discuss iran's missile work with foreign powers - tasnim
tehran reuters iran will press on with its missile programme regardless of western pressure to halt it a senior military commander was quoted on monday as saying
iran s missile programme will continue and foreign powers have no right to intervene on this issue the tasnim news agency quoted iranian armed forces spokesman masoud jazayeri as saying
he also said no iranian official had permission to discuss this issue with foreigners tasnim reported
france s foreign minister was in iran on monday to reaffirm europe s commitment to a nuclear deal reached between iran and six major powers but also to echo concerns raised by president donald trump who has threatened to quit the accord
