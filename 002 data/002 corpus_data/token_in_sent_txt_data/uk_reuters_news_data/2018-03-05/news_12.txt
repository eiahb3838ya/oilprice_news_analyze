ceraweek - opec planning 'workshop' on companies that operate in member countries, u.s. shale fields -nigerian energy minister
houston reuters oil producers that operate in shale fields and opec member nations should do more to help stabilize crude prices nigerian oil minister emmanuel ibe kachikwu said on monday
the organization of the petroleum exporting countries plans a workshop with companies that operate in both areas kachikwu said on the sidelines of the ceraweek energy conference in houston
exxon mobil corp chevron corp and royal dutch shell plc have large shale operations and also operate in nigeria
some of the same companies that are working in shale are the same companies working in opec kachikwu said
we need to begin to look at companies that are very active in these areas and begin to get them to take some responsibilities in terms of stability of oil reporting by ernest scheyder editing by cynthia osterman
