nigeria draft oil reforms seek to establish powerful industry regulator
abuja may reuters nigeria s government plans to create a powerful energy regulator with broad oversight of the oil and gas sector according to draft versions of sweeping reforms known collectively as the petroleum industry bill pib
the draft laws were posted on the nigerian legislature s website on april and are the versions intended for the senate the upper house of parliament
