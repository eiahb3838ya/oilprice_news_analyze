oil minister says iran needs foreign investment, but can survive without it - tv
ankara may reuters iran s oil minister told state television on thursday that foreign investment was needed to develop its oil industry but that it could survive if foreigners decided to stay away after the decision to pull out of a multinational nuclear deal
if foreigners invest in iran it will accelerate the development of our oil sector but if not we will not die said bijan zanganeh
