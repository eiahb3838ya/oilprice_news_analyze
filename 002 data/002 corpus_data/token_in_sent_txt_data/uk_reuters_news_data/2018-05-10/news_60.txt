24 killed, around 2,000 affected by kenyan dam burst - governor
nairobi may reuters floodwater from a dam that burst after days of torrential rain in kenya s rift valley killed at least people as it swept through homes the local governor said on thursday
nakuru county governor lee kinyanjui told reuters as many as people had been affected by the rupturing of the dam on wednesday night
engineers had been sent to carry out safety checks on three other nearby reservoirs
