iran says u.s. exit from nuclear deal will not have any impact on its oil
ankara may reuters iranian oil minister bijan zanganeh said on thursday that president donald trump s decision to quit a multinational nuclear deal would not affect tehran s oil exports
trump s decision will not have any impact on our oil export that era is history now he told state television
trump announced the withdrawal from the nuclear deal on tuesday and said he was preparing new sanctions against iran
