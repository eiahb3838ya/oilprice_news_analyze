turkey's erdogan tells iran's rouhani that u.s. decision on nuclear deal wrong - turkish source
ankara may reuters turkish president tayyip erdogan on thursday told his iranian counterpart hassan rouhani that he found the decision to withdraw from a nuclear deal with iran to be wrong a source in erdogan s office said
in a phone call erdogan also told rouhani that turkey wanted the deal to be maintained after president donald trump on tuesday announced that the united states would pull out from the deal
reporting by ece toksabay and tuvan gumrukcu
