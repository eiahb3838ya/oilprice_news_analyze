edf's flamanville reactor could suffer further delays - regulator
paris jan reuters french utility edf s nuclear reactor under construction in flamanville northern france could suffer further delays if certain weldings need to be redone said the country s asn nuclear regulator on tuesday
at the asn s annual review of nuclear safety the regulator s new chief bernard doroszczuk said eight weldings on pipes between the reactor building and the turbine building were hard to access and that there was a problem with one of these eight weldings
if the asn were to rule that those least accessible weldings need to be redone it is possible that edf s calendar may not be respected doroszczuk told reporters
