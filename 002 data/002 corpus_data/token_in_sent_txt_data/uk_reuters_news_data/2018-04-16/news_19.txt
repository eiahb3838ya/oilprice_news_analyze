fortum gets contract to raise finland's solar power capacity by 30 pct
oslo reuters finland s fortum a majority utility company won a tender to increase finland s total solar production capacity by nearly percent the firm said on monday
fortum will install a solar electricity system on rooftops of about commercial buildings across finland
even though their combined output will be of only megawatts mw the project will be the biggest ever of its kind in the nordics
the total solar power production capacity in finland neared mw by the end of and adding the new installations will increase the country s output by about percent
the installation work will start in spring and will be completed during autumn the company said in a statement without giving the value of the deal
