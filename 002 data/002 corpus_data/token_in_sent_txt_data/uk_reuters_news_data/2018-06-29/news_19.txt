libya's noc sees force majeure at zueitina and hariga ports from july 1
tunis june reuters libya s national oil corporation said on friday it anticipated declaring force majeure on loadings from the eastern ports of zueitina and hariga from july
the move would cause an output loss of barrels per day bpd increasing the total disruption loss at eastern ports to bpd the noc based in tripoli said in a statement
the noc said blockages at the ports may also force sarir refinery to cease operations which would restrict local fuel supplies
