south africa cannot afford major nuclear expansion - top anc official
johannesburg july reuters south africa can not afford expansion of its nuclear power fleet but would still be open to future nuclear deals with russia african national congress treasurer general paul mashatile said on thursday
mashatile said the government would not take the big bang approach to nuclear expansion
he was speaking at a business breakfast on the sidelines of a brics summit in johannesburg
