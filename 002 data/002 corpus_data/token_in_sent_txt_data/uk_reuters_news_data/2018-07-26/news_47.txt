south africa's ruling party wants private investment in eskom
johannesburg july reuters south africa s ruling party wants greater private investment in struggling power utility eskom african national congress treasurer general paul mashatile said on thursday
mashatile also said the ruling party was holding discussions about splitting up eskom s operations
he was speaking during a business breakfast on the sideline of a brics summit in johannesburg
eskom reported a billion rand million loss for the financial year which ended in march
