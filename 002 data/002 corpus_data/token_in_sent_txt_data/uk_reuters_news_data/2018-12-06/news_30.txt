bad weather keeps eastern libyan oil ports closed
benghazi libya dec reuters the eastern libyan oil ports of es sider ras lanuf brega and zueitina were still closed on thursday because of bad weather port and shipping sources said
libyan state oil company noc had said on wednesday that all the country s oil export terminals were closed by bad weather
