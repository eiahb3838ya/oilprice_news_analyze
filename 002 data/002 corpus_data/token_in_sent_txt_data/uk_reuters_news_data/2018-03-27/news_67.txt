edf to invest 8 billion euros in power storage
paris march reuters french utility edf plans to invest billion euros billion between and to become a european market leader in electricity storage the company said on tuesday
edf said in a statement it will develop gigawatts of additional storage capacity worldwide by in addition to the gw it already operates
edf which already operates pumped storage hydropower plants and power storage batteries added that it aimed to become a french and european market leader with an offer of storage batteries for clients in the retail power sector
it also aims to develop a portfolio of about million clients in africa by through local partnerships
with storage we can smooth out the intermittence of renewable energy and guarantee the performance and balance of the grids edf chief executive levy told reporters
