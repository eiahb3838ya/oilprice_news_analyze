german consumer protection body to present class action against vw
berlin sept reuters german consumer protection association vzbz said on tuesday it would along with auto club adac on wednesday present a class action against volkswagen over the carmaker s manipulation of emissions software
vzbz wants to get compensation for owners of volkswagen diesel cars that are not as environmentally friendly as the company says
that could affect more than million diesel car owners
