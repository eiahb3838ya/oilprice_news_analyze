tata power to offer suite of services in rooftop solar drive
to offer consultancy installation maintenance to offer cheap finance options through tata capital will reach out to customers through dealer network by promit mukherjee mumbai sept reuters india s tata power company ltd plans to offer a range of services from advice and financing to installation and maintenance as it strives to increase its share of the market for rooftop solar panels a senior executive said on tuesday
tata power solar the renewable energy arm of tata power is currently india s biggest rooftop solar panel supplier
but with a market share of just percent it sees plenty of scope for growth and aims to leverage its position as an integrated solar power company with a presence in manufacturing engineering construction and maintenance to offer customers an service
we will give the benefit of low cost solar panels to our consumers and also help them save money with other services praveer sinha chief executive officer and managing director of tata power told reuters
ashish khanna ceo of tata power solar added the services would include consultancy sourcing of solar panels installation and maintenance of rooftop structures as well as low cost financing and liaising with distributors
he said the company would offer low cost financing through tata group s financing arm tata capital
india has plans to install up to gigawatts gw of renewable energy capacity by out of which gw is expected to come from rooftop solar panels
so far however the pace of rooftop installations has been just around percent of the target according to government figures
tata power solar is also planning to launch a dealer network to better take on its myriad of competitors which include chinese companies
it plans to have master dealers across nine states with each having five sales people under them said a senior executive who did not want to be named as he is not authorised to speak to the media
