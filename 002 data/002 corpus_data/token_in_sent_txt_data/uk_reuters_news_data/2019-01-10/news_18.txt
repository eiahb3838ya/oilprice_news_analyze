germany's bosch to pay $130 mln to settle u.s. diesel emissions claims
jan reuters german auto supplier robert bosch gmbh will pay million to settle claims from fiat chrysler automobiles nv diesel owners and resolve an investigation by state attorneys general into its involvement with diesel vehicles
bosch and its unit must pay million to states the district of columbia and puerto rico and guam and million to fiat chrysler diesel owners
bosch must refuse to accommodate requests for software development and programming that could result in the installation of defeat device software the new york attorney general s office said as part of the settlement
bosch which did not immediately comment is also making a million payment to a state attorneys general group
