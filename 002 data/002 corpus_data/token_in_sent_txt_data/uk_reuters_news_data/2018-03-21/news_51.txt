update 1-nigeria parliament aims to pass 2018 budget on april 24 after delays
adds detail background abuja march reuters the nigerian parliament aims to pass the country s budget on april lower house speaker yakubu dogara said following several months of delay
president muhammadu buhari presented a budget of trillion naira the country s biggest ever to lawmakers in november and was targeting for its passage
but government agencies and department had been slow in defending their spending plan before lawmakers which prompted the president to intervene
dogara said the budget would be laid out on april and passed the following week
both houses of parliament had agreed to the dates he added
last year s budget passed in may was delayed for months due to disagreements between lawmakers and the presidency cutting the supply of government money and deepening an economic crisis
