update 1-yemen's houthis fire missile at aramco facility, saudi says it falls in desert
riyadh reuters yemen s armed houthi movement launched a ballistic missile at a saudi aramco facility in saudi arabia s southwestern jizan province on monday the group s tv reported but a coalition said the rocket fell in the open desert
the houthis an group that holds much of yemen including the capital sanaa have fired a series of missiles into the kingdom in recent months part of a conflict in yemen widely seen as a proxy battle between saudi arabia and iran
a military alliance intervened in yemen s civil war in to fight against the houthis on behalf of the government of president abd rabbu mansour who lives in exile in riyadh
iran and the houthis dismiss saudi accusations that tehran arms the group
coalition spokesman turki said saudi air defence forces had tracked the rocket but that it landed in an uninhabited area according to a report from the official saudi press agency
the houthis fired a salvo of missiles at riyadh last week with one falling short of the capital and another intercepted by saudi air defence forces malki said at the time
