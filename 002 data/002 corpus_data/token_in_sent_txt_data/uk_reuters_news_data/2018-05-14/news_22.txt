iraqi pm abadi says ready to work with election winners
baghdad may reuters iraqi prime minister haider called on monday for all political blocs to respect the results of his nation s parliamentary election and said he was ready to work with the winning blocs to form a government free of corruption
we are ready to work and cooperate in forming the strongest government for iraq free of corruption abadi said in a live televised address
