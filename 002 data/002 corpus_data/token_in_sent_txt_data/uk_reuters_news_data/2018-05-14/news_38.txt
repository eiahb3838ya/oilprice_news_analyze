yemen's houthis say they fired missile at saudi aramco centre
riyadh may reuters yemen s armed houthi movement launched a missile at a distribution centre belonging to oil giant saudi aramco in saudi arabia s southwestern jizan province on monday the group s tv reported
the houthis an group that holds much of yemen including the capital sanaa have fired a series of missiles into the kingdom in recent months part of a conflict in yemen widely seen as a proxy battle between saudi arabia and iran
there was no immediate confirmation of the report from saudi authorities
