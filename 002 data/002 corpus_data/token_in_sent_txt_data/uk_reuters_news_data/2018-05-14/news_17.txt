mnangagwa says china's sinosteel to invest $1 bln in zimbabwe
harare may reuters zimbabwean president emmerson mnangagwa said on monday that chinese firm sinosteel corporation had agreed to invest billion in zimbabwe by building a power plant and increasing ferrochrome output
sinosteel president andong liu said the chinese company planned to increase ferrochrome output at zimasco in which it holds a majority stake by tonnes per year to tonnes over the next five years
sinosteel also plans to build a megawatt coalbed power plant in western zimbabwe the firm s president added at a news conference with mnangagwa
