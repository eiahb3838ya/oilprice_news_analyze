iran says saudi arabia, russia violated opec deal on output cuts - shana
london oct reuters iran s opec governor said on wednesday that saudi arabia and russia have violated opec s agreement on output cuts by producing more crude adding that the two countries would not be able to produce enough oil to replace a reduction in iranian output
a set of sanctions targeting iran s oil industry is due to take effect in november
hossein kazempour ardebili was quoted as saying by shana the oil ministry s news agency that sanctions on iranian oil were impossible and called on president donald trump to return to a nuclear deal with tehran
