ophir energy says optimistic can agree fortuna deal by year-end
cape town reuters ophir energy is cautiously optimistic it will meet a looming deadline for a final investment decision and save its stalled fortuna flng project in equatorial guinea a senior official said on thursday
the pioneering liquefied natural gas project in equatorial guinea bogged down by delayed financing ran into further trouble in may when oil services company schlumberger pulled out of the venture
we are continuing to pursue debt financing with chinese banks and that s with a view to reaching fid final investment decision by the end of oliver quinn director of exploration in africa told reuters
