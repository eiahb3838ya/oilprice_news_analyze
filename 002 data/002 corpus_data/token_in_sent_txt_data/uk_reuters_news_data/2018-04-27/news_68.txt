china state gas producers must sign supply contracts by end-april - ndrc
beijing april reuters china s natural gas producers are required to sign supply contracts with major users by the end of this month an official from the country s top economic planner said on friday
china s gas demand will maintain strong growth this year amid a continued gasification drive and an improving economy zhao chenxi a spokesman for the national development and reform commission ndrc told a press conference in beijing
