russia's finmin proposes raising max level of export duty for motor fuel
moscow june reuters russia s finance ministry will propose increasing the maximum level of export duty for light oil products to percent of the tariff on the equivalent amount of crude oil alexey sazanov the head of the tax department in the ministry told reporters in moscow on thursday
the government will be able to use this mechanism in future in case of sharp growth in motor fuel prices in the domestic market he added
