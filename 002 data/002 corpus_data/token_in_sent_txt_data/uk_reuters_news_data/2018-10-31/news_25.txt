reuters summit-cenovus eyes option for expansion of crude-by-rail deals
calgary alberta reuters canadian oil producer cenovus energy inc is considering further expansion of its agreements to move crude through canada s two major railways its chief executive officer said on wednesday
cenovus could expand deals to transport barrels per day bpd in total via canadian national railway co and canadian pacific railway ltd by a further bpd ceo alex pourbaix said in an interview at the reuters global commodities summit
reporting by rod nickel and julie gordon in calgary alberta editing by leslie adler
