cenovus sees crude differentials to ease out by mid-2019
oct reuters canadian oil and gas producer cenovus energy inc expects the spread between western canadian select wcs and benchmark crude wti to significantly ease out by mid chief executive officer alex pourbaix said on a call
cenovus also expects crude by rail to reach barrels per day by the end of this year the company said on a call with analysts on wednesday
last month the company signed deals with canada s two major railways to transport roughly barrels per day from northern alberta to the gulf coast starting in the fourth quarter
reporting by laharee chatterjee in bengaluru
