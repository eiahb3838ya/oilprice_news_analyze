portugal pm says not opposed to likely chinese bid for edp
lisbon may reuters the portuguese government has no objections to a potential bid by chinese investors to take control of the country s largest utility de portugal prime minister antonio costa told reporters on friday
earlier stock market regulator cmvm suspended trading in edp shares after sources and media reports said that a chinese group led by edp s largest shareholder china three gorges was set to make a takeover bid for the firm
the government has nothing against it no reservations costa said adding though that the government does not have to be consulted
the chinese have been good investors be it in ren edp or in other sectors
the important thing is that shareholders can ponder on the project
