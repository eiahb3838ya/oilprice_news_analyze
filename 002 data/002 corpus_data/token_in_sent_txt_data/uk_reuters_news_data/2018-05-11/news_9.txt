white house urges 'responsible nations' to press iran to change behavior
washington may reuters the white house said on friday iran s reckless actions pose a threat to regional security and urged other countries to press tehran to change its behavior
it is time for responsible nations to bring pressure on iran to change this dangerous behavior the white house said in a statement
it cited rockets fired at golan heights from syria and a missile fired by yemen s houthi rebels at the saudi capital riyadh this week
