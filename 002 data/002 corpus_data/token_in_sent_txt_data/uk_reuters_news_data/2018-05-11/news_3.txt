china three gorges launches bid for portugal edp at small premium
lisbon may reuters china s utility china three gorges on friday launched a bid to take control of portugal s largest company edp in which it is the largest shareholder with a percent stake offering a premium of almost percent on edp s closing price
the company said it seeks to reach at least a percent voting stake plus one share in the company
it offered euros a share for edp
it also offered euros a share for edp s wind power unit edp renovaveis which is less than its closing price of euros
edp s market capitalisation was nearly billion euros as of friday
the company serves nearly million electricity market clients and million natural gas customers and has over km of power transmission lines
the portuguese government has no objections to the bid prime minister antonio costa told reporters earlier
