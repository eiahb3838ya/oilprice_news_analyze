thousands protest in armenian capital as sarksyan approved as pm
yerevan april reuters tens of thousands of demonstrators rallied in the centre of the armenian capital on tuesday to protest as parliament cleared a path for former president serzh sarksyan to become prime minister in the former soviet republic
sarksyan was president from and demonstrators said he was switching jobs but clinging to power
under a revised constitution armenia s prime minister will hold power while the presidency becomes largely ceremonial
