e.on says all turbines linked to grid at uk offshore wind farm rampion
london reuters all turbines at top german utility s rampion offshore wind farm in britain are now able to generate electricity and are delivering power to the grid the company said on tuesday
the project is being built off the coast of sussex in southeast england by uk green investment rampion ltd and canadian energy infrastructure company enbridge
over the coming months work will continue both on and offshore to prepare the site for full operation later this year said in a statement
the farm will generate electricity to supply homes equivalent to around half of the households in sussex
in march british wind power output hit a record high at gigawatts accounting for about percent of domestic electricity generation
wind power is needed to bridge a supply gap as old nuclear plants and power stations close
