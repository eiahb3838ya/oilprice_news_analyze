endeavor energy to build thermal power plant in guinea
abidjan march reuters endeavor energy has started construction on a million thermal power plant in guinea it said in a statement
the te power project is expected to come online in the capital conakry in and is being developed jointly by majority stakeholder endeavor and mauritanian company energie environnement et mines which will have a four percent stake
the million comprises million in equity from endeavor and million in debt financing from the overseas private investment corporation opic and cdc group plc cdc
the te power thermal plant will increase power supply by as much as ten percent and help guinea meet its growing energy demand said opic president and ceo ray washburne in the statement
the west african country suffers from a shortage of power supply which has contributed to unrest in some mining regions over the past year
