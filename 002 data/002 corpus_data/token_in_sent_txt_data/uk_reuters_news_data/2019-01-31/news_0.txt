interview-change of government favors china and russia, venezuela's guaido says
caracas jan reuters a change in government in venezuela would favor the country s two main foreign creditors russia and china venezuelan opposition leader and interim president juan guaido told reuters in an interview on thursday
guaido said he had send communications to both countries allies of president nicolas maduro
what most suits russia and china is the country s stability and a change of government he said
guaido also said venezuela would be responsible to its creditors and bondholders
