the company s decision adds to pdvsa s woes after the united states imposed sanctions on the company on to undermine the rule of venezuelan president nicolas maduro
however dropping the company was not a reaction to the sanctions but follows the deteriorating financial status of pdvsa over the past few years said two of the sources both executives with china national petroleum corp the parent of petrochina
there will be no role of pdvsa as an equity partner
at least we don t see that possibility in the near future given the situation the country has been through in recent years said one of the executives asking to remain unidentified because he is not authorized to speak to the media
