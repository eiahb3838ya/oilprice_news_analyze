former vietnam politburo member jailed for 18 years over $35 mln losses
hanoi march reuters a vietnamese court jailed on thursday a former politburo member for years after he was convicted of financial irregularities at a enterprise adding to a sentence handed down separately in january state media reported
dinh la thang was found guilty of deliberate violation of state regulations on economic management the official radio voice of vietnam news website reported
calls to thang s lawyer went unanswered
court officials were not available for comment
thang was accused of wrongdoing in connection with losses of up to billion dong million in a petrovietnam investment in ocean commercial joint stock bank
ocean bank suffered heavy losses and was forcibly taken over in by the state bank of vietnam the central bank
thang the politician to have been sentenced for decades will also have to pay a fine of billion dong million voice of vietnam reported
the politburo is vietnam s top body
thang lost his position in the politburo last year after he came under suspicion of wrongdoing
in january thang was handed a sentence after being found guilty of economic mismanagement at a power plant overseen by energy firm petrovietnam of which he was chairman
the people s court of hanoi also handed sentences of between months of probation to years in prison to six other people for their involvement in the case at the trial starting on monday last week the report said
