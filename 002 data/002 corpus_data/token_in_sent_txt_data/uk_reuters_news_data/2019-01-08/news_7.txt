update 1-basf plans to shed assets to try to salvage solvay deal - sources
frankfurt reuters basf is looking to shed a sizeable portfolio of assets to try to salvage a deal it struck in to buy belgian chemicals group solvay s polyamide business people close to the matter said
the german company is working with investment bank lazard to find a buyer for the plastics assets which could be valued at about million euros million including debt the people said
information packages were sent out to potential buyers just before christmas and bids are expected by the end of the month they added
basf and lazard declined to comment while solvay was not immediately available for comment
to try to allay concerns raised by european union antitrust regulators basf offered in october to exclude parts of solvay s european polyamide business including innovation capabilities from the list of assets it plans to acquire
it also offered to exclude manufacturing assets of solvay s intermediate and engineering plastics business
the european commission has set a provisional deadline of for its ruling on the planned deal
according to people close to the matter basf is marketing the assets to companies that also took part in the auction for the solvay business including south korea s sk innovation china s kingfa and private equity group sk capital which owns peer ascend
the assets with estimated core earnings of about million euros and an expected enterprise value equity plus debt of times that also include engineering plastics that have whet the appetite of peers such as lanxess they added
basf is seeking to strengthen its nylon business and enhance its access to key growth markets in asia and south america with the solvay deal
the purchase would boosts its activities in nylon or polyamide a engineering plastic that is used in textiles and also industrial parts such as tube fittings cooling fans and engine air ducts
for solvay the sale is part of its drive to divest products and focus on specific applications in aerospace automotive and the oil and gas industries where it can achieve higher margins
eu commissioner margrethe vestager said last year that as only a few manufacturers provide essential inputs to produce different nylon products a careful assessment was needed whether the proposed acquisition would lead to higher prices
a main precursor material for polyamide is adn
the main suppliers of this material are ascend as well as butachimie a joint venture of solvay and invista a former dupont business now held by koch industries
