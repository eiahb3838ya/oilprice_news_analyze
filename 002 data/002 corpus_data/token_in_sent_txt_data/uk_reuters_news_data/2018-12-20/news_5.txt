eu countries agree to 30 pct cut of truck co2 emissions by 2030
brussels dec reuters ministers from european union countries agreed on thursday to reduce carbon dioxide emissions from trucks and buses by percent by the eu s austrian presidency said
the government representatives also agreed to an interim target of a percent reduction by relative to levels
the countries collectively known as the council will have to find a compromise in negotiations with the european parliament which envisages a tougher target of a percent cut
