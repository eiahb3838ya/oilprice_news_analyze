swiss bank bcp says halts all new business with iran
london may reuters swiss lender banque de commerce et de placements bcp has suspended new transactions with iran and is winding down activities with the country after president donald trump s pullout from the nuclear deal with tehran the bank said on tuesday
we have suspended any new transaction related to iran after may and started the wind down period within the framework of ofac announcement the bank said in a emailed statement to reuters referring to the treasury s sanctions enforcement arm
trump s withdrawal from the accord on may was announced in tandem with the of sanctions within days prompting several european companies to announce their exit from iran
