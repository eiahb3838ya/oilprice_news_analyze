brazilian farmer losses from truckers' strike reach 6.6 bln reais -cna
sao paulo may reuters brazil s cna farm lobby said on tuesday that producers have lost billion reais billion so far during a nationwide truckers protest over diesel prices that is now in its ninth day
cna said in a statement that its estimate reflected only primary production losses and did not take into consideration losses for agriculture processors industries and makers of inputs
cna said it estimates it will take producers six months to a year to recover from the protest which has paralyzed vast swaths of brazil s economy
