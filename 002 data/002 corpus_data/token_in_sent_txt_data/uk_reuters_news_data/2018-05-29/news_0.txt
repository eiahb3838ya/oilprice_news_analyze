brazil senate passes bill that includes tax exemption for diesel
brasilia may reuters brazil s senate passed a bill on tuesday that includes an exemption of diesel from the payroll and social security tax a pledge made by the government to striking truckers to lower the price of the fuel
president michel temer however is expected to line veto the diesel provision inserted by the lower chamber because it did not cut the price by the cents per liter agreed upon
the government will still have to find a way to deliver the price reduction
