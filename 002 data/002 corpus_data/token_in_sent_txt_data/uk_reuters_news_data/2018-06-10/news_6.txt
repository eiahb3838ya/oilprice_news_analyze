motor racing-gasly drops to back row in canada after engine penalty
montreal reuters toro rosso s pierre gasly will start the canadian formula one grand prix from the back row of the grid on sunday after being penalised for an engine change
the frenchman had qualified on saturday and drops to just ahead of compatriot romain grosjean who failed to set a timed lap in saturday s session when his haas had an engine failure
ferrari s sebastian vettel will start from pole position with mercedes driver valtteri bottas alongside the german on the front row
red bull s max verstappen who qualified third starts next to mercedes championship leader lewis hamilton who is chasing a seventh win on the island circuit gilles villeneuve
reporting by steve keating in montreal
