update 1-iran will never take part in one-sided talks with u.s. under threat - ministry
beirut reuters iran will never take part in negotiations with the united states under threat foreign ministry spokesman bahram qassemi said on wednesday
his comments appeared to be a reaction to president donald trump s statement on tuesday that he is ready to make a real deal with iran
america should forget forever the idea of negotiations under the shadow of a threat tasnim news agency quoted qassemi as saying
according to the irna news agency qassemi also said that iran has filed an official letter of objection to the swiss embassy in tehran which oversees diplomatic relations in the islamic republic
this concerned a speech made by secretary of state mike pompeo in california on sunday in which he compared iran s leaders to a mafia
