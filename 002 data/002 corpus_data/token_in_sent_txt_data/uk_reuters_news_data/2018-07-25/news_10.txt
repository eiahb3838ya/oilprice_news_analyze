yemen's houthis hit saudi oil tanker causing slight damage-saudi tv
dubai reuters yemen s houthi movement attacked a saudi oil tanker in the red sea causing slight damage saudi state television quoted the coalition as saying on wednesday
the statement followed an earlier claim by al masirah tv that the group had targeted a saudi arabian warship off the western coast of yemen
the saudi oil tanker was subjected to slight damage due to the attack by the houthi militia saudi state media said
