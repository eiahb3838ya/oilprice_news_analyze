germany condemns syria gas attack, says evidence points to assad
berlin april reuters germany on monday condemned the use of chemical weapons in douma a town in syria s eastern ghouta and said the circumstances pointed to the regime of syrian president bashar being responsible for the attack
the government condemns this new use of poison gas in the strongest terms german government spokesman steffen seibert told a news conference
the regime s actions are those responsible for the use of poison gas must be held to account he added
