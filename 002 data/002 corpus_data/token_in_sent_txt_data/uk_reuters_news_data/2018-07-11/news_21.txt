nigeria's president buhari says will soon sign african continental free trade agreement
abuja july reuters nigeria s president muhammadu buhari said on wednesday the country will soon sign up to an african continental free trade agreement
buhari made the comment while speaking at a press conference during a visit by south africa s president cyril ramaphosa
the agreement was signed by countries in march with south africa joining earlier this month
