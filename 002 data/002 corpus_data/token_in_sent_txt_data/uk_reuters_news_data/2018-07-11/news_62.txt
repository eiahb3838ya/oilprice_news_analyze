india's iranian oil imports in june fall 15.9 pct from may as sanctions fears bite - sources
new delhi july reuters india s oil imports from iran declined by percent in june the first month after the united states said it would reimpose sanctions on the country according to data from shipping and industry sources
in june india imported barrels per day bpd of oil from iran compared to bpd in may the data showed
the sources declined to be identified
india iran s top oil client after china has asked refiners to look for alternative oil as the nation may have to drastically cut imports from tehran to comply with the renewed sanctions
the united states said it would reimpose the sanctions after withdrawing from a agreement with iran russia china and several western european countries where iran agreed to curtail its nuclear activities in return for the lifting of earlier sanctions
