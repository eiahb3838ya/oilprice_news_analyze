brazil state fines anglo american 125.6 mln reais over pipeline leak
rio de janeiro april reuters the brazilian state of minas gerais slapped a million real million fine on anglo american on wednesday for pollution caused by one of two spills last month from a pipeline that carries iron ore from the company s mine
the leak in the pipeline which carries slurry to a port in rio de janeiro state occurred on march and forced the miner to suspend operations until march
a second leak in the same pipeline meters away and two days after the resumption prompted the company to again halt operations for days while it completes an inspection
the initial spill caused pollution and environmental degradation that resulted in damage to water resources and may have caused harm to public health and the wellbeing of the population minas gerais s environmental regulator semad said in a statement
semad also said it required the company to clean the stream where the spill occurred and resume water supply to the local population which was interrupted due to the incident
in a statement anglo said it was analyzing the notice from semad
the company said it took all measures to minimize the effects of the incidents on march and including cleaning the stream and being in contact with residents
the state watchdog also said it was still studying the second leak and would announce a fine in that case as well
reais reporting by marta noguiera and alexandra alper
