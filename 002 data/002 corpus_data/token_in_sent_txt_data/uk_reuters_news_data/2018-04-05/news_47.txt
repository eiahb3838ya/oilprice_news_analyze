six miners killed in georgia in roof collapse - interior ministry
tbilisi reuters six miners were killed and three injured on thursday when the roof of a coal mine in western georgia collapsed the country s interior ministry said
the ministry said in a statement that the accident had occurred at the mindeli mine in tkibuli km miles west of the capital tbilisi
the mine is operated by sakhnakhshiri a georgian company
ten miners have died at the mine in different accidents since
