iraq signs oil agreement with chevron - minister
basra iraq june reuters iraq has signed an agreement with chevron allowing the company to conduct scans and studies on oil sites and installations oil minister jabar said on wednesday
chevron has signed a memorandum of understanding mou with basra oil company and dhi qar oil company that also includes it helping the two companies improve their technical administrative and financial performances luaibi said at the signing ceremony
