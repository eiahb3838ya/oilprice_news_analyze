update 1-iraq starts exporting first crude oil cargo through iotc
reuters iraq has started shipping crude oil through its iraqi oil tankers company iotc for the first time since the oil ministry said on wednesday
the two million barrel cargo is destined for the united states the ministry said in a statement
a trading source familiar with the matter said valero chartered the vessel from iotc
iraq has formed a joint venture with a shipping company owned by arab states to transfer store and trade crude and oil products
the venture shipping services and oil trading aissot is owned by state firm iotc and arab maritime petroleum transport co amptc a company in which arab oil producers such as saudi arabia the united arab emirates and kuwait hold a share
reporting by ahmed aboulenein in baghdad and rania el gamal in riyadh editing by mark potter
