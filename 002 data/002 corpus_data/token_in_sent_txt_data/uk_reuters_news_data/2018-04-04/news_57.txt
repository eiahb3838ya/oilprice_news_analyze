four marine helicopter crew presumed dead after california crash
reuters a marine helicopter crashed during a training mission in southern california tuesday afternoon and all four crew members are believed to have died a marine spokeswoman announced
no details about the nature of the training mission were released other than it was routine and held in the desert in el centro about miles km east of san diego
the helicopter was a sikorsky super stallion from the marine aircraft wing based at the marine corps air station in miramar according to a statement from the marines
it is the largest and heaviest helicopter in the military
the accident is under investigation and no other information was available
the wreck is the deadliest marine accident since a cargo plane crash in the mississippi delta that killed marines in july
