chile's enap, argentina's ypf inaugurate $354 mln offshore gas project
santiago april reuters the energy firms of chile and argentina said on wednesday they had inaugurated a million project to increase production of natural gas off the southern tip of south america
the project operated by chile s enap in partnership with ypf will boost production of natural gas to million cubic metres daily from the current million while increasing petroleum production by nearly percent at the site the firms said in a statement
this ambitious project contributes to the supply of energy in argentina and bolsters regional integration enap general manager marcelo tokman said in the statement
the project which includes five oil platforms is at the mouth of the eastern end of the strait of magellan in argentina s santa cruz province
