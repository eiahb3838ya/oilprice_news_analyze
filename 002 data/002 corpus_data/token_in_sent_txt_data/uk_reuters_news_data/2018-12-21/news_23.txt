update 3-lebanon's hariri hopes government will be finalised on friday
beirut reuters lebanese prime saad said he hoped to finalise a new national unity government on friday after more than seven months of political wrangling
by the early evening there was still no sign of an announcement
lebanese politicians have indicated a government deal is close but have yet to declare a final agreement
