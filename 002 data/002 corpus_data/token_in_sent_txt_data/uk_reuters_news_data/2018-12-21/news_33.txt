update 1-russian opec coordinator marshavin to leave energy ministry
updates after energy ministry confirms marshavin is leaving moscow dec reuters roman marshavin one of the russian energy ministry officials who orchestrated the landmark deal with opec is leaving his job the energy ministry said friday
the ministry said marshavin was leaving the ministry this month to take another job but did not give more details
the order about marshavin s resignation is signed the ministry said in a statement sent to reuters
two government sources said marshavin may be taking up a role at the world bank
alexei gospodarev from the ministry of industry and trade may take over as head of the department for international cooperation the sources said
marshavin was part of the team headed by energy minister alexander novak which brokered a deal at the end of with the organization of the petroleum exporting countries on oil production cuts
he had lead the department since june
the deal helped the global oil market to rebalance while oil prices recovered
russian president vladimir putin honoured marshavin last year with russia s order of friendship
