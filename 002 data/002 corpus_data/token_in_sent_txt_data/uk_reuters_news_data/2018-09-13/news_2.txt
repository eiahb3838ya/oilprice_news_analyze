update 2-united airlines targets 50 pct cut in greenhouse gas emissions
chicago reuters united airlines said on thursday it has set a goal to cut its greenhouse gas emissions by percent over the next few decades to help reduce its carbon footprint and its dependence on fossil fuels
the air carrier will invest more than billion a year in more aircraft expanding its use of biofuels in daily flights and implementing ways to better conserve fuel
this is not only good for the environment but guards against oil price instability aaron stash a united manager of environmental strategy and sustainability told reporters
fuel costs account for a major portion of airlines expenses and rising oil prices over the past year have eaten in to industry profits sending carriers scrambling to mitigate the impact
while rivals delta air lines inc and american airlines group inc cut their earnings forecasts due to an oil price spike united raised its target in july as capacity and fare increases helped it recapture about percent of higher fuel costs
speaking at a separate conference on thursday united president scott kirby said the airline was marching toward recapturing percent of higher fuel expenditures in a year when those costs are already surpassing billion
still united said it wants to be the world s most environmentally conscious in it invested million in fulcrum bioenergy with an agreement to help the household maker install refineries near its hub airports and purchase roughly billion gallons of biofuel
on friday united will use gallons of biofuel at a blend with conventional jet fuel on flight from san francisco to zurich which it said will be the longest flight to date by a airline powered by that much biofuel
united said that halving its emissions by from levels as projected would be the equivalent of removing million vehicles from the road each year or the total number of cars in new york city and los angeles combined
overall the airline industry is still lagging its own climate targets on transatlantic routes despite steps to improve fuel efficiency according to a study by the international council on clean transportation released on wednesday
according to the study budget carrier norwegian air shuttle asa which operates new boeing dreamliners and max aircraft was ranked first of the transatlantic carriers for fuel efficiency
