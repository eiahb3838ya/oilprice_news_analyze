s.korean firms plead guilty to rigging u.s. military fuel prices -justice dept
washington nov reuters three south korean firms have agreed to plead guilty to criminal charges and pay about million in fines for their role in a conspiracy to rig the price of fuel sold to military bases across south korea the united states said on wednesday
the justice department said sk energy co ltd gs caltex corp and hanjin transportation co ltd also agreed to pay about million to the united states for civil antitrust and false claims related to the conspiracy
