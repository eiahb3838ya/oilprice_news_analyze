pg&e shares dive after utility flags impact from camp fire
nov reuters shares of the utility pg e corp fell percent after the company said if it was found liable for the camp fire raging in california the damages could be more than its insurance coverage and hurt cash flow and liquidity
the company drew down billion from its credit line in anticipation of a liability it said in a filing on late tuesday
no additional amounts are available under the utility s and pg e corp s respective revolving credit facilities the company said
the causes of the deadly camp and woolsey fires are under investigation
southern california edison and pg e reported to regulators they experienced problems with transmission lines or substations in areas around the time the blazes were reported to have started
