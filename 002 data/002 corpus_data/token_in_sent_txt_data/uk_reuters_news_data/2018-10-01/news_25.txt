indonesia says it needs more heavy equipment, rescuers in quake zone
jakarta oct reuters indonesia s disaster agency said more heavy equipment and rescue personnel were needed to help recover bodies after friday s quake and tsunami disaster
the agency had reported confirmed dead by monday most of them in the city of palu but the toll was expected to rise with many areas yet to be reached by rescuers
police were providing escorts for aid convoys to prevent supplies being intercepted
