hungary to invest $172 mln in angolan economy
luanda feb reuters hungary will invest million dollars in the angolan economy through various projects including a line of credit through its bank the european nation s trade minister said on wednesday in luanda
hungarian foreign and trade minister péter szjjárto said companies from hungary operating in angola mainly in the agriculture and energy sectors would make the investments resulting in a increase in bilateral trade according to state media agency angop
szjjárto was meeting with angolan bornito de sousa
