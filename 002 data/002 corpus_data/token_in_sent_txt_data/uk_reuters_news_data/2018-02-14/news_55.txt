france's rte keen to partake in eu grid consolidation
paris feb reuters french grid operator rte would be keen to partake in european union power grid consolidation and would be in favour of changing its legal status in order to achieve that its chief executive said on wednesday
most european power grids have been split off from the utilities that owned them more than a decade ago
however rte majority owned by french utility edf is one of a few independent transmission operators itos still owned by vertically integrated utilities
these ito firms are not allowed to buy stakes in independent power grid operators but rte chief executive francois brottes said he would be in favour of changing rte s legal status to be able to take part in any power grid consolidation
rte is not meant to be merely an observer in the consolidation of the european power grid industry brottes told reporters
he said there was no urgency in changing rte s legal status and that this was a project
he also said he was not too keen on an initial public offering ipo
