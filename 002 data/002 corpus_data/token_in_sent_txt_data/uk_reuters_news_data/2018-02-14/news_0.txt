update 1-brazil's odebrecht mulls selling part of stake in braskem -report
adds comment from odebrecht sao paulo feb reuters brazilian conglomerate odebrecht sa may sell part of its stake in petrochemical company braskem sa or try to get credit from banks using its stake as collateral brazilian newspaper valor economico reported on wednesday
odebrecht needs cash to pay debt maturing this year including million reais million in bonds according to the paper which cited unnamed sources
in a statement the group said it is constantly talking to its creditor banks but did not confirm any new transactions
part of the braskem stake is already pledged in collateral to banks and all dividends have also been surrendered to service the debt reuters reported last year
odebrecht has around billion reais billion in outstanding debt
