anglo needs 90 days to inspect leak at brazil iron ore operations
reuters anglo american plc will need about days to inspect a pipeline at its suspended iron ore operation in brazil it said on tuesday after the country s environmental regulators ordered the miner to halt operations following a leak
anglo halted operations after a leak was discovered on march on a pipeline which carries iron ore in slurry form from the mine to an export terminal
it said the leak of iron ore slurry was stopped within eight minutes and caused no disruption to local water supply
the company said there were no injuries
a previous leak prompted anglo to suspend operations from march to at which transports iron ore from the company s mine and processing facility in the state of minas gerais to a port in the state of rio de janeiro
