turkish permit for land section of turkstream pipeline still pending -novak
ankara april reuters russian energy minister alexander novak said on tuesday that turkey s approval for gazprom s onshore portion of the turkstream gas pipeline s second line is still pending
as for the onshore part this is now at the discussion stage the russian and turkish companies are discussing the protocol novak told reporters
all permissions for the offshore part had been received and it was under construction he said
sources told reuters in february that turkey has yet to issue a permit for gazprom to start building the part of the turkstream gas pipeline stoking fears the project may be delayed
