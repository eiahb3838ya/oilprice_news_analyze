ankara reuters the leaders of turkey and russia marked the official start of work to build turkey s first nuclear power station on tuesday launching construction of the billion akkuyu plant in the southern province of mersin
the plant will be built by russian state nuclear energy agency rosatom and will be made up of four units each with a capacity of megawatts
russian president vladimir putin and turkey s tayyip erdogan marked the start to construction watching by video link from ankara
