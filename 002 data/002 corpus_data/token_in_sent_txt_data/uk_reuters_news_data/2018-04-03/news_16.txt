turkey's erdogan says may cooperate with russia on other defence projects besides s-400
ankara reuters turkey may cooperate with russia on defence projects besides the missile defence system which moscow has agreed to supply to ankara president tayyip erdogan told a news conference on tuesday without giving further details
turkey signed an agreement to buy the system in late december in a move which raised concern in the west because they can not be integrated into nato s military architecture
