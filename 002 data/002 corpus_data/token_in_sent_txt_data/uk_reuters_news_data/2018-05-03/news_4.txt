ex-volkswagen ceo winterkorn charged in u.s. over diesel scandal
washington may reuters former volkswagen chief executive martin winterkorn was charged in federal court in detroit with conspiring to mislead regulators about the german automaker s diesel emissions cheating
volkswagen did not immediately comment
an indictment filed in secret in march was unsealed on thursday in district court and names numerous former executives
winterkorn resigned soon after the scandal over polluting vehicles in the united states became public in september
