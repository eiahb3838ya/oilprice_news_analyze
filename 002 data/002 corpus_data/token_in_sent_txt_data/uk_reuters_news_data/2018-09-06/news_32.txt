iraqi authorities declare city-wide curfew in basra to quell protests
erbil iraq sept reuters iraqi authorities declared a curfew from gmt to quell protests in basra a spokesman for the interior ministry said at a news conference on thursday
seven protesters have died in iraq s second biggest city in recent days as protesters angry over electricity outages water contamination a lack of jobs and proper government services clashed violently with security forces
