united airlines in talks to buy wide-body jets -sources
paris may reuters united airlines is in talks with planemakers airbus and boeing over the purchase of passenger jets to replace a fleet of boeing aircraft people familiar with the discussions said on tuesday
europe s airbus is expected to offer its upgraded jet against boeing s dreamliner in a deal potentially worth some billion at list prices they added
both planemakers declined to comment
we are always talking to our manufacturers about future fleet plans but we won t comment on the nature of those discussions united spokesman frank benenati said
