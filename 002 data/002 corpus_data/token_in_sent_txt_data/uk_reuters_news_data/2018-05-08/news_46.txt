iraqi kurdistan pm in moscow for talks on may 9 -kurdistan regional government official
london may reuters the prime minister of iraq s kurdistan region will hold talks in moscow on wednesday with russian officials a kurdistan regional government official said on tuesday
nechirvan barzani will travel to moscow at the request of rosneft chief igor sechin the official said
rosneft is a major investor in iraqi kurdistan and controls its oil pipelines
