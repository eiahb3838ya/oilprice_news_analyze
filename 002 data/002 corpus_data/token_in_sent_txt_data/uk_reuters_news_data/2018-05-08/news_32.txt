trump open to exported ethanol qualifying under biofuel laws -source
new york may reuters president donald trump told a group of republican senators on tuesday that he is considering allowing exported ethanol and other biofuels to count towards the annual volumes mandated by the environmental protection agency according to a source briefed on the discussions
the president also backed off plans to put a price cap on compliance credits refiners must submit to the epa and supports lifting restrictions on the sales of higher ethanol blends of gasoline the source said
trump hosted a meeting with a group of senators and administration officials on tuesday to outline a plan to help refiners with regulatory cost of the renewable fuel standard rfs while boosting demand for ethanol
