turkey will keep trading with iran, not accountable to other - economy minister
ankara may reuters turkey will continue its trade with iran as much as possible and will not be answerable to anyone else economy minister nihat zeybekci said on tuesday as president donald trump said the united states was withdrawing from the iran nuclear deal
from now on we will carry out our trade with iran within the possible framework until the end and we will not give account to anyone for this zeybekci said in an interview with broadcaster cnn turk
