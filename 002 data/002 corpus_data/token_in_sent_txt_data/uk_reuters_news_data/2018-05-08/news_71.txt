germany probing audi for new emissions device - der spiegel
frankfurt may reuters german motor vehicle authority kba is probing premium auto maker audi to see whether its models have been fitted with a previously unkown defeat device german weekly der spiegel said on tuesday
in germany around vehicles diesel vehicles conforming to the euro emission standard may have been fitted with an illegal software emissions device spiegel said
the kba has requested a hearing on suspicion the audi models have been fitted with an illegal defeat device spiegel cited a spokesman for germany s transport ministry as saying
audi and germany s transport ministry were not immediately available for comment
