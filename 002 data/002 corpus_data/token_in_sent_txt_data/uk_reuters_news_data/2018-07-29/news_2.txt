iran currency extends record fall as u.s. sanctions loom

in may the united states pulled out of a deal between world powers and tehran under which international sanctions on iran were lifted in return for curbs on its nuclear programme
washington decided to reimpose sanctions on iran upon its withdrawal accusing it of posing a security threat and has told countries they must halt all imports of iranian oil from or face financial measures
