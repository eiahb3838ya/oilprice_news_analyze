norway's prime minister to announce cabinet reshuffle on friday
oslo reuters norway s prime minister erna solberg will announce a cabinet reshuffle on friday her office said in a statement on thursday confirming media reports
business daily dagens naeringsliv reported that oil and energy minister terje soeviknes and transport minister ketil would resign from the cabinet
freiberg a former deputy minister at the oil ministry will become norway s new oil and energy minister dn said quoting unnamed sources
the prime minister s office declined to comment on the dn report
