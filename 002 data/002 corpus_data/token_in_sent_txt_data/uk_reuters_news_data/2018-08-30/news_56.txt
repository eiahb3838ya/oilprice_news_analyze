china says u.s. remarks on n.korea issue are "irresponsible"
beijing aug reuters china said on thursday that remarks on the north korea issue are irresponsible and hard to understand after president donald trump questioned beijing s role in resolving the crisis over pyongyang s development of nuclear weapons
chinese foreign ministry spokeswoman hua chunying made the comment at a regular news briefing adding that the united states should look at itself when encountering difficulties on the issue
