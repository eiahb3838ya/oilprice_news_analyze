iran is complying with nuclear deal restrictions -iaea report
vienna aug reuters iran has stayed within the main restrictions on its nuclear activities imposed by a deal with major powers a confidential report by the atomic watchdog indicated on thursday
in its second quarterly report since president donald trump announced in may that the united states would withdraw from the accord and reimpose sanctions the international atomic energy agency iaea said iran had stayed within caps on the level to which it can enrich uranium its stock of enriched uranium and other items
in its last report in may the iaea had said iran could do more to cooperate with inspectors and thereby enhance confidence but stopped short of saying iran had given it cause for concern
thursday s report to member states seen by reuters contained similar language
