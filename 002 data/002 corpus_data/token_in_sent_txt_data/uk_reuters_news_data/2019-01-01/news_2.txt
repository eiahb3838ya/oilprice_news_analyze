infant found alive in rubble after russian gas blast - tass
moscow jan reuters rescuers have found a child alive in the rubble of a russian apartment block that partially collapsed in an explosion tass news agency reported on tuesday citing the local emergency operations centre
the blast thought to have been caused by a gas leak damaged apartments in a building in the industrial city of magnitogorsk on monday killing at least seven people with people still missing
