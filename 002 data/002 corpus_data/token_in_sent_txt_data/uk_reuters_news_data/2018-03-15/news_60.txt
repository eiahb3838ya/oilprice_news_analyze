exclusive-shell close to clinching hong kong's first lng import deal - sources
singapore march reuters royal dutch shell is close to bagging a deal to supply hong kong with liquefied natural gas lng beating out major competitors for the right to be the first company to supply lng to the area
shell has edged out companies such as malaysia s petronas to supply lng through a contract to hong kong utility clp power two sources familiar with the matter told reuters
the deal will be for about years to supply about million tonnes per annum mtpa of lng from after the sources said declining to be named as they were not authorised to speak to the media
