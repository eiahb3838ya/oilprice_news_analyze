brazil's energisa withdraws offer to buy eletropaulo shares -filing
sao paulo may reuters brazil s power company energisa sa withdrew its offer to buy a controlling stake of eletropaulo metropolitana brazil s largest power distribution company according to a securities filing on friday
eletropaulo has been target of bids from other two companies besides energisa italy s enel spa and neoenergia sa which is controlled by spain s iberdrola
energisa s offer was the lowest of the three bidders
an auction for the potential sale of a controlling stake of eletropaulo has been scheduled for june
reporting by marcelo teixeira
