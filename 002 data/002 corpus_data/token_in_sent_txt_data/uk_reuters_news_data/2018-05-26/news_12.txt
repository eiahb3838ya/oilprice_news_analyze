fire broke out on onshore oil rig operated by depthwize in nigeria's niger delta -witnesses
yenagoa nigeria may reuters a fire broke out on an onshore oil rig in nigeria s niger delta on saturday three witnesses told reuters adding that the fire was still burning as of noon
the barge rig the majestic is operated by nigerian oil firm depthwize according to the company website
here depthwize did not immediately respond to a call and email seeking comment
