u.s. to continue referring adult illegal border crossers for prosecution
washington june reuters the border patrol will continue to refer for prosecution adults who are caught crossing the border illegally a spokesman for the customs and border protection said on thursday
family unity will be maintained for families apprehended crossing the border illegally and they will be transferred together to immigration and customs enforcement the spokesman said in a statement
for those children still in border patrol custody we are reuniting them with parents or legal guardians returned to border patrol custody following reporting by yeganeh torbati editing by paul simao
