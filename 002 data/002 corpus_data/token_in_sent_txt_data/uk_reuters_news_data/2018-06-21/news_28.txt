east libya forces claim control of es sider, ras lanuf oil ports
benghazi libya june reuters eastern libyan forces are in full control of the es sider and ras lanuf oil ports in the country s oil crescent a spokesman for the force said on thursday
staff were evacuated from the terminals and exports were suspended last thursday when armed opponents of military commander khalifa haftar stormed the ports and occupied them
