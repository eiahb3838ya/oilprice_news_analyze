fighting resumes near libya's ras lanuf oil port after lna advance
benghazi libya june reuters fighting resumed on thursday around libya s ras lanuf oil port after forces loyal to eastern commander khalifa haftar said they had taken full control of the area a military source said
haftar s libyan national army lna has lost at least seven men in the fighting around ras lanuf and the neighbouring terminal of es sider according to military and medical officials
