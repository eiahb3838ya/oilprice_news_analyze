eu observers "very concerned" about nigeria judge suspension-statement
lagos reuters the european union s team observing nigeria s election on saturday said it was very concerned about the process by which the country s top judge was suspended by president muhammadu buhari and the timing of the move
the chief justice of nigeria was suspended on friday three weeks ahead of the presidential election
the judiciary has resolved disputes to determine the outcome of disputed polls in the past
the eu s observation mission said the timing raises concerns about opportunity for electoral justice
