coal compromise would secure german energy supply-altmaier tells paper
berlin jan reuters a compromise hammered out early saturday by a commission would provide a secure energy supply while allowing germany to meet its climate change target for economy minister peter altmaier told a german newspaper
altmaier told the frankfurter allgemeine sonntagszeitung the german government would carefully and constructively examine the proposals which call for germany to shut down all power plants in germany by at the latest
he called germany s plans to exit coal production one of the most challenging transformational processes in the last decades
