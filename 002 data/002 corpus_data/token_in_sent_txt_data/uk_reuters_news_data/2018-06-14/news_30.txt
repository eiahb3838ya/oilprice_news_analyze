south african regulator says eskom can recover $2.5 billion through higher tariffs
pretoria june reuters south african energy regulator nersa on thursday gave power utility eskom the to recover billion rand billion of costs incurred over the past three years through higher tariffs about half what the utility had requested
eskom which is battling to emerge from governance and financial difficulties had applied for billion rand to compensate for costs that were not factored in previous tariff increases granted by the regulator
the regulator said it would announce at a later date when eskom can raise tariffs to recover the costs
eskom had said it would seek to recover the costs over a period of three years
