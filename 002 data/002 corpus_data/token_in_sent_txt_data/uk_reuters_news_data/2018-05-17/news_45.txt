eu takes france, germany and others to court over air quality
brussels may reuters the european commission will take france germany hungary italy romania and britain to the eu court of justice for failing to respect air quality limits the eu executive said on thursday
the move follows a summit in january in which the commission said it would get tough on member states who were still in breach of targets introduced for and
france germany and britain will be taken to court over their failure to respect limits for nitrogen dioxide while hungary italy and romania failed to reduce the level of particulate matter
