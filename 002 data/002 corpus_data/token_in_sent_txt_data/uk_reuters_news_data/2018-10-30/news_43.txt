turkish drilling ship to begin mediterranean operations wednesday -minister
antalya turkey oct reuters a turkish drilling ship will start operating in the mediterranean sea on wednesday km miles off the coast of antalya province energy minister fatih donmez said adding it would aim to open two drill holes annually
donmez made the comments on tuesday during a ceremony to mark the launch of the ship and added that turkey is very close to buying another drilling ship
