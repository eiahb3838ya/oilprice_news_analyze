update 1-poland's pkn expects oil shipment from uae by december
adds details quotes warsaw oct reuters poland s biggest oil refiner pkn orlen expects a shipment of tonnes of crude oil from the united arab emirates uae by december as it strives to reduce its reliance on russian supplies the company said on tuesday
pkn orlen and smaller rival lotos rely mostly on russian oil delivered via pipelines built in the but both have increased purchases from other sources
earlier in october pkn bought its first oil from nigeria
the united arab emirates is a new source of supplies for pkn orlen the company said in a statement
pkn also said the murban crude from the uae had a lower sulphur content than russian rebco russian export blend crude oil
murban crude has properties that allow for the production of a relatively large amount of products such as diesel and gasoline the company added
pkn said percent of oil refined in its refineries currently comes from sources other than russia
