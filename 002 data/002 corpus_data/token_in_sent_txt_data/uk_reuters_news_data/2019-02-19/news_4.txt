update 1-transcanada restarts shut portion of keystone oil pipeline
adds phmsa statement share activity winnipeg manitoba feb reuters transcanada corp restarted a section of the keystone oil pipeline on tuesday following a leak in missouri earlier this month company spokesman terry cunha said
transcanada had shut an arm of keystone from steele city nebraska to patoka illinois on after leaking barrels of crude oil
the shutdown restricted the flow on the keystone pipeline system a critical artery taking canadian crude from northern alberta to refineries in the midwest
the line restarted with a percent reduction of pressure a spokesman for regulator phmsa pipeline and hazardous materials safety administration said
transcanada shares finished up percent at c in toronto
