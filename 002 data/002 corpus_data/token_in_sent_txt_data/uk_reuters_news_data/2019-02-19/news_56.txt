germany's altmaier: most difficult part in eu-u.s. trade talks is now
berlin feb reuters germany s economy minister peter altmaier said on tuesday the most difficult part in trade negotiations between europe and the united states started now
we are not yet where we want to be
we might have made of the way and the most difficult part will be now altmaier the minister in charge of europe s biggest economy told dlf radio
asked about threats to increase car tariffs he said he did not believe the argument that imported cars would threaten the national security of the united states was sustainable
