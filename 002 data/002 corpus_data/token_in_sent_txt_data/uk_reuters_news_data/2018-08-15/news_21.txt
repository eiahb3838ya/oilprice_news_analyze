output at libya's sharara oilfield recovers to 260,000 bpd after abduction - engineer
benghazi libya aug reuters libya s sharara oilfield is producing barrels per day bpd after the restart of a control station that had been closed due to the kidnapping of two workers an engineer at the field said
station was restarted on sunday amid tightened security and was producing about bpd around half its normal capacity the engineer said
