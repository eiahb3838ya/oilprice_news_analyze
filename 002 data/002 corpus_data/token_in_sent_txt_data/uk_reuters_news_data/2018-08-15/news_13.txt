china's jiangsu province to force mills to move to coast in efficiency drive
beijing aug reuters china s steelmaking province jiangsu will require its steel mills to move to two new areas on the coast in the next few years according to a provincial government document to reduce air pollution and to make the industry more efficient
the push to create two steelmaking hubs on the coast and move the mills there by is similar to one outlined by hebei the country s top steel producing region earlier this year
the plan was distributed on tuesday by the jiangsu government and reviewed by reuters on wednesday
the provincial government declined to comment
