anglo american fined $21 mln for brazil pipeline leaks
rio de janeiro reuters brazil s environmental regulator ibama fined anglo american plc million reais million for two leaks in the pipeline transporting iron ore from its minas gerais mine to port in rio de janeiro state the watchdog said on tuesday
ibama ordered anglo american to halt mining operations after the leaks which took place on march and the regulator said in a statement
