two filipinas kidnapped in iraq -security sources
baquba iraq july reuters two filipina women were kidnapped on saturday in iraq on a road connecting baghdad to oil city kirkuk military police and local officials told reuters
the women were travelling with three other nationals of the philippines on their way to erbil the capital of the kurdistan region when their car broke down two military sources said
