sk energy's global head of crude oil trading quits -sources
march reuters veteran crude oil trader steven lim has quit sk energy international the trading arm of south korea s largest refiner three sources with knowledge of the matter said on monday
lim joined sk energy a unit of sk innovation in as its global head of crude oil participating in the platts market on close process which sets the middle east crude oil benchmark
it was not immediately clear where lim formerly at trading house phibro will be heading next
an sk innovation spokesman declined to comment on the matter
the sources declined to be identified as they were not authorised to speak to media
