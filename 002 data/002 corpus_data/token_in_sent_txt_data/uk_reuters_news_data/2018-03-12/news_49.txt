innogy asset swap deal could boost competitiveness- german minister
berlin march reuters the asset swap deal by germany s top utilities to divide assets of renewable energy provider innogy could make the sector more competitive economy minister brigitte zypries said on monday signaling berlin s support for the agreement
it s good if there are competitive and internationally oriented energy providers in germany
the current plans can be a contribution to this zypries told reuters in an emailed statement
concentrating on specific business areas can promote necessary investments in generation and distribution networks zypries added
germany s top utilities announced plans on sunday to break up innogy whose assets will be divided among parent rwe and rival in the sector s biggest overhaul since a landmark decision to exit nuclear power
