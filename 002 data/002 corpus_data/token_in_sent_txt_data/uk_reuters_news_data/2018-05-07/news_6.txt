u.s. vice president pence urges oas to suspend venezuela from organization
washington may reuters vice president mike pence called on monday for the organization of american states to suspend venezuela s membership in the group and he announced new sanctions targeting drug kingpins said to be linked to the government
pence in a speech to the oas urged socialist venezuelan president nicolas maduro to suspend elections scheduled for may saying they would be a fraud and a he also urged venezuela s neighbors in the hemisphere to step up their own measures against caracas imposing visa restrictions for leaders and taking other action to hold maduro accountable
