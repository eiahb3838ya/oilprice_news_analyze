u.s. strongly condemns attacks on libyan oil ports -state dept
washington june reuters the united states on wednesday strongly condemned recent attacks on libyan oil ports by militia loyal to ibrahim jathran and called for all armed actors to withdraw immediately from oil installations the state department said
the united states believes these vital libyan resources must remain under the exclusive control of the national oil corporation and the sole oversight of the government of national accord the statement said
