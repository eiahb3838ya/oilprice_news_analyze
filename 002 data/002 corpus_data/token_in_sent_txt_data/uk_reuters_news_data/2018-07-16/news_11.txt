qatar airways adapting to blockade, may not post a loss in current year
farnborough england july reuters the chief executive of qatar airways said the airline was adapting to a regional blockade that has prevented it flying some routes and that those restrictions would not necessarily push it into the red for the current financial year
there is a possibility that we will post also a loss in our current financial year but it s only a possibly chief executive akbar told reporters at the farnborough airshow on monday
the restrictions mean the company is set to post a loss for the financial year ended march but the results have not been published yet
said these would be made public in the coming weeks
he also said the airline was only moderately hedged on fuel and may need to hedge more in future as the price of oil rises
