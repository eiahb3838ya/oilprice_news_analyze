woolworths group enters 15-year fuel supply deal with caltex australia
july reuters woolworths group ltd australia s largest supermarket operator said on thursday it entered a fuel supply deal with caltex australia ltd even as it continues to pursue an ipo or sale of its petrol business
the firm will also start a wholesale food supply to over existing caltex convenience sites as part of the deal
thursday s deal comes two weeks after woolworths cancelled a a billion billion sale of its petrol stations to bp plc after it was blocked by australia s antitrust regulator
