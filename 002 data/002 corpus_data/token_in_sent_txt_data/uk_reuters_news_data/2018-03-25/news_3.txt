fire in shopping mall in siberia kills 5 and injures 32
moscow march reuters at least five people were killed and injured on sunday when fire tore through a shopping mall in the siberian city of kemerovo the russian state investigative committee said
seventeen people were missing following the incident in a region some km miles east of moscow according to local officials who spoke to russian news agencies
three women one child and a man died in the mall and were in hospital the investigative committee said
