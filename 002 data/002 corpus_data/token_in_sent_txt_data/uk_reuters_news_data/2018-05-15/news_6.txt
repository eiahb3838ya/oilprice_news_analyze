iran's zarif says "good start" after talks with eu powers
paris may reuters iran and european powers have made a good start in talks over how to salvage the nuclear deal but much depends on what happens in the next few weeks foreign minister mohammed javad zarif said on tuesday
we are on the right track a lot will depend on what we can do in next few weeks zarif said after a meeting with the foreign ministers of britain france and germany and the european union s foreign policy chief
