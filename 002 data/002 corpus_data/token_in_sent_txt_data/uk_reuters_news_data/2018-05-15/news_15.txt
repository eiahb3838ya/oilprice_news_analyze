iran to resume uranium enrichment if nuclear talks with eu fail -spokesman
brussels may reuters iran will restart its uranium enrichment if it can not find a way to save the nuclear deal with the european union after the united states pulled out last week tehran s government spokesman said on tuesday
if our interests are not preserved we will pull out of the deal and will resume our enrichment to percent or any level that we desire to the tasnim news agency quoted government spokesman mohammad bagher nobakht as saying
under the terms of the deal iran is allowed to enrich uranium to around percent
