conoco aiming to seize oil cargoes near citgo's aruba terminal -sources
may reuters oil company conocophillips has brought court actions to seize at least two cargoes of crude and fuel near a terminal operated by pdvsa subsidiary citgo petroleum in aruba according to three sources familiar with the matter
conoco is aggressively moving to enforce a billion arbitration award issued last month by the international chamber of commerce over venezuela s expropriation of two projects in the south american nation
reporting by sailu urribarri and marianna parraga editing by jonathan oatis
