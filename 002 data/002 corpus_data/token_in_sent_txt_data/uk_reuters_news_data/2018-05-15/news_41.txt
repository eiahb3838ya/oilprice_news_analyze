iraq invites bids to build oil refinery in diwaniya
baghdad may reuters investors interested in bidding to build a oil refinery in diwaniya south of baghdad have until july to make offers the iraqi oil ministry said in a statement on tuesday
the refinery is one of several crude oil processing projects offered by iraq as part of a plan to become in oil products
iraq is the crude oil producer in the organization of the petroleum exporting countries after saudi arabia
