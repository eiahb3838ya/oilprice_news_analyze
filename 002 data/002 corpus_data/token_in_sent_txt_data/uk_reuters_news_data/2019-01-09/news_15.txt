saudi aramco to issue bonds in q2 2019, list in 2021 - energy minister
riyadh jan reuters saudi aramco will issue bonds in the second quarter of and the state oil giant will be listed saudi arabia s energy minister said on wednesday
the valuation of aramco would depend on the market but petroleum reserves would be one of the bases for establishing its value energy minister khalid said
banks including jpmorgan and morgan stanley were working on the planned stock market listing of aramco before the plan to list the company and offer a percent stake to other investors was pulled last year
