saudi arabia confident cuts by opec and allies will balance oil market
riyadh jan reuters saudi arabia s energy minister said on wednesday he was confident that action to rein in output by opec and its allies would bring the oil market into balance unless there was an unexpected development
khalid also said he would not rule out calling for further action by the organization of the petroleum exporting countries and its allies in future adding that market conditions looked better than a few weeks ago
