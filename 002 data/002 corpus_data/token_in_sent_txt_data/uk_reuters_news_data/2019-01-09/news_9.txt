rpt-exclusive-indian refiners pay for iranian oil in rupees - uco bank executive
repeats story earlier story for wider readership with no change to text by nidhi verma new delhi jan reuters india has begun paying iran for oil in rupees a senior bank official said on tuesday the first such payments since the united states imposed new sanctions against tehran in november
washington gave a waiver to eight countries including india allowing them to import some iranian oil
india the world s third biggest oil importer wants to continue buying oil from iran as it offers free shipping and an extended credit period while iran will use the rupee funds to mostly pay for imports from india
today we received a good amount from some oil companies charan singh executive director at uco bank told reuters
he did not disclose the names of refiners or how much had been deposited
new delhi recently issued a notification exempting payments to the national iranian oil co nioc for crude oil imports from steep withholding taxes enabling refiners to clear an estimated billion in dues
an industry source said india s top refiner indian oil corp and mangalore refinery petrochemicals have made payments for iranian oil imports
neither was immediately available for comment
iran is devising payment mechanisms including barter with trading partners like india china and russia following a delay in the setting up of a european special purpose vehicle to facilitate trade with tehran its foreign minister javad zarif said earlier on tuesday
in the previous round of sanctions india settled percent of oil payments in rupees and the remainder in euros but this time it has signed deal with iran to make all payments in rupees as new delhi wants to fix its trade balance with tehran
indian imports from iran totalled about billion between april and november with oil accounting for about percent
singh said indian refiners had previously made payments to banks but they will now be making deposits into the accounts of only iranian lenders as one had since closed and the has imposed secondary sanctions on five others
the nine banks are saman bank bank pasargad bank eghtesad novin karafarin bank bank samayeh middle east bank city bank bank hekmat iranian and tourism bank
