ramaphosa has made revamping firms like eskom a top priority as he seeks to reverse years of economic stagnation and mismanagement under his predecessor jacob zuma
but he faces a difficult balancing act as talk of selling state assets is anathema to large sections of society including the country s powerful trade union movement
south africa is exposed to eskom via over billion rand billion of state guarantees a point ratings agencies regularly cite as a risk to its sovereign rating
