uk's ofgem launches probe into cadent's record keeping
reuters britain s gas and electricity markets regulator ofgem said on wednesday it launched an investigation into british gas distribution business cadent s record keeping
the office of gas and electricity markets said it would investigate if cadent failed to keep records of gas pipes risers in some blocks of flats that are part of its network
ofgem is also investigating whether cadent kept and maintained records for all of its risers and whether it has the appropriate systems in place to do so
cadent formerly national grid gas distribution owns the gas distribution networks in north london central england the east of england and northwest england
it was renamed in may after national grid sold the majority stake to a consortium
