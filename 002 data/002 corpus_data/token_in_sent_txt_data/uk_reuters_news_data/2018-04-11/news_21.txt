greek power grid launches tender for crete link project
athens april reuters greece s power grid operator has launched an international open tender for a project linking the island of crete to the mainland grid via undersea cables the energy ministry said on wednesday
the project linking crete to the peloponnese peninsula is part of a billion euro billion investment plan to connect some of greece s islands to the mainland
the tender via an will be awarded to the most economically advantageous offer based solely on the lowest price the ministry said
the crete link is expected to be completed by and is budgeted to cost million euros the ministry said
crete will also be connected to athens in a later phase
power production on crete the largest of the greek islands is currently provided by three plants that must be shut down to comply with a european union directive
energy minister george stathakis said the project would contribute to the drastic reduction of electricity costs improve crete s ecological footprint and lead to million euros in annual savings mainly through lower service charges
grid operator admie was spun off last year from power utility public power corp under greece s international bailout
