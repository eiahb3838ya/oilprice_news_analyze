iraq reopens airspace as elections proceed without incident
baghdad may reuters iraqi prime minister haider ordered the reopening of the nation s airspace and resumption of air traffic on saturday state television reported
the shutdown had come into effect at midnight on friday as a security measure ahead of the voting which started on saturday morning
there was no significant incident reported by midday
islamic state militants who overran a third of iraq in had threatened attacks ahead of the elections the first since the defeat of the militants last year
