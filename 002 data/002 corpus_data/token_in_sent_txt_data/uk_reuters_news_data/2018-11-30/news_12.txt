poland and denmark take final decision to build baltic pipe gas link
warsaw nov reuters poland s gas grid operator and its danish counterpart have given the final to invest in building a gas pipeline linking poland with norway s deposits via the baltic sea and denmark said on friday
the pipeline known as the baltic pipe is a polish project designed to reduce its reliance on russian gas
the baltic pipe is expected to be ready in when poland s deal with russia s gazprom expires
