turkey to hike electricity prices by 9-14 pct from sept 1, government says
ankara sept reuters turkey s energy regulator will raise electricity prices by percent for industrial use and percent for residential use from according to figures published in the government s official gazette overnight
the rate hikes which will further feed inflation highlight another impact of turkey s currency crisis
the lira currency has fallen some percent this year hitting energy producers hard as turkey is almost wholly reliant on imports for its energy needs
