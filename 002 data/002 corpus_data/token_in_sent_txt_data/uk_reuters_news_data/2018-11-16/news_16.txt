south africa's eskom to revise coal contract strategy
johannesburg nov reuters south africa s power utility eskom said on friday it had revised its coal strategy to revert coal supply to contracts and extend contracts for mines
eskom which has warned of possible power outages after low coal stockpiles said it would in the short term purchase four million tonnes of coal for immediate supply to recover all power stations to above the minimum required level by the end of march
