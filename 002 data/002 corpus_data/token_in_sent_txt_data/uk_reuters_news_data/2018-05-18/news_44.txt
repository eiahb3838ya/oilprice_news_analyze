porsche to recall 60,000 suvs for emissions problems -german regulator
hamburg reuters volkswagen s brand porsche has been asked by germany s kba automotive regulator to recall around macan and cayenne vehicles a kba spokesman said
german magazine der spiegel reported the recall earlier on friday saying the two models equipped with the latest diesel engine generation were fitted with more defeat devices capable of reducing emissions in laboratory tests
porsche was immediately available for comment
the manufacturer earlier this year already issued a recall for macan models to update software with the aim of averting a regulatory ban on sales
