putin, merkel discuss u.s. withdrawal from iran nuclear deal
sochi russia reuters russian president vladimir putin said on friday he had discussed the withdrawal from the iran nuclear deal at talks with german chancellor angela merkel in sochi
the deal between iran and six world powers lifted most international sanctions in return for tehran curbing its nuclear programme under strict surveillance by the nuclear watchdog
european powers this week vowed to shore up the deal after president donald trump announced washington s withdrawal
