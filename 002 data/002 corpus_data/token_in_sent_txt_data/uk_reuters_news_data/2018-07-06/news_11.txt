trafigura promotes luckock, gillon to co-heads of oil
london july reuters trading house trafigura has promoted its two of risk ben luckock and robert gillon to become of oil as part of an ongoing management reshuffle trading sources said
trafigura previously had only one head of its oil division jose larocca who will remain an executive director
larocca a trafigura veteran and one of the closest allies of its late founder claude dauphin will continue to oversee the oil division while helping luckock and gillon assume their leadership responsibilities the sources told reuters
