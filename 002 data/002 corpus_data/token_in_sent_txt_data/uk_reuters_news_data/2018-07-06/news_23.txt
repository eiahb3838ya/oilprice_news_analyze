eu's mogherini says iran, world powers continue talks to save nuclear deal
paris july reuters eu foreign policy chief federica mogherini said on friday that world powers and iran had agreed to continue talking including on economic measures over how to save its nuclear deal
speaking after the first ministerial meeting between the remaining parties to the deal since the united states withdrew in may mogherini who chairs the talks read a statement saying all sides would continue to look at ways salvage the deal despite the reimposition of sanctions
she took no questions from reporters
