total says no impact from farmers' blockade on refinery operations
paris june reuters france s total said personnel from the oil and gas company had access to four refineries in france and a blockade of the sites by farmers had not had an impact on operations
total which operates four of france s seven refineries said the blockade was however hampering the distribution of products by road from four refineries and six depots
it said service was normal at its petrol stations in france
french farmers blocked access to oil depots and refineries using tonnes of onions wood and rubble as part of a protest against plans to allow total to use imported palm oil at a biofuel plant
reporting by bate felix
