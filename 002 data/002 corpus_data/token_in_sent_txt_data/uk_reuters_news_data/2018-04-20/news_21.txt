gazprom seeks termination of ukraine gas contracts in arbitration
moscow april reuters russian gas giant gazprom said on friday it has lodged a claim to an international arbitration seeking to cancel its supply and transit contracts with ukraine
it said talks with kiev over the contracts had ended without concrete results and it had lodged a claim to a stockholm arbitration court
the contracts are due to expire at the end of
last month gazprom appealed against a previous stockholm arbitration ruling which obliged the company to pay ukraine s naftogaz billion following a long dispute between the companies over gas delivery
ukraine is a major transit country for russian gas supplies to europe where gazprom accounts for around percent of the gas market
gazprom said last month it would terminate its gas contracts with ukraine after it lost the court case escalating a dispute which had left ukraine struggling to stay warm and which the european union said could threaten gas flows to europe
ukraine has not bought gas from russia since november due to a broader political conflict between the two countries following russia s annexation of crimea in and support for separatists in eastern ukraine
