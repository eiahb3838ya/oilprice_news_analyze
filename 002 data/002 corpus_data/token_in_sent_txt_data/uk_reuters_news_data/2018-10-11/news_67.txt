german economy minister doubts constitutionality of diesel fines
berlin oct reuters german economy minister peter altmaier said on thursday the government could run the risk of breaking the law if it imposed fines on carmakers who fail to offer diesel hardware retrofits
fines are justified under german law when someone has breached his legal obligations
but i have doubts whether this path is even feasible under constitutional law altmaier a member of chancellor angela merkel s conservatives told deutschlandfunk radio
a senior member of the social democrat spd coalition party said on wednesday the government should fine carmakers if they fail to fix dirty diesel vehicles
