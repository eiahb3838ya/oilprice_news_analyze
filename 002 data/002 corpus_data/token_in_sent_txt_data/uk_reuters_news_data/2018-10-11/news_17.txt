smoke billows from venezuela amuay refinery, worker says issue fixed
punto fijo venezuela oct reuters thick beige smoke billowed out of the amuay refinery on venezuela s caribbean peninsula of paraguana on thursday according to a reuters witness
it was not immediately clear what caused the smoke and pdvsa did not respond to a request for comment
one worker with state oil company pdvsa said the smoke was due to a problem with the refinery s cat cracker but that the issue had been solved
