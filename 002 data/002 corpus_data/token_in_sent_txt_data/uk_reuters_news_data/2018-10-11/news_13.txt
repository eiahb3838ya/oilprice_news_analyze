update 2-a third washington refinery curbs operations after natgas pipe fire
adds marathon acquisition of andeavor in paragraph updates fuel prices in paragraphs and oct reuters andeavor s anacortes plant on thursday joined a list of oil refineries in washington state forced to shut or curb operations after natural gas supply to the facilities was disrupted by a pipeline fire in canada on tuesday
andeavor which was just acquired by marathon petroleum corp last week said it had begun shutting various units at the anacortes refinery due to a disruption in natural gas supply to the plant following an upset in a third party the shutdown follows actions by two other refineries and sent fuel prices in the pacific northwest region and across the west coast soaring
tuesday s fire followed a rupture on the enbridge inc pipeline in the canadian province of british columbia
the line carries natural gas to the pacific northwest
enbridge on wednesday said it was approved to restart operations on an adjacent natural gas pipeline which had been depressurized following the fire
the stoppage of operations at the anacortes plant follows royal dutch shell s announcement on wednesday that it was shutting units at its bpd puget sound refinery
phillips also said it was adjusting operations at its bpd ferndale plant until natural gas supply could be restored
pacific northwest gasoline for prompt delivery in the physical market strengthened by about cents a gallon thursday trading at as much as cents per gallon above gasoline futures on the new york mercantile exchange market participants said
pacific northwest diesel fuel for physical delivery also rose climbing to cents a gallon above the futures contract from cents a gallon in the previous session
the increases in the pacific northwest lifted fuel prices across the west coast with gasoline strengthening in san francisco and los angeles as well
the region already has some of the highest retail prices in the united states
