russia's putin says no hard figures on possible oil output cuts
buenos aires dec reuters president vladimir putin said on saturday that had no concrete figures on possible oil output cuts though russia would continue with its contribution to reducing global output
russia one of the world s major crude producing nations has been bargaining with opec s leader saudi arabia over the timing and volume of any reduction
opec and oil producers will hold a ministerial meeting
