rouhani says u.s. wants to cause insecurity in iran but will not succeed
dubai sept reuters iranian president hassan rouhani said on sunday the united states wants to create insecurity in the islamic republic a day after an attack on a military parade that killed members of the country s elite revolutionary guards
speaking before leaving tehran to attend the general assembly in new york rouhani accused gulf arab states of providing financial and military support for ethnic arab groups
reporting by michael georgy
