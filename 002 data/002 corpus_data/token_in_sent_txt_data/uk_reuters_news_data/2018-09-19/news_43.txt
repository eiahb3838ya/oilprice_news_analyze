french energy firm neoen registers for possible ipo
paris sept reuters neoen has registered with france s amf financial regulator over a possible initial public offering ipo the renewable energy company said on wednesday
neoen which has around billion euros billion worth of assets said the possible ipo could raise million euros and would fund future investment plans and growth in production capacity
its plans call for total capacity in operation or under construction of at least gigawatts by the end of chief executive xavier barbaro said
neoen was founded in by jacques veyrat the former chief executive of commodities trading company louis dreyfus who still owns a percent stake in neoen through his investment vehicle impala
neoen said impala would remain its majority shareholder
neoen is also one of several french renewable energy companies competing with market leaders edf and engie
