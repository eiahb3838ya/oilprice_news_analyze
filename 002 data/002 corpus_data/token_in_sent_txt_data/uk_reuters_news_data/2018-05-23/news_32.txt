china welcomes high-quality u.s. products to enter its market
hong kong reuters china welcomes and competitive products made by the united states to enter its market the commerce ministry said on wednesday
both china and the united states will seriously implement a deal reached in washington including the purchase of farm and energy products the ministry said in a statement
china said on tuesday it would cut import tariffs for automobiles and car parts in the effort to further open up its markets
