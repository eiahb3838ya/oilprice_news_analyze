petrobras ceo monteiro not expected to stay on - brazil president-elect
brasilia nov reuters brazil s jair bolsonaro said on wednesday that the chief executive of oil company petroleo brasileiro sa ivan monteiro is not expected to stay on during the new administration
speaking to reporters bolsonaro said he has not spoken yet to his chief economic adviser paulo guedes on whether the president of the central bank ilan goldfajn will remain in his job
bolsonaro who takes office on said the foreign minister in his government will be a career diplomat
