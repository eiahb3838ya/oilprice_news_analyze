australia's apa group slumps as govt opposes hong kong buyout
sydney nov reuters shares in australia s biggest gas pipeline company apa group fell percent on thursday after australia s treasurer said he intended to block a buyout by hong kong s ck group
treasurer josh frydenberg said after market hours on wednesday that his preliminary view was that the a billion takeover was against the national interest because it would create a concentration of foreign ownership in the sector
apa group shares which had never traded at the a offer price fell to a an almost low wiping a billion million off its market value
the broader market opened higher
