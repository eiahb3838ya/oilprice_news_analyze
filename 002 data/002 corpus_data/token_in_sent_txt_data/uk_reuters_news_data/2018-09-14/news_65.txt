china stats bureau says rebound in investment growth will be difficult
beijing sept reuters china s statistics bureau said on friday that it expects investment growth to stabilise but a rebound will be difficult to come by
a slowing trend in infrastructure investment will be halted in coming months bureau spokesman mao shengyong told reporters in a news conference
china s investment growth slowed to a new record low in the first eight months this year official data showed on friday
reporting by beijing monitoring desk
