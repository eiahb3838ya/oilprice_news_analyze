norway's hydro drops plan to buy rio tinto assets
sept reuters rio tinto said on friday that norway s hydro had dropped plans to buy several of the miner s assets including an aluminium plant in iceland for million
in february hydro made an offer to buy the aluminium plant as well as a percent stake in a dutch anode facility and percent of the shares in a swedish aluminium fluoride plant as part of its efforts to make its output as green as possible and bolster its position in europe
hydro said in a separate statement it had dropped plans to buy the assets because approval from european commission competition regulators was taking longer than anticipated
