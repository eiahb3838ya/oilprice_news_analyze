new petrobras ceo says dominating domestic refining, gas market bad for company
rio de janeiro jan reuters roberto castello branco the incoming chief executive of petroleo brasileiro sa made the case for divestments in the natural gas and refining sectors at a ceremony on thursday
at the event in rio de janeiro the new head of petrobras as the firm is known said the company s domestic domination of the natural gas supply chain was bad for the company and bad for brazil
the firm would like competitors in the domestic refining industry and was always open to new partnerships he said
