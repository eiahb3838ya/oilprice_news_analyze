iraq police fire in air as protesters try to storm basra govt building
basra july reuters iraqi police fired in the air as hundreds of protesters tried to storm the main provincial government building in basra on sunday wounding four people in the seventh day of unrest that has swept southern cities over poor services police sources said
some of the protesters tried to storm the building
we prevented them by using water cannons and tear gas said one of the police sources
