saudi arabia says ballistic missile fired by yemen's houthis intercepted
dubai reuters saudi arabian air defences intercepted a ballistic missile fired in the direction of the city of najran by yemen s houthi group the saudi press agency reported on tuesday citing a military spokesman
there were no casualties as a result of the interception said the spokesman of the coalition fighting the houthis colonel turki
