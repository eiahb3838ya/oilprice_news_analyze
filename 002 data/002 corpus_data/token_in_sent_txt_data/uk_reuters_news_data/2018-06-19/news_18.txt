houthis say launch missile at aramco facility in saudi arabia's asir -houthi media
dubai june reuters the houthi movement in yemen said it had launched a missile targeting a facility of oil firm aramco in asir in southern saudi arabia media tv and saba reported on tuesday
there was no immediate comment from aramco or saudi officials about the claim
the houthis have launched several missiles at saudi cities in recent months most of which have been intercepted by saudi air defence forces
