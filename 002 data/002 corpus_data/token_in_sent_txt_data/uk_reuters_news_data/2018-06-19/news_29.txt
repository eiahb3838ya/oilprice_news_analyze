naftogaz says british court grants gazprom asset freeze in uk
kiev june reuters ukraine state oil and gas firm naftogaz said on tuesday a london court has granted its request to freeze the british assets of russia s gazprom to enforce an earlier arbitration ruling from sweden
that ruling made by the stockholm arbitration court in february was meant to conclude a legal battle over gas deliveries
but naftogaz says gazprom has not complied with the ruling which obliged the russian company to resume gas supplies to ukraine at market equivalent prices and pay billion
naftogaz has said it would go to court to seize gazprom assets in europe but would not touch russian gas transit through ukraine
