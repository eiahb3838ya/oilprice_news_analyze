china says will 'fight back firmly' if u.s. publishes additional tariffs list
beijing june reuters china will take qualitative and quantitative measures if the government publishes an additional list of tariffs on chinese goods the commerce ministry said on tuesday
the ministry said in a statement on its website that china would take comprehensive measures to fight back firmly
president donald trump threatened on monday to impose a percent tariff on billion of chinese goods escalating a trade war with beijing
he said the move was in retaliation for china s decision to raise tariffs on billion in goods
