citic group in talks for up to 49 pct stake in cefc unit - cefc exec
beijing reuters cefc china energy is in talks with china s citic group for citic to take a maximum percent stake in the private conglomerate s european unit a cefc executive briefed on the matter told reuters on tuesday
a cefc spokesman said he couldn t immediately comment on the matter
cefc europe said late on monday that the firm s activities in czech republic would continue and a new unnamed chinese shareholder would join the firm
czech news website said without naming its sources the new shareholder would be china s citic group and that it would take a percent stake in cefc europe
