activist investor icahn raises stake in oil producer energen
reuters activist investor carl icahn boosted his stake in energen corp a filing showed on tuesday as he eyes a potential takeover of the oil and gas producer
in may hedge fund manager keith meister of corvex management and icahn said they may try to buy the producer which has operations in the prolific permian basin of texas and new mexico
icahn now owns percent of the company shares up from percent last week according to the filing
corvex which is energen s second largest shareholder with an percent stake sold million shares in the company to icahn last month and granted him an option to buy another million shares
