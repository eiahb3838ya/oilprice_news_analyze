gabon oil workers threaten 15-day strike at total facilities
libreville june reuters gabon s oil workers union onep plans to launch a strike at the facilities of french oil producer total on sunday if its demands are not met for higher pay and other issues it said in a letter to total gabon seen by reuters on tuesday
in the letter dated on monday onep also demanded new bonuses for employees and a reduction in the number of foreign workers
