kosmos energy abandons suriname well after it fails to find oil
june reuters oil and natural gas explorer kosmos energy ltd said on tuesday it was abandoning one of its wells offshore suriname after it failed to find oil
the company said its exploration well located in block offshore suriname encountered high quality reservoirs in the targeted zones but did not find hydrocarbons
kosmos however said its drilling program would remain unchanged given the independent nature of the prospects and that it was still in the early stages of exploring the basin
