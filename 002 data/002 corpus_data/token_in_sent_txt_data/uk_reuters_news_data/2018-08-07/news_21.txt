ab inbev to build brewery in mozambique
johannesburg aug reuters ab inbev the world s biggest brewer will start building a million hectolitre a year brewery in mozambique in the second half of next year the company s africa head said on tuesday
we re not disclosing the value of the investment at this point said ricardo tadeu ab inbev africa zone president at a media briefing in johannesburg
