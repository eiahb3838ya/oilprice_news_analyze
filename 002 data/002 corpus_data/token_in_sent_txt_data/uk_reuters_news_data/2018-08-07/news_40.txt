kong reuters saudi aramco and malaysia s petronas have approached banks to replace a billion loan raised earlier this year for a joint venture with financing of approximately the same size banking sources familiar with the matter said
the two state energy companies borrowed billion billion pounds from a large consortium of international banks in march for a refinery and petrochemical joint venture in the southern malaysian state of johor
the project refinery and petrochemical integrated development rapid is a billion billion pounds complex located between the malacca strait and the south china sea conduits for middle east oil and gas bound for china japan and south korea
