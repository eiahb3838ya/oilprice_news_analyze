bmw recalls 324,000 cars in europe after korean engine fires -faz
frankfurt reuters german luxury carmaker bmw is recalling about diesel vehicles in europe to fix a technical fault that has caused engine fires in south korea german newspaper frankfurter allgemeine zeitung faz said
a faulty exhaust gas recirculation module in bmw diesel cars caused engine fires in south korea this year prompting the company to recall vehicles there and issue an apology
bmw is now widening the recall to europe with vehicles affected in germany to replace the module if the fault is confirmed faz said on tuesday citing bmw
bmw officials were not immediately reachable for comment
