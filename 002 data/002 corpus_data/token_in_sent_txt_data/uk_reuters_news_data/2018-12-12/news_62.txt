poland's pm says there will be no energy price hikes
warsaw dec reuters poland s prime minister told parliament on wednesday there would be no sharp rises in energy prices in the face of a warning from the regulator that state utilities had asked for bills to go up
there will be no energy price hikes said mateusz morawiecki whose law and justice pis party has been trying to build up its support before national elections next year
