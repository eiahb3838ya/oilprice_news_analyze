corrected-china says u.s. trade talks hard to proceed with knife at its neck
corrects china vice commerce minister s comments on trade talks with beijing sept reuters trade talks have been difficult to proceed with the united states putting a knife to china s neck chinese vice commerce minister wang shouwen said on tuesday
when the talks can restart completely would depend on the will of the united states wang said at a press conference
one can not say that all previous trade discussions have been useless but the united states has abandoned its mutual understanding with china wang said
