nigeria's central bank governor optimistic over $8.1 bln mtn transfer dispute
abuja sept reuters nigeria s central bank governor godwin emefiele said on tuesday he was optimistic the bank would resolve a dispute linked to allegations that south african telecom firm mtn moved funds out of the country illegally
the bank month ordered mtn and its banks to bring billion back into the country that mtn allegedly had sent abroad in breach of foreign exchange regulations
reporting by camillus eboh in abuja alexis akwagyiram and chijioke ohuocha in lagos editing by john stonestreet
