france expects to support revised nord stream 2 directive
paris feb reuters france expects to support a revised directive on the nord stream pipeline project said the country s foreign ministry on thursday adding it was working with partners including germany on possible changes to a text on the matter
germany has been pressuring other european capitals to block an eu proposal to regulate russia s nord stream pipeline ahead of a key meeting on friday diplomatic sources have said
