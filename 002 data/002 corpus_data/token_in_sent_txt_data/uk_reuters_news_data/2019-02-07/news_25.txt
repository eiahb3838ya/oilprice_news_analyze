macron says france to decide on new nuclear reactors around 2022
paris reuters french president emmanuel macron said on thursday the country will decide around whether utility edf will be allowed to build new nuclear reactors
in november macron said france would reduce the share of nuclear in the power mix to percent by down from percent today and said that a decision on whether to build new nuclear plants would be taken
because of repeated delays and budget overruns of edf s epr reactor model under construction in flamanville northern france macron s government has not yet committed to building new reactors once old reactors close
we will decide around about what france does with the new generation of eprs depending on the outcome of studies and any progress made macron said during a debate with young people in the region
