merkel: germany won't become dependent on russia for gas due to nord stream
bratislava feb reuters germany will not become dependent on russia for gas due to russia s nord stream pipeline chancellor angela merkel said on thursday stressing that it was crucial to ensure ukraine remained a transit country for gas
do we become dependent on russia due to this second gas pipeline
i say no if we diversify at the same time merkel told a news conference in bratislava where she met the leaders of the visegrad group czech republic hungary poland and slovakia
diplomatic sources have said germany is pressuring other european capitals to block an eu proposal to regulate nord stream ahead of a key meeting on friday but may fail to convince france threatening the project s construction
