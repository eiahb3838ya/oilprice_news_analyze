libya's noc sees 160,000 bpd sharara oilfield loss after abductions
tunis july reuters libya s national oil corporation noc said on saturday it was expecting to lose barrels per day bpd of output at sharara oilfield after two staff were abducted in an attack
four of the station staff were initially kidnapped but two of them have been since released noc said
oil wells in the surrounding area have been shut down as a precaution and all other workers noc operates sharara in partnership with repsol total omv and equinor formerly known as statoil
