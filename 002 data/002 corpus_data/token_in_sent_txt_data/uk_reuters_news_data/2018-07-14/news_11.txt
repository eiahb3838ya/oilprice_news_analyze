south korea summons russian officials over air zone incursion
seoul reuters south korea summoned a russian embassy official on saturday to express regrets about the incursion of two russian military aircraft into south korea s air defence identification zone and to urge that it not be repeated
south korea said on friday it had scrambled military aircraft to intercept two russian bomber jets that flew into its air defence identification zone kadiz four times that day
the south korean foreign ministry summoned a senior russian diplomat and expressed regrets and urged the incident not to happen again it said in a statement
south korea s defence ministry called in a russian military official and lodged a protest about the incursion
russian military planes kadiz entry will never help stabilise the korean peninsula as well as northeast asia and the possibility of collision can not be ruled out the defence ministry said in a statement
russia s defence ministry said on friday that two russian bombers escorted by fighter jets flew over the international waters of the sea of japan the yellow sea and the eastern part of the pacific ocean
the russian military said they were escorted by korean and fighter jets and japanese war planes on some stages of the route
