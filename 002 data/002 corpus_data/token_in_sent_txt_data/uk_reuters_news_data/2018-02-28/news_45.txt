total, edison get greek go-ahead for oil and gas exploration
majority of lawmakers approve lease agreements for exploration licences for four blocks were pending parliament approval energy minister says greece turning new page in hydrocarbons athens feb reuters greece s parliament gave the on wednesday for companies including france s total and italy s edison to explore for oil and gas in the west of the country
licences for four blocks one offshore and three on land were awarded in but had to be ratified by parliament for exploration work to begin
encouraged by recent large gas finds in the eastern mediterranean greece is eager to attract investment in its energy sector as it tries to emerge from years of economic crisis
western greece is an underexplored area and very little data exists on its hydrocarbon potential
