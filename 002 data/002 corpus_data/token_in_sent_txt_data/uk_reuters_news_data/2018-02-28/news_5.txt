u.s. interior panel votes for lower federal offshore oil and gas royalties
washington feb reuters a department of the interior committee voted on wednesday to recommend to secretary ryan zinke that the agency lower royalty rates for federal offshore oil and gas drilling to percent from percent through to spur more production
the agency s royalty policy committee consisting of department officials state and tribal officials and energy companies voted unanimously to lower the rates which were set during the administration of former president george bush
the panel also voted to increase the amount of acreage available for offshore oil and natural gas leasing in the outer continental shelf
