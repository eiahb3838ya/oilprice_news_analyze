toyota to build new auris car at uk plant, in brexit boost for pm may
london feb reuters toyota said on wednesday it will build the next generation of its auris car at its english plant in a welcome boost to prime minister theresa may as britain leaves the european union confirming a previous reuters report
the japanese carmaker will make its new auris hatchback at burnaston derbyshire replacing the current run of the model made there the company said in a statement
