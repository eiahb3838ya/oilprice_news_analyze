indonesia state power utility urges widodo to set new coal pricing rules
jakarta feb reuters sofyan basir the chief executive of indonesia s power utility perusahaan listrik negara pln said on wedenesday he hoped president joko widodo would sign a new regulation on domestic coal pricing in early march
pln hopes the rules will fix coal prices at between and per tonne basir said without clarifying further
the regulations are needed to shield pln from coal price fluctuations he said adding the change would help pln return to big profits and avoid increasing tarrifs
this year pln plans to spend around trillion rupiah billion on transmission distribution and power stations in remote areas he said
the government has been working on plans to regulate domestic coal prices since late and the discussions have impacted some miners share prices
