russia says current oil prices beneficial to producers and consumers, no interest in overheating market- tv
vladivostock russia sept reuters russian energy minister alexander novak said on wednesday that current oil prices were beneficial to producers and consumers
novak told media at an economic conference in the russian far eastern city of vladivostok that no one was interested in overheating the oil market
oil prices have pushed higher in supported by production curbs solid demand and looming sanctions against iran
