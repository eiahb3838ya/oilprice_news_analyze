italy's salvini says hopes macron support dwindles at eu elections
rome jan reuters deputy prime minister matteo salvini on wednesday said he hoped the french people would not choose president emmanuel macron s party at the european parliament elections later this year heightening a dispute between rome and paris
relations between italy and france traditionally close allies have grown frosty since the league and movement formed a coalition last year and took aim at macron s en marche on the move party
salvini who is the leader of the league party also said he is not concerned that the row will affect the government s effort to save troubled airline alitalia
italian newspapers have reported that airline air is in talks with rome to take a stake in alitalia
