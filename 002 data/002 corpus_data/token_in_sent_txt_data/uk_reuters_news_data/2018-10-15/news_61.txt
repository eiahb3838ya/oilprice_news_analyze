tensions mount over disappearance of saudi journalist south korea imported no iranian crude in sept iea has trimmed its demand outlook beijing oct reuters crude oil futures rose sharply on monday as geopolitical tensions over the disappearance of a prominent saudi journalist stoked worries about supply although concerns about the outlook for demand dragged on prices
crude markets were also supported in the wake of data that showed south korea did not import any oil from iran in september for the first time in six years before sanctions against the middle east country take effect in november

crude futures climbed cents or percent to a barrel extending gains they racked up on friday after hefty losses on wednesday and thursday

saudi arabia has been under pressure since jamal khashoggi a prominent critic of riyadh and a resident disappeared on after visiting the saudi consulate in istanbul
