russia should not be allowed to use energy as a weapon - perry
budapest nov reuters russia has historically used energy as a coercive weapon and that continues today energy secretary rick perry said on tuesday during a visit to budapest
we should no longer allow the kremlin to use energy as a weapon perry told a news conference in budapest stressing the importance of diversifying energy supplies in the region
perry was speaking during a visit to east european countries as the administration of president donald trump seeks to encourage the purchase of gas from the united states or other suppliers rather than increasing purchases from russia
