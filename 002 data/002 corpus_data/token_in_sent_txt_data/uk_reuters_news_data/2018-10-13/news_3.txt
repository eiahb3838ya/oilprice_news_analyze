trump says saudi arabia faces "severe punishment" if khashoggi was killed
washington oct reuters president donald trump said in a cbs interview on saturday that there would be severe punishment for saudi arabia if it turns out that missing journalist jamal khashoggi was killed in the saudi consulate in istanbul
khashoggi a saudi journalist is a prominent critic of the saudi authorities and a legal resident of the united states
he disappeared on after visiting the consulate
trump said in an interview for minutes that there was much at stake with khashoggi case maybe especially so because he was a reporter
