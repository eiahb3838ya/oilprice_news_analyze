strike cuts french electricity generation by 2.8 gw
paris oct reuters a power sector strike reduced electricity generation at three french nuclear reactors and two generators by gigawatts gw on tuesday data by french power grid operator rte showed
the strike by the energy branch of the hardline cgt trade union is part of a nationwide protest by several unions against president emmanuel macron s reforms
rte s data showed that power generation was reduced at edf operated laurent cruas and chinon nuclear reactors while output was at zero at the cordemais and coal power plants
the generators were expected to resume production later on tuesday evening
