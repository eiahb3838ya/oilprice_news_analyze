uae to build oil pipeline between eritrea and ethiopia -fana broadcasting
nairobi aug reuters the united arab emirates will build an oil pipeline connecting eritrea s port city of assab with ethiopia s capital addis ababa an ethiopian broadcaster said on friday
fana broadcasting said the information was revealed during a meeting in addis ababa between ethiopia prime minister abiy ahmed and reem al hashimy the uae s state minister for international cooperation
fana did not provide further details
