iraq's sadr retains election victory after vote recount -state media
baghdad aug reuters iraqi cleric moqtada retained the largest amount of seats won in a may parliamentary election following a manual recount of votes results announced by the electoral commission showed early on friday
the official state news agency and broadcaster quoted the electoral commission as saying the results of the recount matched the initial results from of iraq s provinces
parliament had ordered a manual recount of votes which were initially counted electronically after a government report concluded there were serious violations
