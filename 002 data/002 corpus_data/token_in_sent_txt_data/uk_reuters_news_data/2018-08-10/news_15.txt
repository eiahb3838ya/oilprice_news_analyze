saudi arabia intercepts two missiles fired by yemen's houthis on jizan - arabiya tv
baghdad aug reuters saudi arabia intercepted two missiles on friday fired by yemen s houthi group on its southern jizan province tv reported
the houthis s masirah tv earlier announced firing a number of ballistic missiles on jizan and one missile on a saudi military base in the aseer province
both jizan and aseer provinces lie on the border with yemen
