oil output at libya's agoco back to 250,000 bpd after power problems - official
benghazi libya may reuters production at libya s arabian gulf oil company agoco has returned to normal levels at barrels per day bpd after output losses caused by power supply problems because of high temperatures an official from the company said on sunday
production had fallen to less than bpd on wednesday after unusually hot weather caused the power supply problem
