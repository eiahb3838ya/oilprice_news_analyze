yemen's aden refinery seeking 92,000 tonnes of oil products
riyadh june reuters yemen s aden refinery is seeking tonnes of oil products in a tender to address a deficit in electricity generation the state news agency saba said on thursday
the refinery is seeking tonnes of diesel and metric tonnes of heavy fuel oil mazut for power plants in aden as fuel supplies run low because of yemen s civil war
the refinery stipulated that bids should be presented in dollars by local companies with a deadline of july
the refinery offered two tenders last month to buy tons of oil products to supply the local market and address power shortages in aden
