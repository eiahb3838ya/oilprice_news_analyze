britain's quercus to cease iran activity, including 500 mln euro solar plant
oslo aug reuters british renewable energy investor quercus will cease all activities in iran including a million euro million solar power project due to recently imposed sanctions on the country the firm told reuters on tuesday
following the us sanctions on iran we have decided to cease all activities in the country including our mw project
we will continue to monitor the situation closely quercus chief executive diego biasi told reuters in an email
