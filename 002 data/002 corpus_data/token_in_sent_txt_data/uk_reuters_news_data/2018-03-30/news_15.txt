update 3-magnitude 6.9 quake off papua new guinea, tsunami danger passes
sydney reuters a magnitude earthquake struck off the southern coast of papua new guinea s new britain island on friday initially triggering a tsunami warning for surrounding coastlines but there were no immediate reports of casualties or damage
the shallow quake struck close to the coast around miles km southwest of rabaul a much more remote region than the country s mountainous mainland highlands where a magnitude tremor struck on killing people
the pacific tsunami warning centre ptwc issued a threat warning for the country s coastline located within km of the quake s epicenter but later advised that the threat had passed
dellie minding a receptionist at the rabaul hotel in the east of new britain around minutes from the coast told reuters that the earthquake was felt with many guests running outside but there was no damage
at the rapopo plantation resort on the coast receptionist may dovon said she had not heard of any casualties or damage
we felt the earthquake everything was moving so we went out of the building dovon told reuters
nothing was australian authorities said there was no threat to the australian coastline from the quake which was initially reported as a magnitude
quakes are common in papua new guinea which sits on the pacific s ring of fire a hotspot for seismic activity due to friction between tectonic plates
rabaul lies in the shadow of mount tavurvur an active volcano that destroyed the town in during a severe eruption
the latest quake came as papua new guinea struggles to get aid to survivors of the quake which flattened whole villages and spoiled water supplies on the country s main island
the impoverished country is also missing its largest revenue earner since the quake forced a shutdown of exxon mobil corp s liquefied natural gas lng project which has annual sales of billion at current lng prices
the company is still assessing quake damage at its facilities
