update 1-weak el niño likely to prevail through spring -u.s. forecaster
reuters weak el niño conditions are present and there is a chance the weather pattern will prevail through the northern hemisphere spring this year a government weather forecaster said on thursday
the last el niño a warming of ocean surface temperatures in the eastern and central pacific every few years occurred from around to and caused crop damage fires and flash floods
due to the expected weak strength widespread or significant global impacts are not anticipated
however the impacts often associated with el nino may occur in some locations during the next few months the national weather service s climate prediction center cpc said in its monthly forecast
while the chance of the weather phenomenon emerging during spring was pegged at percent in the cpc s forecast early last month the latest data showed el niño conditions formed in january
el niño can affect rainfall and temperature patterns in many regions with important consequences for agricultural and food security sectors according to the united nations world meteorological organization
