venezuela opens investigation into opposition-appointed pdvsa directors -prosecutor
caracas reuters venezuela s chief state prosecutor said on thursday an investigation had been opened into directors of oil firm pdvsa and its refiner citgo that the congress appointed on wednesday
prosecutor tarek saab in comments broadcast on state television announced the opening of an investigation against people designated illegally as directors of pdvsa and saab also said they would investigate foreign ambassadors named by opposition leader juan guaido who on jan invoked constitutional provisions to assume an interim presidency
president nicolas maduro says guaido is leading a coup
