nigeria militants vow to cripple economy if buhari re-elected
abuja feb reuters nigerian militants threatened on thursday to cripple africa s biggest economy if president muhammadu buhari is in a vote due to be held on saturday the niger delta avengers said in a statement on thursday
the group said it hoped to change buhari s rule through elections and that its preferred choice for president was opposition candidate atiku abubakar
the militants were behind a wave of violence that helped push nigeria into recession
