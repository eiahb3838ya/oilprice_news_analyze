norway's equinor bids to supply new york with offshore wind power
oslo feb reuters norway s equinor has submitted a bid to supply new york with offshore wind power from its empire wind project south of long island the company said on thursday
equinor s bid comes in response to the mw procurement of offshore wind by the new york state with the state expected to choose a supplier in spring this year
the company secured the lease in a federal auction in december with a potential capacity of up to gigawatt gw offshore wind power
