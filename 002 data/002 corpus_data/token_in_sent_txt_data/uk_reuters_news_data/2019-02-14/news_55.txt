south africa's num union warns anc over eskom split plan
johannesburg feb reuters south africa s national union of mineworkers num warned the ruling african national congress anc on thursday not to take its members votes for granted at a may election as it expressed anger at a government plan to split power firm eskom
the num which is the biggest union at eskom with around members said at a news conference that it barged into the anc s headquarters in downtown johannesburg after president cyril ramaphosa said eskom would be split into three entities
senior anc officials assured the num that the plan to split eskom would not lead to job losses or privatisation of the power utility num general secretary david sipunzi said
