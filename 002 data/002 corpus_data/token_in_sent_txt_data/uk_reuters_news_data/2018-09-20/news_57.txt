saudi's acwa power delays plans for ipo - sources
dubai sept reuters saudi arabian utility developer acwa power has delayed plans for an initial public offering originally envisaged for this year sources familiar with the matter said on thursday
it is the latest company to delay a public listing in saudi arabia at a time when the government is focused on attracting more investment to the market under its push to move the economy away from a dependence on oil revenues
a spokesman for acwa power declined to comment
the company has billion of assets under management and counts saudi sovereign wealth fund public investment fund as a shareholder
