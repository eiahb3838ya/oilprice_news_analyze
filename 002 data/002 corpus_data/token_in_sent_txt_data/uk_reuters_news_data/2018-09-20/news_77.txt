woodside to supply lng to germany's uniper for four years from 2019
singapore sept reuters woodside energy trading singapore said on thursday it signed a heads of agreement hoa with germany s uniper global commodities for the supply of up to million tonnes of liquefied natural gas lng per annum
the agreement is for a period of four years starting from the company said in a statement
the lng will be supplied from woodside s portfolio sources to markets in europe and asia chief executive peter coleman said in the statement
woodside said the hoa is conditional on the execution of a fully termed lng sales and purchase agreement
