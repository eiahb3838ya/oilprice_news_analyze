iran to spare no efforts to maintain oil production - shana
london may reuters iran will spare no efforts to maintain its oil production and exports at current levels oil minister bijan zanganeh said on wednesday adding tehran would overcome pressures resulting from the united states withdrawal from a nuclear deal
the current situation will pass and iran will emerge as a winner the oil ministry s news agency shana quoted zanganeh as saying
iran is a nation and honours its contracts zanganeh added
his comments were published after french energy group total said it would pull out of a gas project in iran if it couldn t secure a waiver from sanctions
