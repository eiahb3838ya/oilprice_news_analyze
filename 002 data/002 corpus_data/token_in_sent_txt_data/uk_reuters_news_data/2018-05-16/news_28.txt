france's total says unable to continue iran south pars project
paris may reuters french oil and energy company total said on wednesday it could not continue with the iran south pars project in light of a decision by president donald trump to withdraw from an international nuclear deal with iran
on may president donald trump announced the united states decision to withdraw from the jcpoa nuclear agreement and to reinstate the sanctions that were in force before the jcpoa s implementation subject to certain wind down periods total said in a statement
it added that as a consequence total will not continue the project and will have to unwind all related operations before november unless total is granted a specific project waiver by authorities with the support of the french and european authorities
