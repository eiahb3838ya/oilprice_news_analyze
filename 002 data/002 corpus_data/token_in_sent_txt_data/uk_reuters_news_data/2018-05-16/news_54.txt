chongqing oil and gas exchange sold 28 million cubic meters of pipeline gas in debut trading
beijing may reuters friday s debut trading on the chongqing oil and gas exchange racked up sales of about million cubic metres of pipeline gas for delivery to distributors of city gas a statement from the exchange showed
the volume was offered by the southwest sales unit of petrochina at a premium of yuan per cubic metre over city gate benchmark prices an exchange official told reuters on wednesday
the exchange sold another million cubic metres of pipeline gas from the same seller at the same price on tuesday
