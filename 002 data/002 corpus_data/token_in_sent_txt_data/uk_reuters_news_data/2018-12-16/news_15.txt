qatar petroleum signs deal with italy's eni for stake in mexican oilfields
doha dec reuters qatar petroleum qp signed on sunday an agreement with italy s eni to buy a stake in three offshore oilfields in mexico qp s chief executive said
under the deal qp will acquire a percent stake in the mexican oilfields where production is expected to start by mid and ramp up to barrels per day by qp ceo saad said at a news conference in doha
