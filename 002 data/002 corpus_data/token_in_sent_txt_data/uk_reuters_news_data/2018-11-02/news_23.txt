u.s. to grant iraq waiver over iran sanctions for gas, food items - iraqi officials
baghdad nov reuters the united states has told iraq that it will be allowed to keep importing crucial gas energy supplies and food items from iran after washington reimposes sanctions on tehran s oil sector three iraqi officials said on friday
the waiver is conditional on iraq not paying iran for the imports in dollars said the officials who included a member of iraq s ministerial committee that oversees energy activities
