u.s. top court rejects trump administration bid to halt climate trial
washington nov reuters the supreme court on friday rejected a bid by the president donald trump s administration to block a trial in a lawsuit filed by young activists who have accused the government of ignoring the perils of climate change
the loss for the administration means it now faces a examination of climate change policy during the trial that was due to begin on in eugene oregon but has since been postponed by the judge
