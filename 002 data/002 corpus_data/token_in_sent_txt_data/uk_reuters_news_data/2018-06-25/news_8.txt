bnsf says cleared tank cars from iowa track, can resume service tuesday
june reuters crews were nearly finished removing oil from derailed tank cars and repairing a stretch of northwestern iowa track damaged when a train left the track and spilled crude oil operator bnsf railway co said on monday
the crude oil train derailed near doon iowa on friday sending rail cars off the track and spilling an estimated gallons of oil into flood waters and toward a nearby river
crews are using booms to contain the oil and skimmers to remove it from the waters bnsf said
