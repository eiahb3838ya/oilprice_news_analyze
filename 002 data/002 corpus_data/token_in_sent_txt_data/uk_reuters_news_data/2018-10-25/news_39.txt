saudi energy minister expects sabic's stake purchase to be finalized in h1 2019- al-ekhbariya tv
dubai reuters saudi energy minister khalid said on thursday he expected the details of the saudi basic industries corp sabic deal to be finalised within the first half of saudi state tv quoted him as saying
added that the sale of stake in petrochemicals firm sabic to state oil giant saudi aramco is subject to regulatory conditions
aramco chief executive officer amin nasser had said on tuesday that regulations abroad will mean that the company s planned acquisition of a stake in saudi basic industries corp sabic will take time
