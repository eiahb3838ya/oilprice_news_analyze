kuwait sets oct-nov prices for new super light crude -source
oct reuters kuwait petroleum corp set the price for kuwait super light crude kslc at a barrel above quotes for october and at a premium of a barrel for november a source with knowledge of the matter said on wednesday
the official kslc selling prices osps were competitive an asian crude buyer said as they were in between the prices for two saudi light grades
kslc s osps are more than a barrel below that of saudi s arab super light crude and cents a barrel higher than saudi s arab extra light reuters data showed
kuwait the fifth largest opec producer increased its oil exports to asia by launching this new grade in the middle of this year
kslc s production is expected to reach barrels per day
