petrobras, total could form jv, bid on power projects -petrobras executive
rio de janeiro reuters an executive at brazil s petroleo brasileiro sa said the oil company could form a company with france s total to explore renewable energy business options adding that the companies could jointly bid on power generation projects
petrobras strategic director nelson silva made the comments after petrobras and total announced a agreement to assess potential businesses in onshore solar and wind power in brazil
