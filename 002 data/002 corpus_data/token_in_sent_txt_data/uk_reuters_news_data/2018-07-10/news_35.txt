iraq extends bid deadline for kut oil refinery project
baghdad july reuters iraq has extended the deadline for foreign companies and investors to bid for a project to build a refinery in kut province the oil ministry said on tuesday
investors interested in bidding have until to make offers the ministry said in a statement
the refinery south of baghdad is one of several crude oil processing projects offered by iraq as part of its plan to become in oil products
bidding documents provide for two investment models boo and boot it said
