usda to end embargoed release of data
washington july reuters the department of agriculture will no longer release data under embargo to media beginning on officials said on tuesday
the department said it was changing its policy and would only release data live on its website starting next month
the usda said there would be no changes to procedures in july
it said the crop production and world agricultural supply and demand estimates scheduled for release on thursday july at noon gmt would be available under embargo to the press as per usual
