suicide bombing inside erbil governorate-security sources
erbil iraq july reuters one of the gunmen who entered the governorate building in erbil the seat of the kurdistan regional government in northern iraq on monday carried out a suicide bombing a witness and kurdish security sources said
there was no immediate word on casualties
two policemen were wounded as the attack unfolded
