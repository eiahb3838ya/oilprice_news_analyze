beijing reuters a private oil refiner and an agricultural processor in china s shandong province have filed for bankruptcy as tightening credit and weakening market conditions exact a toll on one of china s industrial engines

both haiyou and hongju were previously controlled by sunrise
haiyou is now majority owned by the local government according to local government documents
the company is headquartered in rizhao in the eastern province of shandong
