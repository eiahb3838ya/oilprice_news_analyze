u.n. court orders uae to reunite qatari families in discrimination case
amsterdam july reuters the united arab emirates was ordered by the highest court on monday to immediately allow qatari families to reunite imposing a measure before it hears in full a discrimination case filed by qatar in june
judges at the international court of justice said the uae must also allow qatari students to complete their educations
the filing accuses the uae of human rights violations as a result of a boycott against qatar enacted last year by enacting measures including expelling qataris and closing uae airspace and seaports to qatar
reporting by anthony deutsch and bart meijer editing by john stonestreet
