nigerian unions launch general strike over minimum wage
sept reuters nigeria s main unions launched an indefinite nationwide strike on thursday in a dispute over the minimum wage after talks with the government broke down a day earlier
government offices were shut in the capital abuja while most of the city s banks were closed
it was not clear if there was any impact on airlines oil companies and crude production and most filling stations in abuja were operating as normal
