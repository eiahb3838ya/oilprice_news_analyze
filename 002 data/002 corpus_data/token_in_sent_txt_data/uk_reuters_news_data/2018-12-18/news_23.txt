germany: eu deal to cut cars' carbon emissions is a good result
berlin dec reuters german environment minister svenja schulze said on tuesday that new goals agreed by the european union to slash carbon emissions from cars by percent in a decade would help protect the climate and future jobs
schulze who said it was a good result stressed that further steps would be needed to protect the climate
the car industry will have to make an effort but this decision will not weaken europe as a centre for the auto industry rather strengthen it she said
