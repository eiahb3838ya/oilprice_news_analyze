exxon mobil opposes weakening obama-era emissions rules in letter to epa
houston dec reuters exxon mobil corp filed a letter with the environmental protection agency on monday in support of methane gas emission rules put in place under the obama administration
the trump administration in september proposed weakening requirements for repairing leaks of the greenhouse gas in drilling operations in a step toward rolling back an policy to combat climate change
