volkswagen to review investment plans to meet new eu co2 targets - ceo
berlin dec reuters volkswagen will have to review its plans for a shift toward mass production of electric vehicles to meet new european union targets in ceo herbert diess said in a statement on tuesday
the new european rules require carmakers to reduce carbon emissions from cars across their fleets by percent between and
volkswagen s ceo said the rules would mean that vw will have to hike its electric car production to a level that it can not meet with its current overhaul programme
due to the current decisions a review of our planning is necessary which will be done in autumn in line with our planning calendar he said
