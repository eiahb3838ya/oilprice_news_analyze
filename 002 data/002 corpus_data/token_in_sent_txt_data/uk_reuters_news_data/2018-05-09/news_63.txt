britain tells trump: spell out how you will deal with iran now
london may reuters britain called on the united states to spell out its vision of a new negotiated settlement with iran after president donald trump pulled out of the nuclear deal
now that the trump administration has left the jcpoa the responsibility falls on them to describe how they in washington will build a new negotiated solution to our shared concerns foreign secretary boris johnson told parliament
johnson said he had no problem with trump s goal for a lasting solution to iran s nuclear threat but added the question is how does the propose to achieve it reporting by estelle shirbon editing by andrew faulconbridge
