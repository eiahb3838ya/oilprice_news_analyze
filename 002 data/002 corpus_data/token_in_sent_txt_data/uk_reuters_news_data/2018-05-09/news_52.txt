nigeria's parliament aims to pass major oil reforms by july -senate president
abuja may reuters nigeria s parliament aims to pass a bill to overhaul the oil sector by the end of july senate president bukola saraki told reuters
the petroleum industry bill pib which aims to improve transparency attract investors and stimulate growth has been debated for more than a decade and was broken into sections to help it pass into law
