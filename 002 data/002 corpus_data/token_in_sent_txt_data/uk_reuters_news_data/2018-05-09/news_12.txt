opec to discuss possible shortfall in crude supplies -iraqi oil minister
basra iraq may reuters iraqi oil minister jabar said on wednesday that opec will discuss a possible shortfall in global crude oil supplies following president donald trump s decision to withdraw from the iran nuclear deal
