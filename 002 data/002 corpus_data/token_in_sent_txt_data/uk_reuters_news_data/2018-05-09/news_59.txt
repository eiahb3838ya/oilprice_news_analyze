russia's putin held brief meeting with iraqi kurdistan pm - tass
moscow may reuters russian president vladimir putin held a brief meeting with the prime minister of iraq s kurdistan region nechirvan barzani in moscow on wednesday a kremlin spokesman is quoted as saying by tass news agency
on tuesday a kurdistan regional government official said that barzani would travel to moscow at the request of rosneft chief igor sechin
rosneft is a major investor in iraqi kurdistan and controls its oil pipelines
the company did not immediately respond to a request for comment
