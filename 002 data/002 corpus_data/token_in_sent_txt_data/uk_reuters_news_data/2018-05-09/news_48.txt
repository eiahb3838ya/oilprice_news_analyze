s.korea seeks to minimise impact on its firms of u.s. pullout from iran deal
seoul may reuters south korea said on wednesday it will work to minimise the impact on south korean firms of the decision to withdraw from an international nuclear deal with iran
in a statement the presidential blue house also said it supported efforts to resolve the iran issue peacefully
president donald trump on tuesday pulled the united states out of the agreement raising the risk of conflict in the middle east upsetting european allies and casting uncertainty over global oil supplies
