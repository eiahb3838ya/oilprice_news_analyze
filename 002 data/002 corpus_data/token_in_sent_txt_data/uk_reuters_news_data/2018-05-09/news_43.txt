students protest outside former u.s. embassy in tehran -fars news
beirut may reuters iranian students gathered outside the former embassy in tehran on wednesday to protest president donald trump s decision to withdraw from the iran nuclear deal according to fars news
the students chanted death to america and the only demand is to burn the nuclear deal and burned the american flag according to fars news
the report did not indicate how many students attended the protest
