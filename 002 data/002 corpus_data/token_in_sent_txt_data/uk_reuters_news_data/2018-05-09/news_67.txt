iran still implementing commitments under nuclear deal, iaea says
vienna may reuters iran is continuing to implement its nuclear commitments under a deal with major powers the atomic watchdog policing the deal said on wednesday a day after president donald trump said washington was pulling out of the accord
iran is subject to the world s most robust nuclear verification regime under the jcpoa which is a significant verification gain international atomic energy agency iaea chief yukiya amano said in a statement using the deal s official name the joint comprehensive plan of action
as of today the iaea can confirm that the commitments are being implemented by iran he said adding that the iaea was closely monitoring developments related to the deal
