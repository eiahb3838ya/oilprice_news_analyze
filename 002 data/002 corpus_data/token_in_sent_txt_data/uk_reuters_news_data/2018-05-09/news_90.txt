french carmaker psa hopes eu will take common position regarding iran
paris reuters french carmaker and psa said on wednesday that it hoped the european union would adopt a common position on iran following president donald trump s decision to pull the out of a nuclear deal with iran
like other economic players we are following the evolution of the matter and are also following the eu s official position on this topic said a spokesman for psa adding that psa hoped the eu s position regarding iran would be a singular one
on may president donald trump on tuesday pulled the united states out of an international nuclear deal with iran raising the risk of conflict in the middle east upsetting european allies and casting uncertainty over global oil supplies
france which has had close business ties with iran since before the fall of the shah in has sought to deepen trade ties since sanctions against iran were lifted in
major french companies operating in iran include psa rival carmaker renault and oil major total while airbus has also won orders from iranair
