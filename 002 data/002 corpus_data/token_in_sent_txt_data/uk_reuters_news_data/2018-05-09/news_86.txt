iraq sets june 14 bid deadline for kut oil refinery project
baghdad may reuters investors interested in bidding for a project to build a refinery in kut have until june to make offers an oil ministry statement said on wednesday
the refinery south of baghdad is one of several crude oil processing projects offered by iraq as part of plan to become in oil products
iraq is the crude oil producer in the organization of the petroleum exporting countries after saudi arabia
