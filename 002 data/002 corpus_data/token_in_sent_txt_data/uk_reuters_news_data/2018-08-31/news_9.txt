update 1-tunisian energy minister, officials dismissed over corruption accusations - source
adds details tunis aug reuters tunisia s prime minister youssef chahed sacked the energy minister khaled gadour and four other senior officials linked to that ministry on friday over corruption accusations an official source said
chahed ordered an investigation into the accusations and told officials to merge the ministries of energy and industry the source told reuters
the four dismissed officials were the secretary of state for energy the director general of fuel the head of the national oil company etap and the ministry s director general of legal affairs the source added
there was no immediate comment from the sacked minister or officials
