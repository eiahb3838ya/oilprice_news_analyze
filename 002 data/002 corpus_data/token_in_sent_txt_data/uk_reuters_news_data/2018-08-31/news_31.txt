private chinese trader delivers crude oil against shanghai contract
beijing aug reuters shanghai zhida hailan energy co ltd a private energy trader set up in may last year is one of the companies that delivered crude oil against shanghai september futures contract on friday a company spokesman told reuters
the company delivered barrels of iraqi oil basra light into a storage in zhanjiang
the crude contract on the shanghai international energy exchange ine expired on friday with five companies set to deliver a total of barrels of crude oil through the exchange next month
