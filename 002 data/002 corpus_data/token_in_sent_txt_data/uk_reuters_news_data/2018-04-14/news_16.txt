china to set up hainan free trade zone by 2020, port by 2025-xinhua
shanghai april reuters china aims to establish a free trade zone in hainan by and will encourage multinationals to set up their international and regional headquarters in the province state news agency xinhua reported citing a government document
the guidance was jointly issued by the ruling communist party and state council xinhua said
it comes after president xi jinping announced on friday that the country would grant foreign firms greater economic freedom in the southernmost province
known for its sandy beaches and coast hainan is the headquarters of china s services conglomerate hna
this week the province hosted the boao forum asia s equivalent of davos
the document said that the government would set up an investment fund to support the building of a free trade port in hainan by with the aim that the system will become mature by
it will also phase out sales of traditional vehicles for greater environmental conservation in hainan and encourage foreign firms to participate in the building of the free trade port
