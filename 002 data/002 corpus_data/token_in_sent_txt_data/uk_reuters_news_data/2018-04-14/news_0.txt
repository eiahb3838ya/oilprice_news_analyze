lima-u.s. vice president pence says  now is the time to do more to isolate venezuelan president maduro
lima reuters vice president mike pence told regional leaders on saturday that more must be done to isolate venezuelan president nicolas maduro and that washington would not stand idly by as the country crumbles
in a plenary session at the summit of the americas in lima peru pence called for maduro to allow humanitarian aid to reach venezuelans facing food and medicine shortages amid a severe economic crisis
pence also said russia was spreading misinformation about missile attacks in syria late on friday
