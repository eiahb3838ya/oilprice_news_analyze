mercuria agrees to take stake in chemchina's oil refining system - sources
london april reuters global energy trader mercuria group agreed to take an interest in the oil refining system of china s chemchina sources familiar with the matter said
as part of the deal chemchina would increase its stake in mercuria
chemchina already holds a percent stake in the trading firm and would remain a minority shareholder
the deal once signed would be the first time that a foreign company had gained a stake in chinese refineries
reporting by aizhu chen in beijing and julia payne in london editing by toby chopra
