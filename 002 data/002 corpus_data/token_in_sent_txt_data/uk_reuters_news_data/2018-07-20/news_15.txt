gabon oil workers' union ends strike at total facilities
dakar reuters gabon s oil workers union onep on friday ended a strike at total facilities across the country after days it said in a statement
the strike was meant to last until next tuesday over demands for higher pay and career advancement opportunities
it was not clear if total made any concessions although onep said it might resume the strike if the company did not meet its demands
