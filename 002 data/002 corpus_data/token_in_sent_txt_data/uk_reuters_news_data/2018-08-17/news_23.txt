update 1-papua new guinea gas project signs supply deal with bp
reuters an gas project in papua new guinea has agreed a deal to supply liquefied natural gas lng to a unit of british oil giant bp said australia s oil search ltd a partner in the development
the agreement starting this month will provide bp with about tonnes of lng annually over an initial period rising to about tonnes for the following two years oil search said in a statement on friday
it did not give any value for the deal
the move takes the total contracted volumes from the project to approximately mtpa million tonnes per annum said oil search managing director peter botten
the agreement comes a month after oil search announced a similar deal with petrochina co ltd
the company also said that on behalf of the project exxonmobil was in negotiations with several other parties over an additional tonnes per year of supply
earlier this month oil search resumed operations to produce oil at the agogo production facility which was knocked out by a major earthquake in papua new guinea in february
