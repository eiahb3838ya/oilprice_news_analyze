denmark's maersk to spin off drilling business, sell down total stake
copenhagen aug reuters danish shipping company
said on friday it would spin off its offshore drilling operation and list the unit in copenhagen next year
maersk also said in a statement that following the demerger of maersk drilling a material part of its remaining shares in french oil major total will be distributed to its shareholders
the danish company cut its profit outlook this month and as part of a major restructuring last year sold its oil and gas business maersk oil to total in a billion deal that saw it take an equity stake in the french firm
the announcement came as maersk confirmed preliminary earnings before interest tax depreciation and amortisation ebitda of million
