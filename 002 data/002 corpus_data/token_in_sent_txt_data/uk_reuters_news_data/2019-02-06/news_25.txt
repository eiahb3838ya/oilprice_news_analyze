venezuelan opposition to open u.s. fund to receive oil sale revenues -lawmaker
caracas feb reuters venezuela s opposition is opening a bank fund in which income from oil sales could be received a key measure to secure financing for their effort to dislodge president nicolas maduro an opposition lawmaker told reuters on wednesday
this is already quite advanced i hope that next week it can be announced by our representative in the united states carlos paparoni said in an interview
in a separate interview yon goicoechea a member of opposition leader juan guaido s policy team told reuters that guaido was in contact with oil firm pdvsa s partners and they were willing to keep operating in venezuela
