saudi crown prince to visit britain on march 7 - pm may's spokesman
london feb reuters saudi crown prince mohammed bin salman will visit britain on march for talks with prime minister theresa may to include topics such as extremism and societal reform may s spokesman said on tuesday
the visit will usher in a new era in bilateral relations focused on a partnership that delivers benefits for both the united kingdom and the kingdom of saudi arabia the spokesman said
the visit will also provide an opportunity to enhance our in tackling international challenges such as terrorism extremism the conflict and humanitarian crisis in yemen and other regional issues such as iraq and reporting by william james editing by stephen addison
