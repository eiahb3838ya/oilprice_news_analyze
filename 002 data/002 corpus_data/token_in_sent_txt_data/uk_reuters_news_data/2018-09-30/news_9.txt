puk says it will not recognise results of kurdish parliamentary election
iraq sept reuters the patriotic union of kurdistan puk one of the dominant kurdish parties in northern iraq said it would not recognise the results of sunday s parliamentary election in the region
the puk is competing with the kurdistan democratic party kdp in the poll one year after the kurds failed bid for independence from the rest of iraq
