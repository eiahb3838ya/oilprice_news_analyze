update 1-arson at libyan pipeline causes fire, no significant damage - noc
adds details tunis aug reuters libya s national oil corp noc said arson caused a fire next to a station on libya s gas pipeline that was extinguished on thursday without causing significant damage
the noc said in a statement the arson was carried out with grenades gasoline and burnt car tyres by unknown criminals
the pipeline connects wafa field about miles southwest of the capital tripoli to the mellitah complex on libya s northern coast
it is operated by mellitah oil and gas a joint venture between the noc and italy s eni and produces crude oil condensate and natural gas liquids
the noc said the fire broke out next to station in ajaylat close to the coast
