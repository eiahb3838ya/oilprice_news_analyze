france's accorhotels in talks to buy movenpick -le figaro
paris april reuters french hotel group accorhotels is in talks to buy hotel chain movenpick hotels resorts le figaro newspaper reported in its thursday edition
the paper did not provide any further details
a spokeswoman for accor declined to comment
movenpick which manages rooms in more than hotels across africa asia europe and the middle east could not be reached for comment
accorhotels europe s biggest hotel group said last week it will make further acquisitions in coming years using cash from the sale of a majority stake in its property business rather than pay a special dividend
