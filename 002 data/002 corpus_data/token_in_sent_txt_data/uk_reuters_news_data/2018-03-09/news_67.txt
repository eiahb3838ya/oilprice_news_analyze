s.africa's competition watchdog approves sinopec, chevron deal
johannesburg march reuters south africa s competition tribunal on friday approved subject to conditions a deal for china s sinopec corp to buy chevron s assets in south africa and botswana
sinopec was competing for the assets with commodities trader and miner glencore which swooped in last october with a million bid following delays to sinopec s original agreement
