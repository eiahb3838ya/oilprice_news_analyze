norway's hydro sees negative impact from u.s. aluminium tariff
oslo march reuters norwegian norsk hydro asa expects to see a negative impact from the decision to impose import tariffs a company spokesman said on friday
the introduction of unilateral trade barriers such as quotas and tariffs will negatively effect hydro and may distort normal trade said company spokesman oeyvind breivik
there is some uncertainty over which countries can or will be exempted from the barriers so the concrete effects for hydro are somewhat uncertain he added
