china's huarong asset buys 36.2 pct stake in cefc china unit - filing
beijing reuters chinese asset manager huarong asset management co ltd has bought a percent stake in the subsidiary of cefc china energy that is acquiring a billion stake in russia s rosneft a filing by the cefc subsidiary showed on friday
a subsidiary of asset manager huarong acquired the stake in cefc hainan in two tranches in december and in february the filing by cefc hainan showed
cefc and cefc hainan did not immediately comment
