iranian guards kidnapped on border with pakistan - irna
london oct reuters fourteen iranian border guards were kidnapped on the border with pakistan on tuesday an official was quoted as saying by state news agency irna
the official said the kidnappers were members of a terrorist group but gave no more details
these people were kidnapped around am in lulakdan border area irna quoted the official as saying without naming the person
lulakdan is in sistan and baluchestan province
