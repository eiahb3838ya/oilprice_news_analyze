ecopetrol to focus spending on drilling for rest of year - ceo
new york aug reuters colombia s ecopetrol sa will focus spending for the rest of the year on increased drilling activity and securing operational licenses its chief executive said on friday
ceo felipe bayon said in an interview at the new york stock exchange that achieving a billion to billion capital spending plan this year will be challenging because of delays earlier this year
officials were in new york to recognize the anniversary of the company s nyse listing
reporting by devika krishna kumar in new york editing by marguerita choy
