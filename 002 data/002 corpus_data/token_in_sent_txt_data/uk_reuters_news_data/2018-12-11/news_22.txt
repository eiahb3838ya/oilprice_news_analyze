cuadrilla pauses gas fracking at english site after tremor
london dec reuters british shale gas company cuadrilla has paused fracking at its preston new road site in lancashire northwest england after tremor of magnitude was detected the company said
cuadrilla will pause and continue to monitor micro seismicity for at least the next hours
well integrity has been checked and verified cuadrilla said in a statement
