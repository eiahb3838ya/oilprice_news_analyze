chevron says looking to resume share buybacks
march reuters oil and natural gas producer chevron corp said it expects to increase its free cash flow this year onwards putting it in a position to resume its share buyback program
chevron had halted its share repurchase plans in to conserve cash amid a slump in oil prices
the company did not specify when it plans to restart the program
reporting by ernest scheyder in houston and yashaswini swamynathan in bengaluru editing by saumyadeb chakrabarty
