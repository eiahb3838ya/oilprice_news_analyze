update 2-boeing snares hawaiian airlines widebody jet order from airbus
new york reuters planemaker boeing co has snared a hawaiian airlines order for widebody jets from european rival airbus the carrier announced on tuesday in a striking blow to airbus amid dwindling widebody sales
hawaiian said it had placed an order for boeing aircraft with the first expected to arrive in
the carrier dropped an earlier order of airbus to go with the boeing series

it was a complex decision that factored in many aspects of aircraft capability passenger experience fleet complexity and the total lifetime cost of ownership and the came out on top hawaiian spokesman alex da silva said
