russia says opec, non-opec to discuss output scenarios in algeria
moscow sept reuters russian energy minister alexander novak said on monday that all possible scenarios for oil output could be discussed at a meeting of opec and states in algeria this month
asked whether a deal between opec and other producers a group known as could discuss increasing output from levels agreed in june novak said i think we have the possibility to discuss any possible novak said would also discuss supply and demand forecasts for the fourth quarter
he added that russia was also ready to discuss cooperation with the united states to balance the oil market but that such discussions were not being held
