hearings in vw investor lawsuit on hold until end-nov
braunschweig germany reuters investors suing volkswagen for billions in compensation from the hit to the carmaker s share price from its diesel emissions scandal will have to wait until for court hearings to continue a german judge ruled on monday
certain claims that have been excluded from the proceedings so far will then be considered and the basis of calculation for possible compensation payments will be decided by then presiding judge christian jaede said on monday
shareholders representing claims are seeking billion euros billion in damages over the scandal in a court in the city of braunschweig
the scandal broke in september and has cost volkswagen vw billion euros in penalties and fines so far
