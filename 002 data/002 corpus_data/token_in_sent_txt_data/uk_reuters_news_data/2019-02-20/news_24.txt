nigeria ready to reduce oil output to boost prices - statement
abuja feb reuters nigeria is willing to reduce oil output to help secure higher prices the spokesman for president muhammadu buhari said in a statement on wednesday after an envoy from saudi arabia called on the african nation to adhere to a deal on production cuts
the president told the visiting saudi envoy that nigeria was willing to go along with the saudi initiative in limiting output so that prices would go up the presidential spokesman said
buhari added we will nigeria a member of the organization of the petroleum exporting countries is party to a deal to curb output to support prices although opec data showed its output rose in january
