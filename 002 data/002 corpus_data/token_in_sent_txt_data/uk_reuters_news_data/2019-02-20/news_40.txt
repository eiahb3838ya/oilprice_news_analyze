south africa's eskom to get 69 bln rand bailout over 3 years
cape town feb reuters south africa will inject billion rand billion a year over the next three years into state power utility eskom but will not take on billion rand of debt as requested by the struggling firm the national budget showed on wednesday
the treasury said the first step in a plan announced by president cyril ramaphosa this month to split eskom into three entities will be to transfer a portion of the utility s assets to a new transmission subsidiary that will invite equity partners to provide capital
further financial support to eskom would depend on the country s economic growth electricity tariffs and the implementation of the company s strategy the treasury said in its budget statement
