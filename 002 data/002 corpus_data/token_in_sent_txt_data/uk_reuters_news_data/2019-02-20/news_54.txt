dubai's enoc partners with india's ioc to expand abroad
dubai feb reuters united arab emirates national oil company enoc group said on wednesday it had formed an alliance with indian oil company ioc as part of the firm s global expansion strategy
the partnership will expand enoc s presence to over ports in countries to provide its customers with marine lubricants and technical services the company said in a statement
enoc is wholly owned by the government of dubai an emirate in the uae
