nigeria petroleum group cuts petrol price to encourage people to travel to vote
abuja feb reuters nigeria s main fuel marketing group has reduced the price of petrol to encourage people to travel to vote in presidential elections this saturday after the poll was delayed by a week the information minister said on wednesday
lai mohammed said the independent petroleum marketers association of nigeria ipman had cut the price of petrol per litre to naira from naira and that the discount is valid between the of february and the this year to allow those who want to go back home to vote
