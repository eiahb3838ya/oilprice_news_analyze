russian energy minister says he met with u.s. treasury's mnuchin, discussed sanctions
washington june reuters russian energy minister alexander novak said on tuesday that he met with treasury secretary steven mnuchin and they discussed sanctions on russia
novak made the comment in a press briefing in washington on the sidelines of the world gas conference
we met
we discussed energy issues among other things
we touched upon questions related to sanctions novak said
