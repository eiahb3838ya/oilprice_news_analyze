norway oil workers and employers agree deal, strike ends
oslo july reuters close to striking workers on norwegian drilling rigs will end their industrial action after their union and the lobby group reprensenting their employers reached a deal the norwegian shipowners association said on thursday
the strike began on july after the safe union and the norwegian shipowners association could not come to an agreement over wages and pension conditions
the industrial action led to the shutdown of one field
