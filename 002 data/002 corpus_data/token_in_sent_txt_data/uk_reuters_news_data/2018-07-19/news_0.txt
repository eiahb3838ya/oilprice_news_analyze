russia, china delay u.s. push for halt to refined petroleum sales to n.korea
united nations july reuters russia and china delayed on thursday a push for a security council committee to order a halt to refined petroleum exports to north korea asking for more detail on a accusation that pyongyang has breached a cap diplomats said
russia s mission to the united nations told the committee it is closely examining this request and is seeking additional information on every single case of illegal transfer of petroleum diplomats said
china backed the request for information
reporting by michelle nichols
