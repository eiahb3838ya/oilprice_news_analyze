u.s. to sell 7 mln barrels of sweet crude from strategic reserve
washington march reuters the department of energy said on thursday it will sell million barrels of sweet crude oil from the country s petroleum reserve as it complies with a law to help fund the federal government
the doe will accept offers on the oil until march
delivery of the crude will be from may to may from the strategic petroleum reserve s spr west hackberry site and from may to may from its bryan mound site the doe said
the spr currently holds million barrels of oil more than enough to meet a spare supply agreement with the international energy agency
as domestic oil output has grown with the fracking revolution lawmakers have increasingly seen sales from the reserve as a way to help fund the government
