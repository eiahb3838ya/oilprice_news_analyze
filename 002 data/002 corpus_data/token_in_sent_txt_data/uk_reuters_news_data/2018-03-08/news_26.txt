shell, blackstone working on $10 bln joint bid for bhp's u.s. shale assets -sky news
march reuters royal dutch shell plc and american private equity firm blackstone group lp are working on a joint bid for bhp billiton plc s shale assets for billion sky news reported
the shale auction is expected to lead to a deal later this year sky news said
bhp had put up the shale assets for sale last year
bhp declined to comment
shell and blackstone could not be immediately reached for comment
