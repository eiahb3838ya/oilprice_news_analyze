update 2-shell, blackstone eye $10 bln bid for bhp u.s. shale assets -sky news
houston reuters royal dutch shell plc and private equity firm blackstone group lp are working on a joint billion billion pounds bid for bhp billiton plc s shale assets sky news said on thursday
shell and blackstone s offer will only be one of several credible proposals that bhp will receive for its shale operations sky news said citing banking sources
bhp shell and blackstone declined to comment
private equity companies have been active buyers of shale producing assets in recent years
this week devon energy said it sold some assets in texas for million to a buyer that tudor pickering holt energy analysts identified as a private equity firm
devon did not disclose the name of the buyer
bhp s acreage is located adjacent to assets controlled by shell and anadarko petroleum corp in the permian basin of west texas and new mexico
anadarko bid late last year on bhp s onshore properties according to two people familiar with the transaction
anadarko did not immediately respond to requests for comment
steve pastor bhp s president for petroleum operations said this week the company would consider swapping certain onshore oil and gas assets with competitors offshore assets as part of its effort to exit shale operations
bhp the world s largest miner said last month it expected to receive initial bids for the operations in the june quarter
the company last year said it would exit its underperforming shale oil and gas business after coming under pressure from tribeca investment partners to sell the assets
activist hedge fund elliott management has also called for bhp s exit from shale to free up capital
