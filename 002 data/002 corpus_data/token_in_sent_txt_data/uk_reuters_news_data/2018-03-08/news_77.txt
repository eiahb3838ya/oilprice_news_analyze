china's cnpc says to buy 30-pct stake in brazilian fuel distributor
beijing march reuters china s cnpc said on thursday that its international unit petrochina international co has agreed to buy a stake in brazilian oil products trader tt work as it pushes to expand abroad
tt work is brazil s fourth largest oil products distributor with more than storage facilities serving petrol stations in the south american country according to a statement from cnpc
no financial details were disclosed
the step comes as petrochina s oil and gas trading arm looks to expand globally almost a year after a veteran fuel marketing executive took charge of the business
reuters reported on the international expansion plan in december
reporting by meng meng and josephine mason
