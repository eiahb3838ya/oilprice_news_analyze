vietnam revokes $3.2 bln oil refinery project license
hanoi reuters vietnam has revoked the investment licence for a billion pounds oil refinery project in the country s central province of phu yen a local government official told reuters on thursday
the facility was due to be built through investments from technostar management ltd and russia s telloil group to produce liquefied petroleum gas jet fuel gasoline and diesel according to state media
the refinery was scheduled to become operational this year but construction hadn t started yet said tran thien kim deputy director of the province s planning and investment department
the investor failed to deliver on investment pledges as stated in the licence said kim who added that the province had informed the companies of the decision
they are happy with the decision because it s in line with their plan to scrap the project due to unfavourable market conditions he said
it is not uncommon for projects in vietnam to have investment licenses revoked often because of strict regulations and requirements from the authorities related to the speed of construction and the environment
vietnam has one operational oil refinery which meets around of the country s demand for fuels
a second oil refinery will start commercial production from next month
