turkey says to take every initiative to protect firms from u.s. sanctions
ankara reuters turkey will take every initiative to protect its firms from sanctions ankara s foreign ministry spokesman said on friday a day after the united states imposed sanctions on several iranian and turkish companies in a move targeting iranian airlines
hami aksoy also told a news conference that turkey had fulfilled all requirements to procure lockheed martin s jets from the united states and that ankara expected all sides to carry out their responsibilities
a senate committee passed its version of a billion defence policy bill on thursday including a measure to prevent turkey from purchasing the jets
