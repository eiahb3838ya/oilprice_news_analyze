talks with powers to continue over saving nuclear deal, iran says
vienna may reuters talks with european powers on an economic package aimed at salvaging the iran nuclear deal will continue after a round of discussions that took place in vienna on friday iran s deputy foreign minister said
for the time being we are negotiating to see if they can provide us with a package which can actually give iran the benefits of and then the next step is to find guarantees for that package and we need both legal and political commitments by the remaining participants in the jcpoa deal abbas araqchi told reporters
he was speaking after the meeting with counterparts from britain china france germany and russia
