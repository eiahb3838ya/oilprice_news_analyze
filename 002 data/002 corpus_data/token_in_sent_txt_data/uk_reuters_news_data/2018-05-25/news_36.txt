update 1-rosneft boosts clout in iraqi kurdistan with gas pipeline deal
adds detail by dmitry zhdannikov st petersburg may reuters russian state oil major rosneft signed a deal to help kurdistan develop its gas reserves and build a gas pipeline expanding the company s dominance in the energy sector of iraq s region
rosneft said it would conduct a front engineering and design of a gas pipeline in kurdistan as part of its plan to build an integrated gas business value chain in the region
it said it would focus on partnerships and project financing for gas projects in kurdistan
rosneft has agreed to invest billions of dollars in kurdistan s oil projects in the past two years including securing control of the region s oil pipeline
that pipeline gave rosneft access to crucial infrastructure that ships most of the region s oil to global markets via turkish territory
it made the firm the biggest investor in the region by far turning erbil traditionally a strong ally into an important partner for moscow as russian president vladimir putin seeks to expand influence in the middle east
russia chose not to oppose the kurdistan regional government s failed independence referendum last year which was dismissed as illegal by baghdad and caused alarm in washington the european union iran and turkey
rosneft also said it aimed to produce up to barrels per day of oil in kurdistan by the end of this year
