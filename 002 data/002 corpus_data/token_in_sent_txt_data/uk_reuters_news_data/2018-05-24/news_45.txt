indonesia to roll out 25 pct biodiesel rule from 2019 - official
jakarta may reuters indonesia will make it mandatory for bidiesel to contain percent bio content from onwards an energy ministry official said on thursday
starting new and renewable energy director rida mulyana told reuters by text message in response to questions on the matter
mulyana did not provide further detail
rules introduced in require a percent bio content in biodiesel for land transportation from january to january after which a percent bio content would be mandatory
according to a local media report the energy ministry is now drafting technical requirements for the rule
