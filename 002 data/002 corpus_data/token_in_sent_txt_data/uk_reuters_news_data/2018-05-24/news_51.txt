oil prices drop on potential increase in opec output
seoul may reuters oil prices fell on thursday pulled down by expectations that opec members could step up production in the face of worries over supply from both venezuela and iran
international benchmark brent futures were down cents or percent at per barrel at gmt
west texas intermediate wti crude futures were down cents or percent at a barrel
the organization of petroleum exporting countries opec may decide to increase oil output to make up reduced supply from iran and venezuela in response to concerns from washington over a rally in oil prices opec and oil industry sources told reuters
supply concerns in iran and venezuela following new sanctions had pushed both brent and wti to highs with brent breaking through a threshold last week for the first time since november
the chat is still that opec will do something at its june meeting in reaction to the looming prospect of a fall in crude production and exports from both iran and venezuela as the year progresses said greg mckenna chief market strategist at cfd and fx provider axitrader
opec and some major oil producers are scheduled to meet in vienna on june
the group previously agreed to curb their output by about million barrels per day to boost oil prices and clear a supply glut
any signs that the group may be heading towards an early exit from the production cut agreement would weigh on prices anz bank said in a note
a surprise increase in weekly crude stockpiles also kept a lid on oil prices
commercial crude inventories rose by million barrels in the week to may beating analyst expectations for a decrease of million barrels the energy information administration eia said on wednesday
meanwhile libya which is an opec member cut its oil production by about barrels per day as unusually hot weather prompted power problems an official from the national oil corp said on wednesday
