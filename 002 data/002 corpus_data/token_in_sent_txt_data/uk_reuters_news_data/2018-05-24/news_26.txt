iran staying within nuclear deal's restrictions -iaea report
vienna may reuters iran has stayed within the main restrictions on its nuclear activities imposed by its deal with major powers despite the pullout from the agreement the atomic watchdog indicated on thursday
in its first such report since president donald trump announced washington s withdrawal on may the international atomic energy agency said iran had complied with limits on the level to which it can enrich uranium its stock of enriched uranium and other items
it did however rebuke iran for dragging its feet over complementary access inspections under the iaea s additional protocol which iran is implementing under the deal
