u.s. targets airlines in latest iran sanctions move
washington may reuters the united states on thursday imposed sanctions on several iranian and turkish companies and a number of aircraft in a move targeting four iranian airlines
the companies targeted were linked to mahan air and meraj air the treasury department said in a statement on its website
it also said it was targeting a number of their aircraft as well as aircraft from caspian airlines and pouya air
reporting by tim ahmann and makini brice
