iran leader backs rouhani's suggestion iran to block gulf oil exports if own sales stopped
dubai july reuters iran s supreme leader on saturday backed president hassan rouhani s suggestion that iran may block gulf oil exports if its own exports are stopped according to his official website
ayatollah ali khamenei said remarks by the president that if iran s oil is not exported no regional country s oil will be exported were important remarks that reflect the policy and the approach of iran s system khamenei s website said
