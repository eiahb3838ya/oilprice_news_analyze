china's cnpc takes over total's share in iran gas project - iranian agency
dubai aug reuters china s energy major cnpc has taken over the share in iran s south pars gas project held by france s total the iranian state news agency irna reported on saturday
total has said it would pull out unless it secured a sanctions waiver
china national petroleum corp cnpc has replaced total of france with a percent stake in the phase of the south pars gas field irna quoted mohammad mostafavi director of investment of iran s state oil firm nioc as saying
there was no immediate confirmation of the report by cnpc
