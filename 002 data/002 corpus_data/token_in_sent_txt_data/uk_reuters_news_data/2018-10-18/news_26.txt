egypt prosecutor refers 3 oil industry execs for trial for alleged corruption -judicial source
cairo oct reuters egypt s public prosecutor has referred three executives from a private petroleum company for trial over an alleged corruption scam worth close to billion a judicial source said on thursday
the firm s former deputy chairman and two others allegedly manipulated its books and siphoned funds into personal foreign bank accounts in the source and state news agency mena said
the firm then known as tri ocean has since been renamed mog energy
no one at mog energy was immediately available for comment
