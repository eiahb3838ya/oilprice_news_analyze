engie says belgium's tihange 3 reactor to restart ahead of schedule
paris dec reuters engie electrabel the belgian subsidiary of french energy group engie has launched plans to restart its tihange nuclear reactor after completing repair works and receiving the green light from the nuclear regulator
the company said it received clearance from belgium s afcn regulator on and the belgian reactor was now expected back on the electricity grid by january nearly two months ahead of the initial start date of march
