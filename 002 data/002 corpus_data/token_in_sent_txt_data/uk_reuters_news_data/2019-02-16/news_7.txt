nigeria opposition candidate atiku abubakar urges voters to be patient
yola nigeria feb reuters nigerian opposition candidate atiku abubakar on saturday appealed to voters to be patient following the postponement of a presidential election by a week
atiku told reporters at his residence that he was shocked by the electoral commission s decision to delay the vote until
i m appealing to nigerians to please come out and vote and i m asking them to be patient about it he said
