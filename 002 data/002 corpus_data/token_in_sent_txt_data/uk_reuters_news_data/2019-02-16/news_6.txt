iraq lowers majnoon oilfield output to 104,000 bpd - source
basra iraq feb reuters iraq has lowered production at its giant majnoon oilfield to barrels per day bpd this month from bpd in january in line with an opec supply agreement a senior oil ministry source told reuters on saturday
iraq is producing below its maximum capacity of nearly million bpd in line with an agreement between opec and other exporters such as russia to curtail global supply in order to support prices
