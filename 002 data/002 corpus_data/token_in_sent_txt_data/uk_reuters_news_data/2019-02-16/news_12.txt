nigeria presidential election postponed to feb 23 - electoral commission chairman
abuja feb reuters nigeria s presidential election which was due to be held on saturday has been postponed to the chairman of the country s electoral commission said on saturday
addressing journalists hours before polls were due to open independent national electoral commision inec chairman mahmood yakubu said proceeding as previously scheduled was not feasible
he said the delay was needed to hold a free and fair election
