nigeria's president appeals for calm after election delay
abuja reuters nigeria s government is committed to ensuring that saturday s presidential election will be free fair and peaceful president muhammadu buhari said in a televised address on thursday
he also said africa s biggest economy was back on the path of steady growth after emerging from the recession
buhari s main opponent in the election is atiku abubakar a businessman and former vice president
