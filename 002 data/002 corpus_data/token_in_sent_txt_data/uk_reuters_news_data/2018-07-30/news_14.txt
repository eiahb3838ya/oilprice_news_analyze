update 1-workers at total's uk oil platforms stage strike over rotas
london reuters workers at three of total s british offshore oil and gas platforms went ahead with a strike on monday a spokesman for unite said with further industrial action planned if no agreement on new rotas is reached
the strike by workers at the alwyn elgin and dunbar platforms in the north sea began at gmt
it followed a stoppage at the same sites last monday but stored crude cushioned against any immediate oil supply disruption
