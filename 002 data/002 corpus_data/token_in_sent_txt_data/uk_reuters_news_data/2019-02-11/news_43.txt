rouhani says iran to continue expanding its military might, missile work - tv
dubai feb reuters iranian president hassan rouhani said on monday that iran was determined to expand its military power and ballistic missile programme despite mounting pressure from hostile countries to curb iran s defensive work state tv reported
we have not asked and will not ask for permission to develop different types of missiles and will continue our path and our military power rouhani said in a speech at tehran s azadi freedom square where tens of thousands gathered to mark the anniversary of iran s islamic revolution
