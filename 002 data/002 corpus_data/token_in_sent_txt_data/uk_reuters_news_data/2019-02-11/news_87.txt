bp invests more in egypt than anywhere else for second year
cairo feb reuters british oil major bp has invested more in egypt than anywhere else in the world for two consecutive years ceo bob dudley said on monday
for the last two years in a row we ve invested more money in egypt than in any other country
so this is a deep deep commitment to egypt dudley told an energy forum in cairo
