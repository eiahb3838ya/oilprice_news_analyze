white house senior counsellor kellyanne conway on monday also expressed confidence in a possible deal
asked if the two countries were getting close to a trade agreement she told fox news in an interview it looks that way the united states is expected to keep pressing china on longstanding demands that it reform how it treats american companies intellectual property in order to seal a trade deal that could prevent tariffs from rising on chinese imports
the latest talks kick off with working level discussions on monday before discussions later in the week
negotiations in washington last month ended without a deal and with the top negotiator declaring work was needed
we of course hope and the people of the world want to see a good result chinese foreign ministry spokeswoman hua chunying said at a news briefing in beijing
