opec does not fix oil prices - barkindo
cairo feb reuters opec mohammad barkindo said the exporting group was not in the business of manipulating or fixing oil prices when asked on monday to comment on a house committee passing a bill targeting opec oil supply cuts
a house of representatives committee approved a bill on thursday that would open up the organization of the petroleum exporting countries to antitrust lawsuits but it was uncertain if the measure would be considered by the full chamber
we are not in the business of manipulating or fixing prices therefore it would be a misjudgment to accuse us of such barkindo said on the sidelines of an enery forum in cairo
