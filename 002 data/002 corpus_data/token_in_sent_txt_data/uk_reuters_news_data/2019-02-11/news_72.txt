canada watchdog probes allegations of interference in snc-lavalin case
ottawa feb reuters canada s independent ethics commissioner said on monday he was probing allegations that the office of prime minister justin trudeau pressured former justice minister jody to help construction company group inc avoid criminal trial
mario dion made the announcement in a letter he sent to two opposition legislators who had asked him to look into the matter
a copy of the letter was made available to reuters
