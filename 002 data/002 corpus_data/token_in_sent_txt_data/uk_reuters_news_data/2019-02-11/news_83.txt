total ceo says pushing further into lng in 2019
cairo feb reuters total ceo patrick pouyanne said on monday the french oil major was looking to push further into liquefied natural gas lng over the next year with five more projects expected
we have five more projects to come like artic in russia and we re working on four others pouyanne said on the sidelines of an energy forum in cairo
