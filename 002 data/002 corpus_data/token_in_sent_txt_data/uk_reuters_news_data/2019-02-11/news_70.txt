switzerland helps brazil prosecutors with probe into major oil traders
geneva feb reuters swiss authorities are assisting brazilian prosecutors in their investigation into an alleged corruption scheme involving global trading firms vitol glencore and trafigura a spokesman for the office of the attorney geneval of switzerland said
the spokesman said the attorney general s office had received requests between june and january for mutual legal assistance from brazilian prosecutors relating to their probe
brazilian prosecutors have alleged that executives of the commodities companies including some in the united states were involved in improper payments to executives at brazil s petroleo brasileiro sa
the justice department has launched its own investigation into these business dealings
