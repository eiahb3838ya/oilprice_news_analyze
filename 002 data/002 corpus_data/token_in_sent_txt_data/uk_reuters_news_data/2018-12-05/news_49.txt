argentina takes peru to wto in biodiesel row
geneva dec reuters argentina has launched a dispute at the world trade organization to contest peru s and duties on argentina s exports of biodiesel a wto filing published on wednesday showed
argentina s complaint suggests the two countries have failed to settle a row that blew up in october when peru slapped tariffs on imports of biodiesel from argentina for a period of five years after complaints of unfair competition
