equinor, faroe petroleum in norway asset swap
oslo dec reuters britain s faroe petroleum the subject of a hostile bid by dno has agreed to a swap of norwegian oil and gas assets with equinor it said on wednesday
the binding deal will boost faroe s output and cash flow while equinor will secure stakes in fields located in the less mature norwegian sea
the increased cash flow reduction in capital expenditure and reduction in unit operating cost resulting from the transaction will further strengthen our already robust balance sheet faroe chief executive graham stewart said in a statement
this will enable us to give careful consideration to a potential return of capital to our shareholders as an additional element in our capital deployment mix he said
faroe on rejected a million pound million bid from dno which it said significantly undervalued the firm
