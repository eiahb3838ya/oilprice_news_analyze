update 1-ahead of opec meeting, trump urges producers to keep oil flowing
adds details on opec meeting background on trump s comments by susan heavey washington dec reuters president donald trump on wednesday called on the organization of the petroleum exporting countries and its allies not to cut oil production next year saying it would trigger higher oil prices worldwide
hopefully opec will be keeping oil flows as is not restricted
the world does not want to see or need higher oil prices trump wrote in a tweet ahead of the group s meeting this week to discuss possible cuts
opec meets in vienna on thursday to weigh production and on friday will hold talks with allies such as russia
official government oil production and inventory data are also due on thursday
saudi arabia opec s de facto leader has pressed for steep output reductions but has faced pressure from trump to keep oil flows steady to keep prices low rather than reduce inventory
trump who has made the economy one of his top issues has frequently complained about opec this year as higher crude oil prices have in turn raised fuel prices for consumers and businesses
russia s tass news agency reported on wednesday that opec and its partners may return production quotas to levels by reducing oil production by more than million barrels per day
oil markets rose on wednesday ahead of the meeting
