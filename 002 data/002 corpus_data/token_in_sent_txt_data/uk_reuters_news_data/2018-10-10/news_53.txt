enbridge natgas line rupture prompts evacuations in british columbia
oct reuters a natural gas pipeline ruptured northeast of prince george in the canadian province of british columbia on tuesday sparking a massive fire and prompting evacuations news website chek said
the psi gas line is operated by enbridge inc the report said quoting british columbia ministry of environment
there have been no reports of injuries according to a report on ctv news
