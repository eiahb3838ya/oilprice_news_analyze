u.s. heating costs likely to be higher this winter than last - eia
oct reuters most households should expect higher heating costs this winter october through march compared with last winter according to the federal government s winter fuels outlook which will be released in full at gmt on wednesday
the energy information administration eia said in a summary of the report that the increased costs are mainly the result of higher prices for heating fuels since temperatures are expected to be similar to last winter in much of the country
