arrest warrants issued for 20 accused in blast in sadr's baghdad stronghold
baghdad june reuters iraq s supreme judicial council has issued arrest warrants for people accused of involvement in a blast that killed people in the baghdad stronghold of cleric moqtada whose bloc won an election marred by fraud allegations state tv reported
nationalist sadr and paramilitary chief hadi who won first and second place respectively in the may vote announced on tuesday an alliance between their blocs
