siemens to mull sale of gas turbine business -bloomberg
reuters siemens ag is considering strategic options including a potential sale of its struggling business that makes large gas turbines for power plants bloomberg reported on wednesday citing people familiar with the german engineering company is looking at all possibilities such as a combination with a rival the report added
siemens has seen a drop in demand at its power and gas business which makes gas and steam turbines
seimens declined to comment on the report
