venezuela appoints bank exec medina as oil company cfo
caracas june reuters the venezuelan government has appointed bank executive iris medina as new chief financial officer for state oil company pdvsa according to the official gazette on wednesday
medina currently vice president of the national housing bank replaces iliana ruzza at the key post in the opec member s energy company which produces percent of venezuela s foreign currency revenues
medina takes the job at a difficult time for pdvsa which is facing production lows financial sanctions and anxiety among investors over unpaid foreign debt
