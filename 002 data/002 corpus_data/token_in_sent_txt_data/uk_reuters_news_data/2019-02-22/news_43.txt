saudi, china sign $28 billion worth of economic accords - spa
dubai reuters saudi arabia and china signed economic cooperation agreements worth a total of billion at a joint investment forum during a visit by saudi crown prince mohammed bin salman to beijing saudi state news agency spa said on friday
it said agreements had been signed at the forum held by saudi arabia s investment agency sagia
it also said four licenses for chinese companies had been awarded at the forum without giving any details
reporting by sylvia westall editing by gareth jones
