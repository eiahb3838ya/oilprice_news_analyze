venezuela, united states still discussing status of u.s. diplomats -minister
united nations feb reuters venezuela and the united states are still in discussions about the status of diplomats in caracas ahead of next week s deadline imposed by president nicolas maduro for them to leave venezuela s foreign minister said on friday
in a press briefing at the united nations foreign minister jorge arreaza said venezuelan officials would meet with the special envoy for venezuela elliott abrams to discuss the issue
