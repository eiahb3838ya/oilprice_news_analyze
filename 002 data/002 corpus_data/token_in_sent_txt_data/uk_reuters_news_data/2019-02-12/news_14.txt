republican leader says u.s. senate will vote on green new deal
washington feb reuters republican senate leader mitch mcconnell said on tuesday that the senate will vote on a green new deal introduced by democrats that seeks to eliminate greenhouse gas emissions within a decade
the document unveiled last week marked the first formal attempt by lawmakers to define legislation to create big investments in clean energy and infrastructure to transition the economy away from fossil fuels
