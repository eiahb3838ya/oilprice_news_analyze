venezuela's guaido says humanitarian aid will arrive on feb 23
caracas feb reuters venezuelan opposition leader juan guaido said on tuesday that humanitarian aid will enter venezuela on feb to bring relief to people struggling with widespread shortages of food and medicine
speaking at a rally in caracas guaido said the armed forces which remain loyal to president nicolas maduro should allow the aid to enter the country
guaido denounces maduro s presidency as illegitimate while maduro says a coup seeks to oust him
reporting by angus berwick and vivian sequera editing by sarah marsh
