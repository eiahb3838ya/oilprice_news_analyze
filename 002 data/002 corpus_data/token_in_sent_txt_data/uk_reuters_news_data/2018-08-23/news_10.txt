moves-equinor bulks up singapore crude oil trading team with new hires
singapore aug reuters norwegian oil major equinor said on thursday it has hired two crude oil traders in singapore to form a team as it steps up trading and oil marketing in asia
we can confirm that equinor has hired chen fei fan and chung chong min to become part of our team in singapore led by ragnar bulie equinor spokesman sverre olden mala said in an
chen will be leaving sinochem corp to join equinor in september three industry sources said
chung formerly a derivatives trader at south korea s sk energy joined equinor recently they said
equinor trades in crude oil produced in the middle east and russia as well as markets its oil produced in europe africa and the united states to refiners in asia
sinochem did not respond to a request for comment
