chemicals boss ratcliffe tops sunday times rich list
london reuters jim ratcliffe the founder and boss of chemical giant ineos is the richest man in the united kingdom according to the sunday times rich list first among a record billionaires living in the country
ratcliffe maintains a relatively low profile in britain as the chief executive of the country s largest private company which generates sales of around billion billion by manufacturing petrochemicals speciality chemicals and oil products
the sunday times said ratcliffe had shot up the list of britain s most wealthy due to the success of ineos and after it got access to more detail on the company which is percent owned by its founder
compiling the list for the time the sunday times said the wealth of the richest people had jumped by percent this year and said that they were more diverse than before with more women and entrepreneurs in the list
britain is changing said robert watts the compiler of the list
gone are the days when old money and a small band of industries dominated the sunday times rich list
aristocrats and inherited wealth has been elbowed out of the list and replaced by an army of the paper said that london was now the number one city in the world for billionaires with some billionaires born living or with their businesses based in london ahead of new york in second
