engie extends outages at two belgian nuclear reactors
paris sept reuters french energy group engie has extended outages at two of its belgian nuclear reactors by several months following the discovery of problems with their concrete structures
engie said in a notice on its website that the outage at its tihange nuclear reactor was extended until may from end of october while stoppage at tihange was extended until march from end of september
belgium s nuclear power regulator said earlier this week it had detected concrete degradation in two bunkers adjoining the reactor buildings
engie shares were down nearly two percent in midday trade
