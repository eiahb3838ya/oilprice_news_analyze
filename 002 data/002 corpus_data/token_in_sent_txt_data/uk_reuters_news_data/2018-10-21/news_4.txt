germany's merkel promises legislation to ward off diesel driving bans
berlin oct reuters german chancellor angela merkel campaigning for her christian democrats cdu to retain control of the crucial state of hesse in next sunday s election promised legislative changes to ward off the threat of air pollution leading to driving bans
speaking at a news conference from the state on sunday evening merkel said that new legislation would ensure that driving bans would not be needed in cities like frankfurt hesse s largest state where nitrogen emissions from diesel cars were only marginally above permissible levels
driving bans are disproportionate when it comes to small excess pollution levels she said
