china gasoline, diesel exports fell in feb
beijing march reuters china s monthly diesel exports fell to million tonnes in february the lowest since january while gasoline exports fell again from the peak level in december the general administration of customs said on friday
meanwhile liquefied natural gas imports fell to million tonnes in february from a monthly record high of million tonnes in january customs data showed
china s trade slowed during last month s lunar new year celebration
