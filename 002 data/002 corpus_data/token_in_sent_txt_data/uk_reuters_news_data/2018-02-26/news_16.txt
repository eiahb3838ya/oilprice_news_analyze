stmicro embeds software of french start-up sigfox in chips
paris reuters stmicroelectronics has built software from france s sigfox into its widely used microcontrollers giving a boost to the french by enabling developers to build its network features into industrial and consumer chips
sigfox s technology connects objects and collects data turning a range of simple electronics into internet devices
the french firm is one of the early developers of network lpwan technology designed to connect billions of simple objects beyond computers or phones to the internet of things iot
sigfox s technology competes with a rival industry standard known as lora whose software is already used in the same st software package
