update 2-saudi arabia raised oil output by around 500,000 bpd in june- sources
adds background abu dhabi july reuters saudi arabia has told opec it pumped million barrels per day bpd of crude oil last month an increase of bpd from the level it said it produced in may opec sources told reuters on thursday
saudi arabia s total supplies to the market in june were even higher than production the sources said suggesting the kingdom sold crude from storage
the sources said saudi arabia supplied million bpd in june a figure that includes domestic consumption and all exports including from storage tanks
consultants kpler estimated the kingdom s oil exports rose by bpd in june to million bpd compared with may
opec agreed with russia and other allies last month to raise output from july with saudi arabia pledging a measurable supply boost but giving no specific numbers
president donald trump on wednesday again accused the organization of the petroleum exporting countries of driving gasoline prices higher and urged the oil producer group to do more
saudi king salman bin abdulaziz al saud assured trump that the kingdom can raise oil production if needed and that the country has million barrels per day of spare capacity that could be deployed to help cool oil prices to compensate for falling output in venezuela and iran
