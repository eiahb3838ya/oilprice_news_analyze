preparations for listing aramco stalled - wsj
reuters public listing preparations of saudi aramco have stalled the wall street journal reported on thursday
aramco the world s biggest oil producer has been expanding its global footprint by signing downstream deals and boosting the capacity of its plants ahead of the eagerly awaited initial public offering
the company public listing is a part of saudi arabia plan to open its economy but a senior aramco executive told here the wsj that everyone is almost certain it ipo is not going to happen
aramco did not immediately respond to a request for comment
