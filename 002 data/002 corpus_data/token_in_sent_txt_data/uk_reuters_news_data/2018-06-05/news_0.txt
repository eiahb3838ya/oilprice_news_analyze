brazil plans public consultation on frequency of fuel price changes
rio de janeiro june reuters brazilian oil regulator anp said on tuesday it was planning a public consultation over the frequency of changes in fuel pricing
the consultation which will take place from june to july follows a nationwide trucker strike over diesel price hikes last month that paralyzed much of latin america s biggest economy
president michel temer agreed to cut diesel prices and change the frequency of price adjustments wiping out a third of the market cap of company petroleo brasileiro sa over fears of political interference
