update 1-russian oil refinery gets sberbank credit line, resumes loadings
recasts with sberbank credit line moscow dec reuters oil loadings have resumed at russia s afipsky refinery pipeline monopoly transneft said on friday after top lender sberbank provided a credit line to the afipsky and antipinsky refineries
transneft also said it expected to resume loadings to the antipinsky refinery soon interfax news agency reported
sberbank said on friday that it had provided additional credit lines to the refineries to support production allowing them to fully resume activity
it did not reveal the terms of the credit lines
