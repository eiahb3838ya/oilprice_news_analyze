states announce intent to sue over us rollback of clean car rule
reuters the attorneys general of states and washington on thursday announced their intention to sue over a trump administration plan to roll back federal limits on tailpipe pollution from cars and trucks referred to as the clean car rule
according to a statement from massachusetts attorney general maura healey the expected rollback by the environmental protection agency would cost americans as much as billion on gas and add the carbon emissions equivalent of million cars
the state coalition includes every attorney general from jurisdictions that have adopted california s more stringent vehicle emissions standards the statement said
