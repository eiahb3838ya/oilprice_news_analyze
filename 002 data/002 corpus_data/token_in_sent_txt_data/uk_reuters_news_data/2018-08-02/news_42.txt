moves-trafigura's top china crude oil marketer geng tao leaves firm
beijing aug reuters trafigura s top crude oil marketer in china geng tao said on thursday he has left the swiss commodities trading house the latest sign that business in the world s largest crude importer is getting more challenging
in a phone call he said he departed the company a few days ago
trafigura declined to comment
his exit comes after the company appointed ben luckock and robert gillon as of oil last month
