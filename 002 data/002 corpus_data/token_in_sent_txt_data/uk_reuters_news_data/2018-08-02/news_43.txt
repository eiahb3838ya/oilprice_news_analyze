germany s dax falls pct stoxx dips percent euro zone bond yields edged down oil prices steady by julien ponthus london aug reuters fears of an escalating trade dispute between the united states and china spread from asian markets to europe on thursday triggering a fall in bonds yields and stocks particularly among exporters
germany s index dax which is seen by as trade war proxy fell percent about an hour after the open while the broader stoxx was down about percent
euro zone government bond yields edged down with borrowing costs in germany and france pulling back from highs as demand for debt grew after the administration increased pressure on china by proposing a higher percent tariff on billion worth of chinese imports
msci s broadest index of shares outside japan closed percent down dragged down by a percent fall in chinese
analysts blame the current retreat in world stock markets on the uncertainty around the trade policy of the trump administration while recent corporate results are seen encouraging
one needs to have a strong gut feeling to invest in this environment and in august i doubt many people will have one said herve goulletquer deputy head of research at france s la banque postale asset management in paris
he added that investors badly needed a framework of interpretation to read through the trade statements of the trump administration and the poor visibility on that front was holding markets back
the british pound was slightly down at ahead of an expected rate hike by the bank of england later in the day
