moves-trafigura's top china crude oil marketer geng tao leaves firm
geng s exit follows several changes among senior oil execs comes at tough time for oil traders as teapots retreat trafigura reported big drop in profits adding details throughout by chen aizhu and florence tan aug reuters trafigura s senior crude oil executive in china geng tao said on thursday he has left the swiss commodities trading house the latest sign that business in the world s largest crude importer is getting more challenging
in a phone call he said he departed the company a few days ago
he did not give a reason for his exit
trafigura declined to comment
his exit follows the appointments of ben luckock and robert gillon as of oil for trafigura last month
in april the company named hadi hallouche as head of oil for asia while keeping his role as global head of natural gas
li yuting another senior oil marketer at trafigura left the firm last year said a trading executive briefed on the matter
global oil suppliers are facing a more competitive business environment in china this year as demand has slowed from independent refineries due to tighter government tax rules and as oil prices have risen to highs
in the first half of the year trafigura s profits sank percent due in part to the market shift from a contango where forward prices of a commodity are higher than prompt prices to backwardation which discourages storing oil for sale later
