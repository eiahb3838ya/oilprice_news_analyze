brazil's moro: justice ministry will not be used to persecute opponents
brasilia nov reuters brazil judge sergio moro picked to be jair bolsonaro s justice minister vowed on tuesday that the ministry will not be used for political persecution but for fighting corruption and organized crime
moro said he accepted the job because he is worried impunity could return in brazil
he said the ministry will use the same methods he used successfully in the car wash investigation that saw dozens of businessmen and politicians jailed for graft
reporting by anthony boadle
