update 1-oil market is balanced, says qatar energy minister
dubai reuters the oil market is balanced in terms of supply and demand qatar s energy minister mohammed said on sunday
geopolitical changes are the reason for the recent rise in crude prices the qatar news agency quoted him as saying
there are developments in the oil market including supply and the increase in global demand and these considerations will be taken into account at the next meeting of opec the organization of the petroleum exporting countries he said
oil prices have been rising as sanctions against iran s crude exports are set to start next month
brent crude is trading at nearly a barrel compared to at the beginning of the year
