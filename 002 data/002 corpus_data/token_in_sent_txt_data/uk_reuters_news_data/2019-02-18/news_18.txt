customs clearance typically takes five to days authorities notified traders about possible delays no reason cited for longer australian clearing times tensions have risen recently beijing feb reuters chinese coal traders are halting purchases of australian coal and coking coal as clearing times through china s customs have at least doubled to days or more four traders at major buyers told reuters on monday
the traders said only cargoes from australia the biggest supplier of the fuel to the world s top consumer were affected
we have stopped ordering coal from australia because it is unknown how long the restriction will last said a manager at a trading company who usually buys around tonnes of australian coal every month
customs clearance typically takes five to days
