update 1-iran self-sufficient in gasoline, oil minister tells state tv
adds detail dubai feb reuters iran has become in gasoline production iranian oil minister bijan zanganeh said on monday after inauguration of the third phase of the persian gulf star refinery in the southern port city of bandar abbas
fortunately we do not need to import gasoline anymore
we have reached
we can export our produced gasoline but have no export plans zanganeh was quoted as saying by iran s state tv
opec member iran has for years struggled to meet its domestic fuel needs because of a lack of refining capacity and international sanctions that limited the supply of spare parts for plant maintenance
iran s persian gulf star refinery converts light crude known as condensate into gasoline and naphtha
zanganeh said that with the inauguration of the new phase the refinery would produce million litres of petrol per day
president donald trump pulled out of an international nuclear deal with iran last year and reimposed sanctions on iran s oil and banking sectors
