china unveil guidelines for developing "greater bay area"-xinhua
beijing feb reuters china s cabinet on monday issued guidelines for developing a greater bay area around the pearl river delta in a bid to spur growth and transformation in guangdong province and the cities of hong kong and macau
the area is home to some million people with a combined gdp of trillion roughly that of australia or south korea
authorities will expand and upgrade airports in hong hong macau guangzhou and shenzhen and accelerate construction of oil reserves bases in the pearl river delta area the xinhua news agency said citing the cabinet s guidelines
financial institutions in the greater bay area will be allowed to conduct spot and forward yuan trading and yuan derivatives and conduct yuan lending xinhua said
