brazil's bolsonaro welcomes chinese investment, trade
brasilia reuters brazilian jair bolsonaro said on monday that china is welcome to invest in brazil and that trade between the two countries could grow
bolsonaro s remarks in a television interview contrasted with his portrayal of china on the campaign trail when he depicted it as a predator seeking to dominate key sectors of brazil s economy
chinese diplomats met with bolsonaro on monday and want him to visit the asian country as soon as possible he said
