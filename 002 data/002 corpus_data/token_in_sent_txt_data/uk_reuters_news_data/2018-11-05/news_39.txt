edf extends outage at france's havre 4 coal power plant due to strike
paris nov reuters french utility edf has extended the outage at its megawatts mw havre power plant by another hours until tuesday due to an ongoing strike said grid operator rte
electricity generation at the unit has been suspended since
the strike which started in protest against a french government plan to shut down power generation from coal was initially expected to end on sunday
rte said the unit was expected to restart on tuesday
edf warned on that protests could hit its coal power plants in france until the end of june due to a legislative process that would lead to the adoption of france s energy policy
france which depends on nuclear power for over percent of its electricity needs has shut down most of its coal power plants
only five units with an installed capacity of around mw are currently operational
president emmanuel macron has promised to shut down the remaining coal power plants by the end of to respect climate change obligations
the energy policy known as ppe expected to be unveiled at the end of the month could lay out a time frame for the closure of the remaining coal generation units
