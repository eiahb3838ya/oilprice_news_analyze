france's macron says violence will not force a reversal on energy policy
paris nov reuters president emmanuel macron said on tuesday he heard the anger of citizens and their complaints over higher diesel taxes but that he would not cave in to violent fringe groups and change tack on energy policy
i do not confuse citizens and their demands with thugs macron said
i will not concede anything to those who want destruction and his comments follow more than two weeks of sometimes violent demonstrations by yellow vest protesters in paris and in towns and cities around the country
