libya's noc declares force majeure on zawiya exports as sharara output drops
tripoli july reuters libya s national oil corporation noc said on tuesday it had declared force majeure on exports from zawiya oil terminal as production at sharara oilfield dropped to barrels per day bpd
production at sharara where some staff were evacuated after two workers were abducted on saturday was enough to supply zawiya refinery but left no excess for export the noc said in a statement
