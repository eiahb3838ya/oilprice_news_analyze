turkey can no longer take in more refugees, says turkey's erdogan
istanbul sept reuters turkey can no longer afford to take in any more refugees from syria turkish president tayyip erdogan said during a summit with the leaders of russia and iran in tehran on friday
turkey has accepted million refugees from syria since the beginning of the war
erdogan pledged in june to facilitate the return of refugees
the turkish president was speaking at a summit in tehran with iran and russia s leaders where they met to discuss the fate of the province of idlib
on friday erdogan said steps would be taken to prevent further migration from syria
ocha estimates that if the offensive were to take place people would be displaced
