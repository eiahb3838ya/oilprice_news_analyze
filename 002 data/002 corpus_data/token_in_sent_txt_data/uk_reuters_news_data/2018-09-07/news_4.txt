idlib offensive would cause of collapse of syria's political process, turkey's erdogan says
istanbul sept reuters the continuation of attacks in the province of idlib would lead to a collapse of the political process in syria turkish president tayyip erdogan said on friday
speaking in tehran at a summit with the leaders of iran and russia erdogan said it is crucial to maintain idlib s current status
the three leaders of the main outside players in syria s long war were meeting to discuss the fate of idlib as rival factions there prepared for what could be the conflict s last great battle
