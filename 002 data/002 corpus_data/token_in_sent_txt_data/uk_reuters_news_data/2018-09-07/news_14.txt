iraq protesters enter oilfield facility, hold two staff hostage - lukoil, police
basra iraq sept reuters protesters entered a water treatment facility linked to the west qurna oilfield managed by lukoil and held two iraqi employees hostage on friday according to a lukoil source and a source with basra s energy police
west qurna oilfield lies km miles of basra a city hit by days of protests
the field produces barrels per day and a disruption of three days would be enough to completely shut down the field the lukoil source added
the water treatment facility maintains a supply of water to inject into the west qurna oil wells
