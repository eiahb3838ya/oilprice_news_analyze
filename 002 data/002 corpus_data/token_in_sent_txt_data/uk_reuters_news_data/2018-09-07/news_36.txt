china, exxonmobil discuss $10 bln investment in guangdong -state tv
beijing reuters chinese officials and executives of exxonmobil corp discussed a billion investment by the firm in the southern province of guangdong chinese state television reported on friday
the state tv report during its summary of a meeting between chinese premier li keqiang and exxonmobil chief executive darren woods quoted li as saying he welcomed exxon s large investment in china
the report said china and exxon discussed the billion investment but did not attribute the information to any direct source
exxon said on thursday it signed a preliminary deal to build a petrochemical complex and invest in a liquefied natural gas terminal in china
it said the investment would be worth billions of dollars but did not give a specific figure
reporting by beijing monitoring desk editing by nick macfie
