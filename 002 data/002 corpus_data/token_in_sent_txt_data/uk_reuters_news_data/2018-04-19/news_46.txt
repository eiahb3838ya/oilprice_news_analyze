head of oil major total expects good q1 results due to high oil price
paris reuters french oil major total expects to report good first quarter results next week thanks to the impact of high oil prices said company head patrick pouyanne on thursday
asked on the sidelines of an oil summit in paris if total would announce more strong results next week pouyanne replied you know the price of oil is higher so it should be markets had appreciated the fact that in all results were quite predictable and we never disappointed them he added
oil prices rose on thursday to their highest level since late as crude inventories declined moving closer to averages and after sources told reuters that saudi arabia is seeking to push oil prices higher
pouyanne also said total could achieve synergies of around million euros million from its takeover of direct energie
