china bans imports of 16 more scrap, waste products from end-2018 - ministry
shanghai april reuters china will ban the imports of more scrap metal and chemical waste products from the end of this year the environment ministry said on thursday
the banned products include steel smelting slag that contains more than percent of the metal manganese and ethylene polymer waste the ministry of ecology and environment mee said in a document published on its website
another items will be banned by the end of it said
china s crackdown on waste imports which stood at as much as million tonnes in is part of its war on pollution and designed to help the country upgrade its economy and move up the global supply chain
